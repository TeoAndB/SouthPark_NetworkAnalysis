He tells [[Eric Cartman]] that the reason he is applying for the post is to raise his self-esteem. Unfortunately for him, he was not selected for the job.\n\nHe was later seen in \"[[Butterballs]]\" during Stan's anti-bullying video and in \"[[Cock Magic]]\" at the card duel between [[Kenny McCormick]] and his opponent named \"Slaughterhouse\".\n\n==Appearance==\nMike wears a short-sleeved gray shirt under a darker gray vest, brown pants and black shoes. He has freckles under his eyes and has very short spiky brown hair.\n<gallery spacing=\"small\" caption=\"Images of Mike Cooper\" widths=\"160\">\nMike highres.png|Mike in \"[[Dances with Smurfs]]\".\nMike.jpg|A comparison of Mike's unused model in [[South Park: The Stick of Truth]] and his real model.\n</gallery>\n\n==Personality==\nHe speaks with a severe stutter, not getting past the first word in the passage [[Mr. Mackey]] asks him to read.
