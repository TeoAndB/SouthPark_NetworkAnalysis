During which he asks [[Sheila Broflovski]] where little Kyle was.\n\nIn \"[[More Crap]]\", Murrey is mentioned by Gerald Broflovski who claims that he had taken the largest crap in the world, only for it to be surpassed by [[Randy Marsh]].\n\n==Appearance==\nHe wears white shirt and a dark blue tie. He has gray hair with white steaks. He also wears a dark blue Kippah.
