He also sleeps with [[Sharon Marsh]]. In the episode, \"[[HappyHolograms|#HappyHolograms]]\", he walks into the [[Park County Police Station]], teams up with Michael Jackson, and helps him kill the [[Record Producer]].\n\n==Appearance==\nTupac Shakur appears as a hologram of the real Tupac Shakur. He is bald with a black goatee and mustache, and he wears brown shoes, a silver cross-chain, blue jeans and a belt, and visible white underwear.
