He got himself and Butters in trouble by having a men's underwear catalog in his room. After Butters, not getting what he meant, said he 'like-liked' him, he admitted having \"bad thoughts\" about their friendship. Bradley tried to commit suicide by attempting to jump off a bridge later in the episode, saying he was incapable of being cured. After Butters gives his speech, he gets down safely.\n\nDuring the [[Season Seventeen]] intro, Bradley can be seen fishing off the bridge with the counselor from Camp New Grace as the school bus passes.\n\n==Appearance==\nBradley has messy curly blond hair and is usually seen showing his teeth. He was spotted wearing the [[Camp New Grace]] uniform, which consists of a light blue short-sleeved shirt with the Camp New Grace logo in the middle, light brown pants and black shoes.
