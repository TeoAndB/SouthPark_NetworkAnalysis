"{{South Park character\n|image = DickDastardly.png\n|imagetwo = Muttley.png\n|name = Dick Dastardly (Top) and Muttley (Bottom)\n|gender = Male\n|hair = Gray\n|job = Villains\n|voice = [[Trey Parker]]\n|appearance = \"[[Handicar]]\"\n}}\n'''Dick Dastardly''' and '''Muttley''' are a villainous duo who appear in the [[Season Eighteen]] episode, \"[[Handicar]]\", where they drive their Mean Machine in the TV series ''Wacky Races''.\n\n==Background==\nDick Dastardly and Muttley competed in the ''Wacky Races''. They cheated in the race by cutting down a tree with a saw which took down 2 cars.\n\n==Appearance==\nDick Dastardly and Muttley appear much as they did in the original ''Wacky Races'' series, but they appear to have aged and their hair has grayed.\n\nDick Dastardly wears a dark purple coat and gloves, a black ascot, black pants tucked into brown boots, and a striped hat with goggles. He has gray hair and a small gray mustache.\n\nMuttley wears a red scarf, a blue-green pilot hat with goggles, and black spectacles (presumably due to having bad eyesight from old age). He has a single fang protruding from his lower jaw.
