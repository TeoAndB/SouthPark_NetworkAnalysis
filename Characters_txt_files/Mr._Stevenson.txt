"{{South Park character\n|name = Mr. Stevenson\n|image = Adults-mr-stevenson-hummersalesman.png\n|gender = Male\n|race = Caucasian\n|hair = Brown\n|job = Car Salesman\n|appearance = \"[[Obama Wins!]]\"\n|last = \"[[Sons A Witches]]\"\n}}\n'''Mr. Stevenson''' is a car salesman, selling [[Hummer]] vehicles at [[Stevenson's Hummer]]. He first appears in the [[Season Sixteen]] episode, \"[[Obama Wins!]]\".\n\n==Background==\nIn \"[[Obama Wins!]]\", he is seen advertising his vehicles and conducting a sale at his car lot called 'Roctvember Sales Event'. As people pass by his lot, or conduct their affairs at neighboring businesses, he is shown to unsuccessfully persuade them to visit his car lot. In the end, [[The Boys|the boys]], the [[Park County Police Force]], [[General Tso]], and [[Eric Cartman]] visit Stevenson's Hummer to locate the [[Missing Election Ballots|missing election ballots]]. The salesman naively mistakes this predicament as a sudden rush of clients, citing it to be a 'sales rush'.\n\nMr. Stevenson returns in \"[[Handicar]]\" where he is seen trying to compete in the Wacky Races.\n\nIn \"[[Sons A Witches]]\", he makes a cameo in the background when the other witches corner [[Chip Duncan|Chip]].\n\n==Appearance==\nMr. Stevenson wears brown dress shoes, black business pants, a black belt with a gold buckle, and a blue shirt with a blue, striped tie. He has wavy, light brown hair and a similar colored goatee. His shirt has a tag saying \"HUMMER\" in red letters.\n\n==Prominence==\n*\"[[Obama Wins!]]\" - Debut appearance.\n*\"[[Handicar]]\" - Joins the race.\n*\"[[Naughty Ninjas]]\" - Seen dining in the restaurant.\n*\"[[Truth and Advertising]]\"\n*\"[[Sons A Witches]]\" - (Cameo)\n*\"[[South ParQ Vaccination Special]]\" - Seen at the end celebrating the end of the pandemic.\n\n{{Minor Characters from Season Sixteen}}\n[[ja:ミスター・スティーヴンソン]]\n[[zh:史蒂文森先生]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Minor characters]]\n[[Category:Male characters]]\n[[Category:Adult characters]]\n[[Category:Unnamed characters]]\n[[Category:Characters with unknown voice actor]]"
