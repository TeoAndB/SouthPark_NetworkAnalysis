She is well known for her catchphrases \"Would you like a dance?\", and \"Dance?! Anybody like a dance?\". She first appears in the [[Season Seven]] episode, \"[[Lil' Crime Stoppers]]\". She is later seen working in [[South Park Elementary]]'s Administration Office in \"[[Tegridy Farms]]\".\n\n==Appearance==\nBlaze has brown hair and makeup that consists of purple and blue eyeshadow, mascara, and red lipstick. She is wearing purple lingerie that has fur on it and white heels. \n\n==Prominence==\n*\"[[Lil' Crime Stoppers]]\" – Debut.\n*\"[[Erection Day]]\" – Crying and clapping near the end.\n*\"[[Bloody Mary]]\" – Sitting with the kids at the police station.\n*\"[[The Return of Chef]]\" – Seen performing at [[The Peppermint Hippo]].\n*\"[[Tegridy Farms]]\" – Working as [[PC Principal]]'s secretary.\n*\"[[Buddha Box]]\" – Voice only appearance, tells PC Princial that Vice Principal [[Strong Woman]] needs him.\n*\"[[South ParQ Vaccination Special]]\" - Seen attending [[Margaret Nelson]]'s funeral, wearing a green dress.\n\n==''[[South Park: The Fractured But Whole]]''==\nIn [[The Bowels of the Beast]] mission, she appears briefly during [[The New Kid]] and [[Captain Diabetes]]' fight against the [[VIP Johns]], peeking into the room and asking for a dance.
