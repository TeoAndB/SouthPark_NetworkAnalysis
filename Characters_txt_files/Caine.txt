"{{South Park character\n|name = Caine\n|image = Caine.png\n|gender = Male\n|race = Caucasian\n|hair = Brown\n|job = Cashier\n|voice = [[Trey Parker]]<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.imdb.com/title/tt1397941/|title=\"Margaritaville\" on IMDB}}</ref>\n|appearance = \"[[Margaritaville]]\"\n}}\n'''Caine''' appears in the [[Season Thirteen]] episode, \"[[Margaritaville]]\", where he was seen as the cashier for the [[Sur la Table]] store.\n\n==Background==\nApt to return [[Randy Marsh|his father]]'s ill-purchased Margaritaville blender, [[Stan Marsh]] went to Sur la Table in order to receive a refund. Willing to help Stan, Caine began the refund but was quick to discover that the blender was purchased with a payment plan. Realizing that he was unable to refund the machine, Caine directed Stan to the financing company in order to resolve the payment plan.\n\n==Appearance==\nCaine wore a black shirt and a dark teal apron with a red name tag. He has brown hair and brown eyebrows. He has a strange verbal tic of pronouncing words with 'ble' at the end as 'bluh' instead of the usual 'bul'.\n\n==References==\n{{reflist}}\n{{Minor Characters from Season Thirteen}}\n[[fr:Caine]]\n[[zh:凯恩]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Male characters]]\n[[Category:Adult characters]]\n[[Category:One-off characters]]"
