"{{South Park character\n|name = Count Ravyncrowe\n|image = <gallery>\nrayvencrowe.png|Current\nrcrowee.png|SOT\nravyncool.png|TFBW\n</gallery>\n|gender = Male\n|hair = Black with a Silver Streak\n|age = 10-11\n|job = Student\n|grade = [[The 4th Grade|4th Grade]]\n|appearance = \"[[The Ungroundable]]\"\n}}\n'''Count Ravyncrowe''' is a member of the [[South Park Vampire Society]] who first appear in the [[Season Twelve]] episode \"[[The Ungroundable]]\". His name was revealed in the [https://southpark.fandom.com/wiki/Thread:100893 removed vampire kids quest] in ''[[South Park: The Stick of Truth]]''.\n\n==Background==\nHis first appearance in \"[[The Ungroundable]]\" was going out to the [[Goth Kids|goth kids]]' hangout spot and suggesting it would be a great place for a vampire meeting. He and a younger vampire are scared off by [[Henrietta]].\n\nHe is later seen in \"[[Goth Kids 3: Dawn of the Posers]]\", at the noon meeting for the South Park Vampire Society. He sat in the second row, beside [[Larry]] and behind [[Vladimir]]. He does not play a significant role.\n\nHe and some other vampires in the Vampire Society make a brief appearance during the \"[[Black Friday]]\" trilogy, where they are seen throughout the episodes as part of [[Stan Marsh]]'s army to fight for the PlayStation 4 systems.\n\n==Appearance==\nRavyncrowe wears black shoes, black pants, a black jacket with a wide triangular collar and red trim, square yellow-tinted sunglasses, black fingerless gloves, and a red shirt with some kind of word on it. He has longish black hair with a silver streak on the front left side. He has eyeliner as well.\n\nIn the game he wore a red suit with a white undershirt underneath and his glasses had red lenses.\n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery widths=\"160\" spacing=\"small\" caption=\"Images of Count Rayvencrowe\">\nungroundable ravyncrowe.png|Ravyncrowe and a younger vampire looking to hold a meeting at the [[Goth Kids|goths]]' hangout.\nVladimir Sit.png|Ravyncrowe sitting beside [[Larry]] in \"[[Goth Kids 3: Dawn of the Posers]]\".\nVampires Black Friday.png|Rayvncrowe during the \"[[Black Friday]] Trilogy\", fighting for the PS4 in [[Stan Marsh|Stan]]'s army.\nCount Ravyncrowe.jpg|Ravyncrowe's design in the [https://southpark.fandom.com/wiki/Thread:100893 Cemetery Quest].\nravvv.png|Ravyncrowe leading the [[South Park Vampire Society|vampire kids]] in [[South Park: The Stick of Truth|SOT]] unused quest.\nchain hen.jpg|Ravyncrowe brainwashing [[Henrietta]] in the unused SOT quest.\n</gallery>\n\n==Prominence==\n*\"[[The Ungroundable]]\"\n*\"[[Goth Kids 3: Dawn of the Posers]]\"\n*\"[[Black Friday]]\" - Seen on Stan's side, fighting for the PS4.\n*\"[[A Song of Ass and Fire]]\" - Seen briefly when [[Eric Cartman]] visits the Stan's army.\n*\"[[Titties and Dragons]]\" - Seen as part of Stan's army and at the [[Red Robin]].\n[[fr:Count Ravyncrowe]]\n[[zh:拉文克罗伯爵]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Minor characters]]\n[[Category:Male characters]]\n[[Category:Male 4th graders]]\n[[Category:4th graders]]\n[[Category:Other 4th graders]]\n[[Category:School children]]\n[[Category:Vampires]]"
