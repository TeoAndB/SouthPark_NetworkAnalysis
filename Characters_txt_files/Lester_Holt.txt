"{{South Park character\n|name = Lester Holt\n|image = Lester-holt.png\n|gender = Male\n|race = African-American\n|hair = Gray\n|age = {{age|1959|03|08}}\n|dob = March 8, 1959\n|job = Journalist<br>[[Dateline]] Host<br>Debate Moderator\n|wikipedia = [[wikipedia:Lester Holt|Lester Holt]]\n|appearance = \"[[The Damned]]\"\n}}\n'''Lester Holt''' is an American journalist and was the moderator of the first presidential debate in 2016. He appears in the [[Season Twenty]] episode, \"[[The Damned]]\".\n\n==Background==\nIn \"[[The Damned]]\", Lester Holt takes the role as the moderator of the first presidential debate between [[Herbert Garrison]] and [[Hillary Clinton]] for the 2016 [[United States]] presidential election. He first asks Garrison how he would deal with ground troops in Syria.\n\nGarrison responds with a heart-felt speech about not being qualified for being president, to which Clinton responded by calling him a liar. Holt then goes on to moderate the rest of the debate.\n\n==Appearance==\nHolt wears a blue-gray suit, consisting of black shoes, gray pants, a white shirt with a purple tie, and a gray coat.
