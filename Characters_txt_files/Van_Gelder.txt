Observatory]] who appears in the [[Season Two]] episode, \"[[Roger Ebert Should Lay off the Fatty Foods]]\".\n\n==Background==\nAfter being hypnotized into working at the Planetarium, Van Gelder manages to escape via the school bus. Through [[Mr. Mackey]]'s use of mind-meld, he reveals [[Dr. Tristan Adams]]' plans. He is very unstable due to his hypnosis.\n\n==Appearance==\nHe wears the standard Tantalus V. Observatory uniform; a black shirt, with a light blue V-neck shirt and a picture of a dark cloud and sun.\n\n==Trivia==\n*Van Gelder is a parody of the character Simon Van Gelder who had narrowly escaped the Tantalus Penal Colony in the ''Star Trek: The Original Series (1966-1969)'' season one episode Ten \"Dagger of the Mind\".<ref>{{cite web|url=http://en.memory-alpha.org/wiki/Dagger_of_the_Mind_(episode)|title=\"Dagger of the Mind\"|publisher=Memory Alpha, Star Trek Wiki}}</ref>\n*In the 1999 [[South Park (Video Game)|South Park video game]], entering the cheat \"PLANEARIUM\" unlocks a \"pen and ink mode\", which drastically alters the game's graphics.\n\n==References==\n{{reflist}}\n{{Minor Characters from Season Two}}\n[[zh:范·格尔德]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Male characters]]\n[[Category:One-off characters]]\n[[Category:Characters voiced by Trey Parker]]\n[[Category:Characters with deviations]]"
