"{{South Park character\n|name = Chad Handler\n|image = <gallery>\nChadHandler.png|Current\nChadHandlerFormal.png|Formal\n</gallery>\n|gender = Male\n|hair = Brown\n|age = 10\n|job = Student\n|grade = 4th Grade\n|relation1 = Mother\n|relativename1 = [[Mrs. Handler]]\n|relation2 = Father\n|relativename2 = [[Mr. Handler]]\n|relation3 = Brother\n|relativename3 = [[Louis Handler]]\n|appearance = \"[[Up the Down Steroid]]\"\n}}\n'''Chad Handler''' is [[List of Male 4th Graders|male fourth grader]] who appears in several episodes with other handicapped children.\n\n==Background==\nChad made his first appearance in \"[[Up the Down Steroid]] \" at the Special Olympics, later he appears in \"[[Crippled Summer]] \" on [[Lake Tardicaca]] , he is on the [[Red Team]] along with [[Nathan]], [[Mimsy]], [[Francis (Special Ed)|Francis]], [[Michael (Special Ed)|Michael]], [[Dennis Murray]], and [[Reggie Burke]].\n\n==Appearance==\nChad is usually seen with a pink bicycle helmet and a blue backpack. He has brown hair and is almost always baring his teeth,his head is usually tilted to the right. Chad's current outfit is probably a plain red long-sleeved shirt, yellow pants, and black shoes. In \"[[Crippled Summer]]\" he wore a shirt similar, but with the Lake Tardicaca logo on the right. In \"[[Sponsored Content]]\" Chad wore a light blue ill-fitting sweater with a red tie and black pants.
