"{{South Park character\n|name = Boy with Olive Shirt and Black Pants\n|image = Boy-with-olive-shirt-and-black-pants.png\n|gender = Male\n|hair = Brown\n|age = 8-10\n|job = Student\n|appearance = \"[[Mr. Hankey, the Christmas Poo]]\"\n|speaking = \"[[Chef Aid]]\"\n}}\nThe '''Boy with Olive shirt and Black Pants''' is a male student at [[South Park Elementary]]. He first appears in the [[Season One]] episode, \"[[Mr. Hankey, the Christmas Poo]]\", and remained a recurring background character throughout the first four seasons.\n\n==Background==\nHe first appears as one of the children singing \"[[We Wish You a Merry Christmas]]\" in the choir in \"[[Mr. Hankey, the Christmas Poo]]\". He later received his first speaking role in the [[Season Two]] episode, \"[[Chef Aid]]\", where he is one of the many kids who makes fun of [[Herbert Garrison|Mr. Garrison]]'s hand puppet replacement [[Mr. Twig]]. He agrees with [[Stan Marsh|Stan]] when he says ''\"Mr. Twig sucks\"''. His only other speaking is in ''[[South Park: Bigger, Longer & Uncut]]'', where he has a slight southern accent.\n\n==Appearance==\nHe has brown hair, wears an olive shirt with a dark green collar, and black pants.\n\n<gallery widths=\"160\" spacing=\"small\" caption=\"Images of the boy with olive shirt and black pants\">\nBoyHoldingChinpokomonDoll.jpg|The boy holding a [[Chinpokomon Dolls|Chinpokomon doll]] in \"[[Chinpokomon]]\".\nBoyHoldingPoop.jpg|The Boy holding up a piece of poop to go along with his line ''\"And this poo is cold\"'' in the song \"[[It's Easy, Mmm'kay]]\" from ''[[South Park: Bigger, Longer & Uncut]]''.\n</gallery>\n\n==Prominence==\n{{Incomplete|item=list}}\n*\"[[Mr. Hankey, the Christmas Poo]]\" - Seen in the choir singing \"[[We Wish You a Merry Christmas]]\".\n*\"[[City on the Edge of Forever (Flashbacks)]]\" - Seen on the school bus.\n*\"[[Roger Ebert Should Lay off the Fatty Foods]]\" - Seen in the planetarium.\n*\"[[Chef Aid]]\" - First speaking role;
