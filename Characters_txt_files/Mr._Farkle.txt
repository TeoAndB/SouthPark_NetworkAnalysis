"{{South Park character\n|name = Mr. Farkle\n|image = Mr-farkle.png\n|gender = Male\n|hair = Brown\n|job = Owner of [[Jim's Drugs RX]]\n|voice = [[Trey Parker]]\n|appearance = \"[[Something Wall-Mart This Way Comes]]\"\n}}\n'''Mr. Farkle''' is the owner of [[Jim's Drugs RX]] who appears in [[Season Eight]] episode \"[[Something Wall-Mart This Way Comes]]\"\n==Background==\nMr. Farkle was forced to shut down his business amid the sudden popularity and success of [[Wall-Mart]], telling [[The Boys|the boys]] that he had to find another field to work in.\n\nHowever, following the destruction of Wall-Mart, the South Park residents decided to shop at Jim's Drugs RX from then on. Mr. Farkle reopened his business, which quickly developed into a mega-mart. It eventually met the same fate as Wall-Mart had gone through.\n\n==Appearance==\nMr. Farkle has a balding brown hair with a brown mustache. He wears a red sweater over a teal collared shirt and navy blue pants, along with a pair of square-framed glasses.\n[[zh:法克尔先生]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Male characters]]\n[[Category:Adult characters]]\n[[Category:One-off characters]]"
