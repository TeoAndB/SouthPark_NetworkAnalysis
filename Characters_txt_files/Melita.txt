When Cartman goes after her, she reveals that she didn't ever want to go to college (like most Jim Davis High School students) and that she was pregnant. Cartman tells her to have an abortion, but she didn't want to for Catholic reason and think that abortion is a wrong thing, but after a brief discussion Cartman manages to convince her. She later appears at the end of the episode along with the other students in Cartman's classroom.\n\n==Appearance==\nMelita has long brown hair and thin eyebrows, she also wears black mascara and has a tattoo on her right arm. She wears a short-sleeved red shirt, dark blue pants and red sneakers.
