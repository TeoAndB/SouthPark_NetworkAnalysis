"{{South Park character\n|name = Agent Fields\n|image = Agent-fields.png\n|aliases = Bill Sphinx\n|gender = Male\n|hair = Brown\n|job = FBI Agent\n|voice = [[Matt Stone]]<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0705940/|title=\"South Park\" Lil' Crime Stoppers (TV episode 2003)|publisher=IMDb.com}}</ref>\n|appearance = \"[[Starvin' Marvin in Space]]\"\n}}\n'''Agent Fields''' is an FBI agent who appears in \"[[Starvin' Marvin in Space]]\" and \"[[Lil' Crime Stoppers]]\".\n\n==Background==\nIn \"[[Starvin' Marvin in Space]]\", he is one of two CIA agents in search of an alien ship piloted by [[Starvin' Marvin (Character)|Starvin' Marvin]].\n\nIn \"[[Lil' Crime Stoppers]]\", he and his men inexplicably show up to the shootout underway at [[the Peppermint Hippo]] in \"[[Lil' Crime Stoppers]]\", where he attempts to pull jurisdiction on [[Lieutenant Dawson]]. His relationship with Dawson mirrors that of [[Agent Tucker]]'s relationship with the [[South Park Crime Unit]].\n\n==Appearance==\nHe wears the standard FBI uniform which consists of a black suit, a white undershirt with a black tie, and black pants.\n\n==Personality==\nHe seems to be controlling, completely overtaking [[Lieutenant Dawson]]'s case in \"[[Lil' Crime Stoppers]]\", not letting Dawson help or assist him in any way.\n\n==Trivia==\n*In \"[[Lil' Crime Stoppers]]\", it should have been the Drug Enforcement Administration (DEA), and not the FBI, who showed up, as [[Gino]] was a drug dealer.\n\n==References==\n{{Reflist}}\n[[es:Agente Fields]]\n[[fr:Agent Fields]]\n[[zh:比尔·斯芬克斯特工]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Male characters]]\n[[Category:Minor characters]]\n[[Category:Adult characters]]\n[[Category:Characters voiced by Matt Stone]]\n[[Category:Police officers]]\n[[Category:Characters with changed names]]"
