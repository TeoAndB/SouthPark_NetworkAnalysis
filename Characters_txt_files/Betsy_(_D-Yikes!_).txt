"{{South Park character\n|name = Betsy\n|image = Betsy.png\n|gender = Female\n|hair = Brown\n|voice = [[Trey Parker]]<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0983727/|title=\"South Park\" D-Yikes! (TV episode 2007) |publisher=IMDb.com}}</ref>\n|appearance = \"[[D-Yikes!]]\"\n}}'''Betsy''' is a lesbian woman who first appears in the [[Season Eleven]] episode \"[[D-Yikes!]]\".\n\n==Background==\nBetsy is one of the lesbian women who attends the [[Les Bos]] lesbian bar and joins together with the other lesbians to save the bar from being turned into a hangout for the Persians. She only has one speaking line in the episode, where she suggests that the lesbians hire someone to dig up some dirt on the owner of Club Persh.\n\nShe also makes a non-speaking cameo appearance in the [[Season Twelve]] episode \"[[Britney's New Look]]\" among the group of people taking pictures of [[Britney Spears]] at the end of the episode.\n\n==Appearance==\nBetsy has medium-length brown hair and brown eyebrows. She wears many pieces of jewelry including several earrings on both ears and some bracelets on her arms. She has multiple tattoos on her arms. She also wears a shark tooth necklace, a white and blue WNBA tank top, shorts with a camouflage design, and brown sandals.
