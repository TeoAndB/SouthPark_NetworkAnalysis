He helps Butters realize his power has the \"the key\" to saving [[Imaginationland (Location)|Imaginationland]] by using his imagination to fight the evil that took over the world.\n\n==Appearance==\nGandalf is an old man who wears a grey cloak and a long grey beard. He also wears a large gray wizard hat and a black belt with a silver-colored belt buckle. He wields a black staff with a gold-colored handle.
