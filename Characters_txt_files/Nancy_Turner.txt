She was first shown in the [[Season Nine]] episode, \"[[Marjorine]]\", at Heidi's slumber party. Heidi's other mothers, seen in \"[[Probably]]\" and \"[[Two Days Before the Day After Tomorrow]]\", were likely the result of her character model not being finalized, as she is normally a background character.\n\nHer name was revealed in ''[[South Park: The Stick of Truth]]'', during the quests to recruit the girls.\n\n==Appearance==\nNancy Turner has reddish-brown hair, a pink blouse, a blue skirt, and a purple undershirt. She also wears red lipstick.\n\n==Prominence==\nHeidi's mother appears in various forms with various character models in the following episodes:\n* \"[[Probably]]\" - She was seen with her husband shortly before his death.\n* \"[[Two Days Before the Day After Tomorrow]]\" - She was seen hiding in the [[Park County Community Center]] along with her family.\n*\"[[Marjorine]]\" - She and [[Mr. Turner|her husband]] hosted [[Heidi Turner]]'s sleep over.\n*\"[[Britney's New Look]]\" - Seen from the side leaving and entering the crowds, to take photos of [[Britney Spears]].\n*\"[[Reverse Cowgirl]]\" - Seen at town meeting, discussing various ways to get rid of the [[Toilet Safety Administration|TSA]].\n*\"[[Jewpacabra]]\" - Seen at the Easter egg hunt.\n*\"[[Sarcastaball]]\" - Seen at the PTA meeting with all the other parents.\n*\"[[A Scause For Applause]]\" - Seen in the background while [[Jesus Christ]] gives his speech.\n*\"[[Skank Hunt]]\" - Seen at the school assembly in a slide depicting her photo being vandalized.\n*\"[[The End of Serialization as We Know It]]\"- Seen waiting for Heidi.\n*\"[[Sons A Witches]]\" - Waits for Heidi when she leaves for the pumpkin patch.\n*\"[[Doubling Down]]\" - Goes out for ice cream with [[Heidi Turner|Heidi]].\n*\"[[The Problem with a Poo]]\" - Seen at the Christmas Concert.\n*\"[[Shots|Shots!!!]]\" - Seen at the CDC Child Immunization challenge.\n*\"[[Let Them Eat Goo]]\" - Seen at the town meeting.\n*\"[[The Pandemic Special]]\" - Appeared in a Zoom conference with [[Mr. Mackey]] and other parents.\n*\"[[South ParQ Vaccination Special]]\" - Seen at the end celebrating the end of the pandemic.\n==References==\n{{Reflist}}\n[[zh:南希·特纳]]\n[[Category:Townsfolk]]\n[[Category:Minor characters]]\n[[Category:Female characters]]\n[[Category:Parents]]\n[[Category:Characters in a relationship]]\n[[Category:Adult characters]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Characters voiced by April Stewart]]\n[[Category:Gingers]]"
