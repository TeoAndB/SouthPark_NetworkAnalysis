She appears in the [[Season Nine]] episode, \"[[Erection Day]]\".\n\n==Background==\nIn \"[[Erection Day]]\", Laura and [[Clark]] performed a magic act during the talent show. Clark played the role as the magician, and Laura played the assistant. Clark made her 'disappear' and then brought her back using a box.\n\n==Appearance==\nLaura wore a pink dress, a white cape with a pink lining, white pants, and a pink tutu. She also wore pink shoes, white gloves, and a pink hair tie.
