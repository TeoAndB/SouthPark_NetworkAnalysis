He wears a blue suit and tie.\n\n==Personality==\nGary Condit is portrayed as a liar in \"[[Butters' Very Own Episode]]\" as he claims that \"[[Some Puerto Rican Guy]]\" was actually the person who had an affair with Chandra Levy.
