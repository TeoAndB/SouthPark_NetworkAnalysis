"{{South Park character\n|name = Leonardo DiCaprio\n|image = leonardo-dicaprio.png\n|gender = Male\n|race = Caucasian\n|hair = Blond\n|age = {{age|1974|11|11}}\n|dob = November 11, 1974\n|job = Dream Infiltration Expert<br>Actor<br>Movie Producer<br>Environmentalist\n|voice = [[Vernon Chatman]]<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.imdb.com/title/tt1755897/|title=\"Insheeption\" on IMDB}}</ref>\n|wikipedia = [[Wikipedia:Leonardo DiCaprio|Leonardo DiCaprio]]\n|appearance = \"[[Insheeption]]\"\n}}\n'''Leonardo Wilhelm DiCaprio '''is an American actor and film producer, best known for having been nominated for ten Golden Globe Awards, winning two, five Academy Awards and an Oscar. He has been mentioned several times in the series but only appears once in \"[[Insheeption]]\" when he went into [[Mr. Mackey]]'s dream to save him, [[Stan Marsh]], and [[Mr. Yelman]]. He is voiced by [[Vernon Chatman]].\n\n==Background==\nThroughout ''South Park'', he is mentioned several times and makes one cameo appearance. His first mention is in \"[[Chef's Chocolate Salty Balls]] when [[Kyle Broflovski]] is briefly mistaken for him. \n\nHis first appearance is in the form of an image hung over [[Mr. Twig]]'s bed in \"[[Chef Aid]]\".\n\nIn \"[[Cow Days]]\", [[Eric Cartman]] suffers a head injury, which makes him believe that he was a Vietnamese prostitute named Ming-Li. After recovering the following day, he tells [[Stan Marsh]] and Kyle that he had a dream in which DiCaprio gave him a spanking for several hours. Immediately afterward, a limousine pulls up to Cartman, and an unseen DiCaprio thanks him for the night before.\n\nIn \"[[Something You Can Do with Your Finger]]\", the girls say that they wouldn't go crazy for Leonardo DiCaprio, but they would go crazy for [[Matt Lauer]].\n\nIn \"[[Trapped in the Closet]]\", Stan said he didn't think [[Tom Cruise]] was as good an actor like Leonardo DiCaprio but still thought that Tom Cruise was still an okay actor.\n\n==Appearance==\nLeonardo DiCaprio wears a black suit with a white shirt and a gray tie. He has blond hair and has a blond goatee. He can be seen wielding two pistols, an unsilenced one in the right hand, and a silenced one in the left hand.\n\n==Prominence==\n*\"[[Chef's Chocolate Salty Balls]]\" (mentioned only)\n*\"[[Chef Aid]]\" (mentioned only)\n*\"[[Cow Days]]\" (mentioned only)\n*\"[[Something You Can Do with Your Finger]]\" (mentioned only)\n*\"[[Trapped in the Closet]]\" (mentioned only)\n*\"[[Insheeption]]\" - Debut appearance as his character Dominic Cobb from ''Inception''.\n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery widths=\"160\" spacing=\"small\" caption=\"Images of Leonardo DiCaprio\">\nDicaprio.JPG|Leonardo DiCaprio's picture over Mr. Twig's bed in \"[[Chef Aid]]\".\nInsheeption067.png|DiCaprio and other dream infiltrators in [[Insheeption]]\".\nInsheeption081.png|DiCarpio, along with everyone else, in the dream state.\nInsheeption090.png|DiCaprio and the other dream infiltrators killing Billy Turner.\nInsheeption092.png|The scene after Billy Turner and his friends are shot dead.\n</gallery>\n\n==References==\n{{reflist}}\n{{MovieCelebrities}}\n[[es:Leonardo DiCaprio]]\n[[pl:Leonardo DiCaprio]]\n[[zh:莱昂纳多·迪卡普里奥]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Celebrities]]\n[[Category:Male characters]]\n[[Category:One-off characters]]\n[[Category:Adult characters]]\n[[Category:Movie celebrities]]"
