"{{South Park character\n|name = Alcoholics Anonymous Boy\n|image = Unamed-3rd-4th-graders-alcoholics-anonymous-kid.png\n|gender = Male\n|hair = Brown\n|voice = [[Trey Parker]]<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0761283/|title=\"South Park\" Bloody Mary (TV episode 2005)|publisher=IMDb.com}}</ref>\n|appearance = \"[[Bloody Mary]]\"\n}}\nThe '''Alcoholics Anonymous Boy''' is an unnamed [[The 4th Grade|4th Grader]] who appears in the [[Season Nine]] episode \"[[Bloody Mary]]\".\n\n==Background==\nThe Alcoholics Anonymous Boy appears at [[Whistlin' Willy's]] along with the other men who are part of the program. He tries to make conservation with [[Stan Marsh|Stan]] because his father has stopped drinking and he asks Stan if that means they are friends, though Stan is not interested. It is unknown which of the men is his father.\n\n==Appearance==\nThe boy has short brown hair and large ears. He has a very thin mouth which appears closer to his eyes than most other characters. He also wears a white T-shirt with an image of Doctor Octopus on it, brown pants, and black shoes.\n\n==References==\n{{Reflist}}\n[[es:Alcholico anonimo]]\n[[fr:Alcoholics Anonymous Boy]]\n[[zh:匿名戒酒会男孩]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Male characters]]\n[[Category:One-off characters]]\n[[Category:Unnamed characters]]\n[[Category:Characters voiced by Trey Parker]]\n[[Category:Townsfolk]]"
