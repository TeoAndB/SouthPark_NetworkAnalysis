"{{South Park character\n|name = Unnamed Goth Girl\n|image = 4th graders goth girl unamed.png\n|gender = Female\n|race = Caucasian\n|hair = Black\n|age = 9-10\n|appearance = \"[[Goobacks]]\"\n}}\nThe '''Unnamed Goth Girl''' is an unknown character that was seen to briefly associate with the [[Goth Kids]]. Her only appearances in the show were in the [[Season Eight]] episode, \"[[Goobacks]]\" and the [[Season Nine]] intro sequence. She had no dialogue and for unknown reasons was never seen with [[Firkle Smith|Firkle]] in either of her appearances.\n\n==Background==\nIn \"[[Goobacks]]\" she is seen helping [[Michael]] and [[Pete]] collect trash during the song \"[[The Future Begins With You and Me]]\". She also appears in the [[Season Nine]] intro sequence replacing [[Firkle Smith|Firkle]]. Not much is known about her since she has made no other appearances.\n\n==Appearance==\nShe appears to be wearing a white shirt with black stripes and a black vest with gray buttons, dark red gloves, white socks, and black shoes.
