After a few rounds, the crab people will summon the Crab King (who acted as the new manager) into the battlefield.\n\nOnce the superheroes defeat the Crab King, it will explode and disintegrate into nothing but green slime.\n\n==Appearance==\nThe Crab King has a much redder appearance compared to the subordinates.
