They played a minor role in the [[Season Seven]] episode \"[[Toilet Paper]]\".\n\n==Background==\nWhen [[The Boys]] decide to get revenge on their art teacher Mrs. Streibel for giving them after school detention by TP'ing her house, [[Kyle Broflovski|Kyle]] is reluctant at first to doing it because there are kids inside. Later on, when Kyle has a nightmare about what they had done, the daughters are shown crying for their mother as their parents scream in horror.\n\n==Appearance==\nBoth daughters have blonde hair. The first daughter has her hair in pigtails with purple hairbands. She wears a pink shirt, bright pink pants, and white shoes. The second daughter has green bows in her hair, which itself is cut short. She wears a purple shirt, a blue dress, and black shoes.\n\n==Prominence==\n*\"[[Toilet Paper]]\"\n*\"[[With Apologies to Jesse Jackson]]\" - Seen at the school assembly.\n*\"[[Tegridy Farms Halloween Special]]\" - Seen at the school assembly.\n\n==References==\n{{Reflist}}\n{{Minor Characters from Season Seven}}\n[[zh:史翠贝尔的女儿]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Female characters]]\n[[Category:Unnamed characters]]\n[[Category:Characters voiced by Eliza J.
