"{{South Park character\n|name = Thompson\n|image = Thompson the translator.png\n|gender = Male\n|race = Caucasian\n|hair = Blond\n|job = Interpreter\n|voice = [[Trey Parker]]<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.imdb.com/title/tt1312012/|title=\"South Park\" Pandemic (TV episode 2008)|publisher=IMDb.com}}</ref>\n|appearance = \"[[Pandemic]]\"\n}}\n'''Thompson''' is an interpreter who appears in the [[Season Twelve]] episode \"[[Pandemic]]\".\n\n==Background==\nAfter having captured several Peruvian flute bands, including [[the Llama Brothers]], [[Michael Chertoff]] announces to the flute bands that they will be sent to Guantanamo Bay, with Thompson translating the announcement to Spanish.\n\nLater, a guard calls up Thompson to help translate what the musicians were trying to tell them. Thompson explains that the musicians are the \"protectors\" from [[Furry Death]].\n\n==Appearance==\nThompson has thick blond hair, and wears a brown suit with yellow shirt and a dark blue tie.
