She appears in the [[Season Two]] episode, \"[[Prehistoric Ice Man]]\".\n\n==Background==\n[[Larry (\"Prehistoric Ice Man\")|Larry]] had gone missing on Kenosha Pass, and Leslie sends out a search party in the afternoon. After waiting 3 days, she moved on. However, sometime prior to this, she had met [[Buck]] and started a secret life with him. The two had two kids together, much to the surprise of Larry.\n\n==Appearance==\nLeslie wears black shoes, green pants, and a light-green shirt. Her shirt has the word \"FLO\" written in yellow below red, white, and blue stripes.
