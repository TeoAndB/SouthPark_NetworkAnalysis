His goal of worshiping [[Cthulhu]] will bring the dark lord out of his sleep, so as to rule the Earth as it was foretold in the [[Necronomicon]].\n\n==Appearance==\nThe Cthulhu cult leader wears yellow pants and black belt with a metal D-style belt buckle. He also wears a gray jacket over a blue collarless, button-up shirt and black shoes. He has a bowl-style haircut, shaved hair on the sides of his head with flat hair on top, and he has black eyebrows.\n\nWhen he is conducting his cult meetings, he wears a black full-body robe with a white anarchy symbol painted on the left breast. The hood of the robe covers the top half of this face.\n\n==Trivia==\n[[File:20180316124528 1.jpg|thumb]]\n*His model is reused as one of the crooked cops' enemies in ''[[South Park: The Fractured But Whole]]'', and the model is also used as the police who is found dead during the mission \"[[The Thin White Line]]\".\n\n==References==\n{{Reflist}}\n[[fr:Cthulhu Cult Leader]]\n[[zh:克苏鲁邪教领袖]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Unnamed characters]]\n[[Category:Male characters]]\n[[Category:One-off characters]]\n[[Category:Characters voiced by Trey Parker]]\n[[Category:Cthulhu cult followers]]\n[[Category:Adult characters]]"
