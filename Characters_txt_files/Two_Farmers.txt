"{{South Park character\n|name = Two Farmers\n|image = Farmers-rednecks-janitor-farmer.png\n|gender = Male\n|race = Caucasian\n|hair = Black and gray\n|job = Farmers\n|voice = '''Left''': [[Trey Parker]]<ref name=\"IMDb\">{{cite web|url=http://www.imdb.com/title/tt1947585/|title=\"South Park\" You're Getting Old (TV episode 2011)|publisher=IMDB.com}}</ref><br>'''Right''': [[Bill Hader]]<ref name=\"IMDb\"/>\n|appearance = \"[[You're Getting Old]]\"\n}}\nThe '''Two Farmers''' are two old men at the bowling alley who are [[Randy Marsh|Steamy Ray Vaughn]]'s only audience in the [[Season Fifteen]] episode, \"[[You're Getting Old]]\". The old men feel compelled to steal [[Randy Marsh|Randy]]'s underwear in order to save them from the fate of violent farts caused by his [[Tween Wave|tween wave]] performances.\n\nThe two also appear in \"[[W.T.F.]]\" as part of the enthused redneck crowd who see the boys' [[Wrestling Takedown Federation|wrestling league]] and believe it.\n\nThe farmer on the left later appears being interviewed by the news, both in the [[Season Eighteen]] episode, \"[[The Magic Bush]]\", and the [[Season Nineteen]] episode, \"[[Sponsored Content]]\". He also has a conversation with [[Skeeter]] and Randy in \"[[Truth and Advertising]]\".\n\nThe two farmers make a cameo appearance commenting on the college [[PC Bros]] as they are partying in the [[Season Nineteen]] episode, \"[[Stunning and Brave]]\".\n\nThey both also appear in the Season Nineteen episode, \"[[The City Part of Town]]\" showing the [[Whole Foods Representative]] how they slaughter cows. Both make cameo appearances in the [[Season Twenty-One]] episode \"[[White People Renovating Houses]]\" as among the protesting rednecks, and in the [[Season Twenty-Two]] episode \"[[Unfulfilled]]\" as [[Amazon]] employees.\n\n==Appearance==\nThe farmer on the left wears dark bluish jeans with a brown belt and a big metal buckle. He also wears a red unbuttoned shirt with rolled up sleeves under which he wears a white undershirt, and light brown boots. His hair is black and in the style of a comb-over.\n\nThe other farmer wears brown pants with a black belt and a golden belt buckle. He also wears a dark green T-shirt and black shoes. He is mostly bald and has a large gray beard. His arms are remarkably hairy. This farmer also has a second outfit that can be seen in some appearances. He keeps his normal pants, but now gets a brown cowboy hat which matches his pants. His dark green T-shirt gets replaced by a light green one with yellow text saying \"If it ain't country it aint music!\".\n\n==Prominence==\n*\"[[You're Getting Old]]\" - Debut appearance.\n*\"[[W.T.F.]]\" - Seen among the crowd of the W.T.F. event.\n*\"[[The Magic Bush]]\" - \n*\"[[Sponsored Content]]\" - \n*\"[[Cock Magic]]\" - Seen among the audience of the illegal [[Magic: The Gathering (CCG)|Cock Magic]] fights. \n*\"[[Stunning and Brave]]\" - They comment on the PC bros.\n*\"[[The City Part of Town]]\" - The black-haired farmer is shown describing his cattle business to the Whole Foods representative accompanied by [[Mayor McDaniels]]; the other farmer is shown executing the cows. \n*\"[[White People Renovating Houses]]\" - They appear among the protesting rednecks.\n*\"[[Unfulfilled]]\" - Seen working in the [[Amazon Fulfillment Center]]. \n*\"[[Bike Parade]]\" - Seen protesting against [[Jeff Bezos]]. \n*\"[[Shots]]\" - The two appear in the audience.\n*\"[[Let Them Eat Goo]] \" - The black-haired farmer dumps 300 cows at [[Tegridy Farms (Location)|Tegridy Farms]] in a scheme to frame [[Tegridy Burger]] for the [[Goo Man]].\n\n==Quotes==\n*''Did you know we're livin' in the Tween times?''\n*''I'd never heard that.''\n*''Guess it's the period between 2009 and 2013.
