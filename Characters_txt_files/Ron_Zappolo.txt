"{{South Park character\n|name = Ron Zappolo\n|image = Ron-zappolo.png\n|gender = Male\n|hair = Gray\n|job = News Anchor\n|appearance = \"[[Guitar Queer-O]]\"\n}}\n'''Ron Zappolo''' is the lead anchor of Denver's FOX affiliate KDVR. He appears as a local celebrity at [[Charles Kincade]]'s party in \"[[Guitar Queer-O]]\".\n\n==Appearance==\nRon has abnormally large teeth. He wears a black suit, light gray dress shirt, red, white-spotted tie, and black pants.\n\n==Trivia==\n*When asked about the parody, Zappolo reportedly said that in his 37 years on television, this cameo impressed his daughter the most.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.denverpost.com/entertainment/ci_7423001|title=\"South Park\" drops names, takes jabs|publisher=denverpost.com}}</ref>\n*In show, he was mistakenly referred to as working for Denver's Channel 4, KCNC, when he only worked there from 1977 to sometime in the late 1980s, when he left for rival KUSA; he left that station in 2000 to become the lead anchor for KDVR's then-debuting news operation.
