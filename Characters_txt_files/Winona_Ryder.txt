"{{South Park character\n|name = Winona Ryder\n|image = Moviestars-celebrities-winona-ryder.png\n|gender = Female\n|race = Caucasian\n|hair = Brown\n|age = {{age|1971|10|29}}\n|dob = October 29, 1971\n|job = Actress\n|religion = Judaism\n|voice = [[Toddy Walters]]<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0158983/fullcredits#cast|title=\"South Park: Bigger, Longer & Uncut\" on IMDB}}</ref>\n|wikipedia = [[Wikipedia:Winona Ryder|Winona Ryder]]\n|appearance = ''[[South Park: Bigger, Longer & Uncut]]''\n}}\n'''Winona Laura Horowitz''', better known as''' Winona Ryder,''' is an American actress most famous for her roles in ''Beetlejuice'', ''Heathers'', ''Edward Scissorhands, ''and ''Girl, Interrupted''.\n\nRyder appears in ''[[South Park: Bigger, Longer & Uncut]]'' where she performed her \"famous ping-pong ball trick\" at the USO show. From the camera angles provided, it initially appeared that she was projecting ping-pong balls from her vagina; it was later revealed that she was merely hitting them with a ping-pong paddle. Ryder's voice was provided by [[Toddy Walters|Toddy E. Walters]], who also served as the Production Studio Coordinator on the film.\n\n==Appearance==\nWinona has an abnormally small, blocky head, with short brown hair. She was seen wearing a pink dress.\n\n==Personality==\nDue to the fact she performed at the U.S.O.
