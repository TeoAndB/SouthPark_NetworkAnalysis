"{{South Park character\n|name = PC Ohio State\n|image = Pc-ohio-state.png\n|gender = Male\n|hair = Blond\n|voice = [[Matt Stone]]\n|appearance = \"[[Stunning and Brave]]\"\n}}\n'''PC Ohio State''' is an unnamed member of the [[PC Bros]] and a college student of the Ohio State University who first appears in the [[Season Nineteen]] episode \"[[Stunning and Brave]]\".\n\n==Background==\nIn \"[[Stunning and Brave]]\", he is first seen at [[Crunchy's Micro Brew]] along with the other PC Bros when [[Randy Marsh|Randy]] and the other fathers discuss [[Caitlyn Jenner]]. He makes friends with [[PC Principal]] and agrees to hang out at the [[PC Delta]] with the other PC Bros. At the end of the episode, he brandishes Randy's ass with the PC logo.\n\nHe also appears in \"[[Sponsored Content]]\", alongside the other PC Bros after [[Jimmy Valmer]] writes a story in the [[Super School News|school newspaper]] using the word \"retarded\" and sitting after Jimmy writes another story about \"PC\" standing for \"pussy crushing\".\n\n==Appearance==\nHe has long blonde hair and thin blonde eyebrows. He wears a blue short-sleeve shirt, a black belt, black pants, and black sneakers.
