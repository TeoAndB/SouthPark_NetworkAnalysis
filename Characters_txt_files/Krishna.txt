His powers include shapeshifting and flight. He saved [[South Park]] from [[Barbra Streisand]] by pacifying her in in \"[[201]]\".\n\n==Appearance==\nKrishna's most notable features are his light blue skin and \"halo\" around his head. Krishna wears a bejeweled crown on his head with what appears to be a variously shaded pink feather sticking out of the top of it. Krishna also wears a dark purple necklace, light purple strips of fabric on his wrists, a yellow and brown bracelet on his right wrist, loose fitting yellow dhoti with brown and blue decorations at their top, and a light orange sash.\n\n==Personality==\nHe is a member of the [[Super Best Friends (Group)|Super Best Friends]], so we can assume that he is heroic and cares about the safety of innocents.\n==Powers and Abilities==\nKrishna possesses the power of flight and can shapeshift into any animal or person. \n\n==Prominence==\n*\"[[Super Best Friends]]\" - Helps create the giant stone [[John Wilkes Booth]].\n*\"[[I'm a Little Bit Country]]\" - Sings.\n*\"[[Imaginationland]]\" - Fights with [[Jesus Christ]].\n*\"[[200]]\" - Appears alongside the other [[Super Best Friends (Group)|Super Best Friends]].\n*\"[[201]]\" - Takes down Barbra Striesand.\n\n==References==\n{{Reflist}}\n{{Minor Characters from Season Five}}\n[[es:Krishna]]\n[[ja:クリシュナ]]\n[[pl:Krishna]]\n[[zh:克利须那]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Religious Figures]]\n[[Category:Male characters]]\n[[Category:Immortals]]\n[[Category:Characters with superpowers]]\n[[Category:Super Best Friends]]\n[[Category:Characters voiced by Matt Stone]]\n[[Category:Imaginationland characters]]\n[[Category:Minor characters]]\n[[Category:Asian characters]]"
