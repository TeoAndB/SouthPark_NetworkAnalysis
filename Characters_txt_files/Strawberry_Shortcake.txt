"{{South Park character\n|name = Strawberry Shortcake\n|image = Strawberry Shortcake.png\n|gender = Female\n|race = Caucasian\n|hair = Pink\n|voice = [[April Stewart]]<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0995578/|title=\"South Park\" Imaginationland: Episode II (TV episode 2007)|publisher=IMDb.com}}</ref>\n|wikipedia = [[Wikipedia:Strawberry Shortcake|Strawberry Shortcake]]\n|appearance = \"[[Imaginationland]]\"\n|final = \"[[Imaginationland, Episode III]]\"\n}}\n'''Strawberry Shortcake''' is a character created by American Greetings and seen on greeting cards, dolls, posters, and in animation. She appears in the Imaginationland trilogy, where she is seen being tortured and killed by the evil fictional characters. She is later revived, along with every other imagination character that was killed, when [[Butters Stotch|Butters]] re-imagines Imaginationland.\n\n==Appearance==\nStrawberry Shortcake wears a pink dress, pink pants, and a large pink cherry design hat with a cherry on top. She also wears brown boots.
