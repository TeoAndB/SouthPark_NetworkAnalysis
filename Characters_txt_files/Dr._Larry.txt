"{{South Park character\n|name = Dr. Larry\n|image = Doctor-dr-larry.png\n|gender = Male\n|hair = Brown and Gray\n|job = Doctor\n|voice = [[Trey Parker]]<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0761289/|title=\"South Park\" Kenny Dies (TV episode 2001)|publisher=IMDb.com}}</ref>\n|appearance = \"[[Kenny Dies]]\"\n}}\n{{LinkDisambig|Larry (Disambiguation)}}\n'''Dr. Larry''' is a doctor who works at the [[Alder Research Group]] and appears in the [[Season Five]] episode \"[[Kenny Dies]]\".\n\n==Background==\nDr. Larry first appears when [[Eric Cartman]] attempts to sell some fetuses he acquired, but he refuses to take them because the government had put a ban on stem-cell research. Later on, he is seen when the Alder Research Group starts packing things up due to the ban. He explains how stem-cells work to Cartman and then wishes him good luck in attempting to change the government's mind about the ban on stem-cell research. \n\n==Appearance==\nDr. Larry has short brown hair with gray sideburns, brown eyebrows, and a brown goatee. He wears a white lab coat with two pens in the pocket, a teal shirt underneath, a dark blue tie, a belt with a gold buckle, purple pants, black shoes, and round glasses.\n\n==Trivia==\n*Dr. Larry's character model somewhat resembles the brown-haired [[Star Trek nerds|Star Trek Nerd]] from \"[[Fourth Grade]]\".\n\n==References==\n{{Reflist}}\n{{Minor Characters from Season Five}}\n[[zh:拉里医生]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Male characters]]\n[[Category:Adult characters]]\n[[Category:One-off characters]]\n[[Category:Overweight characters]]\n[[Category:Characters voiced by Trey Parker]]\n[[Category:Townsfolk]]\n[[Category:Medics]]"
