"{{South Park character\n|name = Moira McArthur\n|image = Moira McArthur.png\n|aliases = Sarah\n|gender = Female\n|race = Caucasian\n|hair = Red\n|relation1 = Husband\n|relativename1 = [[Mr. McArthur]]\n|relation2 = Daughter\n|relativename2 = [[Red McArthur]]\n|appearance = \"[[Pandemic]]\"\n}}\n'''Moira McArthur''' is [[Red McArthur]]'s mother who makes her first appearance in the [[Season Twelve]] episode, \"[[Pandemic]]\".\n\n==Background==\nShe makes many cameos in various episodes, first appearing in a news report of the first attack of the [[Guinea Creatures|guinea creatures]] in \"[[Pandemic]]\".\n\nHer name is revealed in \"[[The Pandemic Special]]\" when she appears with other parents on a Zoom conference with [[Mr. Mackey]] about allowing their children to return to [[South Park Elementary|school]]. When the students are quarantined in school for two weeks, she is revealed to be Red's mother when Red is seen on a Zoom call with her parents in the computer lab. [[Mr. McArthur]] tells Red that they miss her.\n\n==Appearance==\nMoira McArthur has shoulder-length red hair with a center parting and long eyelashes. She wears a gray cardigan over an olive green shirt, either a black knee-length skirt or dark blue pants, and black shoes. \n\n<gallery widths=\"110\" caption=\"Images of Moira McArthur\">\nMoiramcarthur.png|Moira on the PTA Zoom meeting in \"[[The Pandemic Special]]\".\nIcon profilepic drunkfemale var c.png|Moira on [[Coonstagram (App)|Coonstagram]].\nMoiraincitywok.jpg|Moira eating at [[City Wok]] in ''[[South Park: The Fractured But Whole]]''.\nMicroaggressionAcademy10.png|Moira seen in the [[Microaggression Academy]] quest in ''[[South Park: The Fractured But Whole]]''.\n</gallery>\n\n==''[[South Park: The Fractured But Whole]]''==\nShe is called Sarah in the game.
