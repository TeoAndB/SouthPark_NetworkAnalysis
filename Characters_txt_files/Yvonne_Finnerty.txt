"{{South Park character\n|name = Yvonne Finnerty\n|image = Groups-nannies-nanny-yvonne.png\n|gender = Female\n|hair = Brown\n|job = Nanny\n|appearance = \"[[Tsst]]\"\n}}\n'''Yvonne Finnerty''' appears in the [[Season Ten]] episode \"[[Tsst]]\" as one of the nannies on the television program ''Nanny 911'' who attempt to \"fix\" [[Eric Cartman]]'s behavior.\n\n==Appearance==\nYvonne has a very elongated nose, a pointy chin, and thin lips. Wrinkles around her mouth, eyes, and neck show her age. Her brunette hair has front bangs with the rest in a bun. She is seen wearing a pink button-up long-sleeved shirt as well as a black pink-striped sweater vest. She also wears a gold earring on her left ear.\n\n==Personality==\nAlthough she is not seen actually attempting to \"fix\" any child's behavior, the fact that she is a nanny, and a good enough one to be on Nanny 911, implies that she is strict but compassionate, easily \"getting through\" to kids and correcting their behavior.\n\n==Trivia==\n*The show she was featured on, ''Nanny 911'', is a real life television show.\n*She is a parody of an actual person of the same name: [http://www.nannyyvonne.com/About_Yvonne.html Yvonne Finnerty].\n\n{{Minor Characters from Season Ten}}\n{{TelevisionCelebrities}}\n[[zh:伊冯·芬纳蒂]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Celebrities]]\n[[Category:One-off characters]]\n[[Category:Minority characters]]\n[[Category:Overweight characters]]\n[[Category:Female characters]]\n[[Category:Television celebrities]]\n[[Category:British characters]]"
