They tell him that it was having sex with women, so Dr. Neeland brings The Boys and Chef to [[the Peppermint Hippo]] to try and bring Chef's old self back.\n\nDr. Neeland also says that he has two sons when Chef asks him if he has any children.\n\n==Appearance==\nDr. Neeland has curly brown hair that is graying on the sides and brown eyebrows.
