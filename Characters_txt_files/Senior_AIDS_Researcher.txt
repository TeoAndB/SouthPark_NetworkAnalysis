"{{South Park character\n|name = Senior AIDS Researcher\n|image = Senior-aids-researcher.png\n|gender = Male \n|race = Caucasian\n|hair = Gray\n|job = Scientist\n|appearance = \"[[Tonsil Trouble]]\"\n}}\nThe '''Senior AIDS Researcher '''is an [[AIDS]] researcher who first appears on \"[[Tonsil Trouble]]\". He is in-charge of [[Mitch]] and [[Black-Haired Scientist|his cohort]].\n\n==Background==\nIn \"Tonsil Trouble\", he and Mitch work on the solution to the disease based upon [[Magic Johnson]]'s money hypothesis. The two realize that if a HIV-positive person is injected with cut and crushed $180,000 into his vein, his AIDS will be cured. Despite some prior disbelief voiced by his cohort, this successfully cures both [[Kyle Broflovski]] and [[Eric Cartman]].\n\nHe makes a cameo appearance in \"[[Over Logging]]\".\n\nHe appears again in \"[[Pee]]\" as one of the scientists trying to resolve the pee flood in [[Pi Pi's Splashtown]].\n\nIn \"[[Sexual Healing]]\" he can be seen helping the team of scholars find a cure to the \"National Sexual Addiction problem\" America is facing.\n\nHe appears in the background as a [[USDA]] worker in \"[[Gluten Free Ebola]]\".\n\n==Appearance==\nHe has balding gray hair, a gray mustache, and wrinkles on his forehead.
