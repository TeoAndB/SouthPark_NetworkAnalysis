After Michael Jackson had done so, the passengers were flown to [[Hell]], and expressed their dismay when they found that Hell was a tow-in gate.\n\n==Appearance==\nDJ AM has short black hair with short beard. He wears a blue T-shirt with a yellow disco player design, gray pants and black shoes.
