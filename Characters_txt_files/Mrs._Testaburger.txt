She also wears gold earrings as of [[Season Six]]. Her appearance is similar to [[Ms. Stevens]]', but with a different nose.\n\n<gallery widths=\"160\" spacing=\"small\" caption=\"Images of Mrs. Testaburger\">\nMrsTestaburger2.png|Mrs. Testaburger at the emergency PTA meeting in \"[[Eat, Pray, Queef]]\".\nTMI11.png|Mrs. Testaburger in a meeting with the other parents in \"[[T.M.I.]]\"\n</gallery>\n\n==Family==\n===[[Wendy Testaburger]]===\nMrs. Testaburger seems to care for her daughter a lot as seen in \"[[Bebe's Boobs Destroy Society]]\" where she was worried about Wendy getting breast implants, as she was concerned about the homeless and oppressed [[Randy Marsh]]'s idea to solve the problem in \"[[Night of the Living Homeless]]\" she may be where Wendy gets most of her liberal ideas from.
