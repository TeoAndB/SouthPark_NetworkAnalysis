"{{South Park character\n|name = New York Girl\n|image = New-york-girl.png\n|gender = Female\n|hair = Orange\n|job = Student\n|grade = 3rd Grade\n|appearance = \"[[World Wide Recorder Concert]]\"\n}}\nThe '''New York Girl''' is a member of the [[New York Kids]] who had two physical appearances on the show, the first in the episode \"[[Roger Ebert Should Lay off the Fatty Foods]]\" and the other in \"[[World Wide Recorder Concert]]\", where she is seen with several other kids from New York.\n\nShe and three other New York kids can be seen on some posters at [[Raisins (Location)|Raisins]]. She is also one of several cut characters from ''[[South Park: The Stick of Truth]]''.\n\n==Appearance==\nShe has short curly Orange hair with a Pink headband, gold earrings and wears a Black shirt and light Blue pants.\n\n<gallery widths=\"160\" caption=\"Images of the New York Girl\" spacing=\"small\">\nNewyorkgirlintfbw.jpg.jpg|The girl seen on a picture frame and a poster inside the [[Raisins (Location)|Restaurant]].\n</gallery>\n\n==Prominence==\n*\"[[Roger Ebert Should Lay off the Fatty Foods]]\" - Seen on television during the [[Cheesy Poofs Theme Song]].\n*\"[[World Wide Recorder Concert]]\" - Seen as a New York kid.\n*\"[[Raisins]]\" - Seen on posters at Raisins.\n*\"[[You Got F'd in the A]]\" - Seen on posters at Raisins.\n*\"[[201]]\" - Seen on a poster outside Raisins.\n*\"[[The Pandemic Special]]\" - Seen on a torn poster outside the Raisins.\n*\"[[South ParQ Vaccination Special]]\" - Seen on a torn poster outside the Raisins.\n\n==Trivia==\n*She seems to use the same character model as [[Nancy]] from \"[[Up the Down Steroid]]\".\n*She, [[Smitty]], and two other unnamed boys are the only New York Kids that can be found in the ''[[South Park: The Stick of Truth]]'' game files, the others that appeared in the episode were absent.\n*Coincidentally, she is also the only New York kid besides Smitty and the other two unnamed boys to appear on Raisins posters.\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Unnamed characters]]\n[[Category:Female characters]]\n[[Category:3rd graders]]\n[[Category:School children]]\n[[Category:Gingers]]"
