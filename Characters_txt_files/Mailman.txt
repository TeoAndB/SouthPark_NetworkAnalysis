"{{South Park character\n|name = Mailman\n|image = Mailman Timmy MHCC.png\n|gender = Male\n|race = Caucasian\n|hair = Brown\n|job = Mailman\n|voice = [[Trey Parker]]<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0705944/|title=\"South Park\" Mr. Hankey's Christmas Classics (TV episode 1999)|publisher=IMDb.com}}</ref>\n|appearance = \"[[Mr. Hankey's Christmas Classics]]\"\n}}\nThe '''Mailman''' was seen as an introductory character in the [[Season Three]] episode, \"[[Mr. Hankey's Christmas Classics]]\".\n\n==Background==\nThe Mailman is the opening narrator who parodies the mailman character, Special Delivery Kluger from the Rankin/Bass' ''[[Wikipedia:Santa Claus Is Comin' to Town (film)|Santa Claus is Comin' to Town]]'' stop motion animation holiday special.\n\nThe Mailman sings the \"[[Mr. Hankey, the Christmas Poo (Song)|Mr. Hankey, the Christmas Poo]]\" with various children of South Park in the first song of \"[[Mr. Hankey's Christmas Classics]]\".\n\n==Appearance==\nThe Mailman is a wooden puppet character. He has brown hair, blue eyes, and pink cheeks. He wears a mailman uniform which consists of a mailman's hat, a navy blue jacket with a teal button-up shirt, and navy blue pants with lace up black shoes.\n\n==Trivia==\n* A transcript given in the description of an upload of the scene on the official ''[[South Park]]'' YouTube channel where \"[[Mr. Hankey, the Christmas Poo (Song)|Mr Hankey, the Christmas Poo]]\" is sung in the episode suggests that his name might be \"Cowboy Timmy\".\n\n==References==\n{{Reflist}}\n{{Minor Characters from Season Three}}\n[[es:Cartero]]\n[[zh:圣诞邮差]]\n[[Category:One-off characters]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Male characters]]\n[[Category:Christmas]]\n[[Category:Unnamed characters]]\n[[Category:Adult characters]]\n[[Category:Characters voiced by Trey Parker]]"
