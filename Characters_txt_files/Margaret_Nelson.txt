She first appeared in the [[Season Nineteen]] episode, \"[[The City Part of Town]]\" and died from [[COVID-19]] in the [[South ParQ Vaccination Special|Vaccination Special]].\n\n==Appearance==\nMargaret wore a skirt, usually blue or black, a magenta v-neck shirt, and a white undershirt. She also wore black, flat shoes and a small wristwatch. She had black hair, which she styles in a bun, and black eyelashes. She had the stereotypical characteristics of Asians seen in the series such as small eyes.\n\n==Prominence==\n*\"[[The City Part of Town]]\" - First speaking role as the Fourth Grade teacher.\n*\"[[Truth and Advertising]]\" - Second speaking role.\n*\"[[Skank Hunt]]\" - Seen speaking with police officers in the school hallway.\n*\"[[Douche and a Danish]]\" - Seen trying to teach the class when [[Herbert Garrison|Mr. Garrison]] barges in and tries to teach.\n*\"[[Hummels & Heroin]]\" - Oversees [[Marcus Preston]]'s presentation in the [[South Park Elementary|school]] cafeteria.\n*\"[[SUPER HARD PCness]]\" - Attends school staff meetings.\n*\"[[Dead Kids]]\" - Hands over math exams to the students and explains them again the addition of fractions and later seen being interviewed by police about one of the school shootings.\n*\"[[A Boy And A Priest]]\" - Seen in church.\n*\"[[The Problem with a Poo]]\" - Watches [[Mr. Hankey]] leave [[South Park (Location)|South Park]].\n*\"[[Buddha Box]]\" - Pulls the Buddha Box off [[Eric Cartman]]'s head.\n*\"[[Bike Parade]]\" - Seen watching the bike parade.\n*\"[[Shots|Shots!!!]]\" - Seen at the PTA meeting.\n*\"[[Let Them Eat Goo]]\" - Ate a [[Tegridy Burger]] in the middle of the class.\n*\"[[Tegridy Farms Halloween Special]]\" - Seen at the school assembly.\n*\"[[Turd Burglars]]\" - Seen at the women's meeting at [[Park County Community Center]].\n*\"[[The Pandemic Special]]\" - Taught the class via online meetings.\n*\"[[South ParQ Vaccination Special]]\" - She dies from COVID-19 before getting the [[COVID-19 Vaccine|vaccine]].
