It is modeled after deceased singer-dancer [[Michael Jackson]].\n\n==Background==\nThe Michael Jackson Hologram is a computer program designed by [[Syntech Hologram Company]]. While performing at the [[Pepsi Center]] Women of Rock live concert, the hologram breaks free and attempts to return to [[South Park (Location)|South Park]].\n\n==Personality==\nThe Michael Jackson Hologram appears to be a sentient being with emotions and memories of its real-life counterpart Michael Jackson. This hologram also likes to use the word \"ignorant\", much like his real-life counterpart in the show.\n\n==Appearance==\nThe hologram looks just like Michael Jackson, complete with black hair and a pale face due to plastic surgery.
