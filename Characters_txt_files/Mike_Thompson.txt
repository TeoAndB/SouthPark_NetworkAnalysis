Her mother brings her to a ranch to choose a pig. Mike holds up a few pigs for her to choose from.\n\n==Appearance==\nMike has long gray hair, thick eyebrows, various warts on his face, and an unshaven beard. He seems to wear a red shirt with the sleeves up (which reveals the fur on his arms), a black belt, gray pants and black boots.
