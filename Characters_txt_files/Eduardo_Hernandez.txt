"{{South Park character\n|name = Eduardo Hernandez\n|image = Townsfolk_eduardo_hernandez.png\n|gender = Male\n|race = Latino\n|hair = Black\n|job = Grocery Store Clerk\n|appearance = \"[[Members Only]]\"\n}}\n'''Eduardo Hernandez''' is a grocery store clerk, originally from Guatemala, who appears in the [[Season Twenty]] episode, \"[[Members Only]]\".\n\n==Background==\nIn \"[[Members Only]]\", President [[Herbert Garrison|Garrison]] is back in town and is lashing out at those who have wronged him in the past. Eduardo Hernandez had once told Garrison that he could not double-bag his groceries in a previous shopping trip. Garrison singles out Hernandez at the store and berates him.\n\n==Appearance==\nEduardo Hernandez is a middle-aged Hispanic man who wears a white polo shirt, a blue apron, and a blue hat. His apron has his name tag affixed to it with his first name. He has black eyebrows and a black mustache.\n\n{{Minor Characters from Season Twenty}}\n[[zh:爱德华多·赫尔南德斯]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Male characters]]\n[[Category:Adult characters]]\n[[Category:Hispanic characters]]\n[[Category:Minority characters]]\n[[Category:One-off characters]]"
