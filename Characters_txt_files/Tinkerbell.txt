She made only one appearance in the [[Season Eight]] episode \"[[Stupid Spoiled Whore Video Playset]]\". \n\n==Background==\nIn Hilton's limo, Tinkerbell uses Hilton's cellphone as a distraction while she gets the driver's revolver, the gun which she uses to commit suicide with. Afterward, Tinkerbell is \"replaced\" by Mr. Biggles ([[Butters Stotch|Butters]]).\n\n==Appearance==\nTinkerbell has dark brown fur with a light blue bow tie, a teal gemstone necklace, and dog shoes.
