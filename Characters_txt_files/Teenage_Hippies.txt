"{{South Park character\n|name = Teenage Hippies\n|image = Teenage Hippies.png\n|gender = Male\n|race = Caucasian\n|hair = Brown/blond\n|voice = [[Matt Stone]]<br>[[Trey Parker]]<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0705934/fullcredits?ref_=tt_cl_sm#cast|title=\"South Park\" Ike's Wee Wee (TV episode 1998)|publisher=IMDb.com}}</ref>\n|appearance = \"[[Ike's Wee Wee]]\"\n}}\nThe '''Teenage Hippies''' first appear in the [[Season Two]] episode, \"[[Ike's Wee Wee]]\".\n\n==Background==\nThese two hippies encounter [[Mr. Mackey]] in the woods and remember him as the counselor who kicked them out of [[South Park Elementary|school]] for smoking weed in the bathroom. They then offer LSD to Mr. Mackey who turns his head into a \"balloon\" upon consumption, floating around town. They were later seen in the [[Betty Ford Clinic]] watching Teletubbies on television while awaiting treatment for their drug addiction.\n\nThey have occasionally reappeared alongside other hippies.\n\n==Appearance==\nThe skinnier hippie wears a small white shirt bearing the words \"Legalize Everything\" with blue shorts and black shoes. The other appears overweight and wears a rainbow shirt tie-dyed with tattered blue shorts and brown slippers.\n\nBoth have long brown/blond hair and a few more strands of hair on their chin.\n\n==Prominence==\n*\"[[Ike's Wee Wee]]\" - They offer LSD to Mr. Mackey\n*\"[[Roger Ebert Should Lay off the Fatty Foods]]\"\n*\"[[Fun with Veal]]\" - Seen with the [[Hippies|hippies]] outside [[Marsh Residence|Stan's house]]\n*\"[[I'm a Little Bit Country]]\"\n\n==References==\n{{Reflist}}\n{{Minor Characters from Season Two}}\n[[zh:青年嬉皮士]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Characters voiced by Matt Stone]]\n[[Category:Characters voiced by Trey Parker]]\n[[Category:Adult characters]]\n[[Category:Male characters]]\n[[Category:Unnamed characters]]\n[[Category:Minor characters]]"
