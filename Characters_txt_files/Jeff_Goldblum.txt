Jeff Goldblum also makes a cameo in the episode, \"[[Britney's New Look]]\", where he can be seen right behind [[Gerald Broflovski|Gerald]] and [[Sheila Broflovski]] as they make their way into the front of the crowd of photographers.\n\n==Appearance==\nAs he appears on South Park, Jeff Goldblum wears a white lab coat with his name tag over a formal red shirt. His name tag reads Dr. Goldbloom. He also wears a gray undershirt, round-framed glasses, and has black hair.\n\n==Personality==\nWhile he is portrayed as intelligent and inquisitive, being heavily interested in researching extraterrestrial life, he relies on a sort of strange circular logic to solve problems.\n\n==Prominence==\n*\"[[Cancelled]]\"\n*\"[[Britney's New Look]]\"\n*\"[[Skank Hunt]]\" - Mentioned by the [[Memberberries]]\n\n==References==\n{{Reflist}}\n{{Minor Characters from Season Seven}}\n{{MovieCelebrities}}\n[[ja:ジェフ・ゴールドブラム]]\n[[zh:杰夫·高布伦]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Male characters]]\n[[Category:Minor characters]]\n[[Category:Characters voiced by Matt Stone]]\n[[Category:Jewish characters]]\n[[Category:Movie celebrities]]\n[[Category:Elderly people]]"
