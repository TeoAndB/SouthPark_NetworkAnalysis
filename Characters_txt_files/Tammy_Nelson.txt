"{{South Park character\n|name = Tammy Nelson\n|image = Tammy.png\n|gender = Female\n|hair = Black\n|age = 9-10\n|job = Student\n|religion = Roman Catholic\n|grade = [[The 4th Grade|4th Grade]]\n|voice = [[April Stewart]]<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.imdb.com/title/tt2079298/|title=\"South Park\" Bass to Mouth (TV Episode 2011) - IMDb|publisher=IMDb.com}}</ref>\n|appearance = \"[[Quest for Ratings]]\"\n|speaking = \"[[Bass to Mouth]]\"\n}}{{LinkDisambig|Tammy (Disambiguation)}}\n{{LinkDisambig|Nelson (Disambiguation)}}\n'''Tammy Nelson''' is a student at  [[South Park Elementary]]. She is mostly seen in the corridors in the school. Her first speaking role was in \"[[Bass to Mouth]]\", where she admitted to [[Emily]] that her mother was an alcoholic. Her name was confirmed in \"[[Bass to Mouth]]\".\n\n==Appearance==\nTammy has long black hair with bangs. She wears a light-purple jacket (similar to [[Wendy Testaburger]]'s) and dark-purple pants along with a purple hair tie. She bears a very close resemblance to [[Beth]].\n\n<gallery widths=\"160\" captionalign=\"left\" caption=\"Images of Tammy Nelson\">\nWendyMarjorine.png|Tammy seen in \"[[Marjorine]]\".\n</gallery>\n\n==Relationships==\n===[[Emily]]===\n[[File:Tammy&emily.png|thumb|Tammy comforting Emily over her issues.]]\nIt was seen in \"[[Bass to Mouth]]\" that her and [[Emily]] are very close friends, probably best friends, considering they both admitted and comforted each other over their mothers being addicts/alcoholics, which is an extremely personal matter. They are also seen sitting together in the school gymnasium in \"[[Cock Magic]]\". At the Board Girls club, they are playing a Marvel game together.\n\n===[[Nichole]]===\nWhen Nichole and Tammy join the boys' board game club, they have prepared characters for Dungeons & Dragons together beforehand.
