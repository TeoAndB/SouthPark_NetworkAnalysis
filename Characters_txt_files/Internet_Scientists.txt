"{{Group\n|name = Internet Scientists\n|image = Internet scientists.png\n|job = Internet Workers\n|gender = Male\n|first = \"[[Over Logging]]\"\n|times = 1\n}}\nThe '''Internet Scientists''' are a group of workers who maintain the internet. They appear in the [[Season Twelve]] episode, \"[[Over Logging]]\".\n\n==Background==\nFollowing nationwide shortage of internet, the US military pays a visit to the internet facility to find out what had happened. The scientist on-site presents to them a colossal internet router with a flashing orange light, and explains that it should have a solid green light to denote a working internet. A few other scientists attempt to communicate with the router to no avail.\n\nThe internet was eventually restored when [[Kyle Broflovski|Kyle]] pulls out the router's plug, then plugging back in.\n\n==Appearance==\nThe lead scientist has a messy brown hair and several freckles on his face. He wears a blue collared shirt with a \"Tech support\" tag and brown pants, and a pair of rectangle-framed glasses.\n\nOther workers wear a full yellow body suit with black gloves and the \"Tech support\" tag, white lab coats with protective goggles, or gray coats with gray helmets.\n\n==Prominence==\n*\"[[Over Logging]]\"\n*\"[[Cock Magic]]\" - The lead scientist is seen spectating a ''[[Magic: The Gathering (CCG)|Magic: The Gathering]]'' match between [[Kenny McCormick|Kenny]] and [[Slaughterhouse]].\n\n{{Minor Characters from Season Twelve}}\n[[zh:网络科学家]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Male characters]]\n[[Category:One-off characters]]\n[[Category:Adult characters]]"
