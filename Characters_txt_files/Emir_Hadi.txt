He appears in the [[Season Eleven]] episode \"[[D-Yikes!]]\".\n\n==Background==\nEmir comes with his crew (or gang of Persians) to [[Les Bos]] to inform the costumers that the bar was purchased by the Club Persh chain.\n\nHe tries to calm the Lesbian women by telling them that they'll be still welcomed and tries to reason with them, only to get physically harmed by [[Janet Garrison]], who leaves him throwing up on the floor after kicking his groin.\n\nHe is evacuated back to his workplace where he notifies his superior, [[Tumon]], of the occurring 30 lesbians' revolt.\n\n==Appearance==\nEmir is a Persian male with a five o'clock shadow and hairs between his two eyebrows.
