"{{South Park character\n|name = Enchorito Mark\n|image = AdultMark.png\n|gender = Male\n|hair = Brown\n|race = Caucasian\n|died = ''[[South Park: The Stick of Truth]]'' (Revived)\n|appearance = \"[[The China Probrem]]\"\n}}\n{{LinkDisambig|Mark (Disambiguation)}}\n'''Enchorito Mark''' is a resident of [[South Park (Location)|South Park]], who usually appears as a [[City People|background character]]. Currently, Mark's most notable appearances were in the game ''[[South Park: The Stick of Truth]]'' and the [[Season Nineteen]] episode \"[[You're Not Yelping]]\".\n\n==Appearance==\nEnchorito Mark wears a dark orange shirt under a dark blue jacket, dark blue pants, and black shoes. He has dark brown hair and thick eyebrows. Mark has no other distinguishable characteristics.\n\n==Trivia==\n*Although Enchorito Mark was killed in ''[[South Park: The Stick of Truth]]'', he still appears as a recurring background character in any episodes after the game's release.\n*During the development of South Park: The Stick of Truth, Mark and his wife were supposed to have unique character models, but later their models were changed to generic background characters.
