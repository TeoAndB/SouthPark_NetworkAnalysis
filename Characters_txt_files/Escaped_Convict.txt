"{{South Park character\n|name = Escaped Convict\n|image = Adults-other-fictitious-escaped-convict.png\n|gender = Male\n|race = British\n|hair = Bald\n|died = \"[[Pip]]\"\n|voice = [[Trey Parker]]<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0705929/|title=\"South Park\" Pip (TV episode 2000)|publisher=IMDb.com}}</ref>\n|appearance = \"[[Pip]]\"\n|final = \"[[Pip]]\"\n}}\nThe '''Escaped Convict''' is a convict who appears in the episode, \"[[Pip]]\". After escaping from prison, he threatens [[Pip Pirrip|Pip]] in a graveyard, but Pip gives him a sandwich and cuts off his handcuffs. Touched by Pip's display of unconditional kindness, he moves to Wales and becomes a millionaire.\n\nAfterwards, he repays [[Pip Pirrip|Pip]]'s favor by sending him to become a gentleman in London. He later tries to help Pip stop [[Miss Havisham]], but is killed when she spits acid on his face.\n\n==Appearance==\nHe has an irregularly-shaped face, badly-kept stubble, thick and bushy eyebrows and a prominent forehead. He initially wears a loose brown shirt and blue trousers, but later dons a morning coat, grey waistcoat and white shirt with white gloves when he becomes a millionaire.\n\n<gallery widths=\"160\" spacing=\"small\" caption=\"Images of the escaped convict\">\nPip007.png|The convict threatening [[Pip Pirrip|Pip]] in the graveyard.\nPip062.png|The gentleman sends [[Pip Pirrip|Pip]] to become a gentleman in London.</gallery>\n\n==References==\n{{Reflist}}\n{{Minor Characters from Season Four}}\n[[es:Convicto Escapado]]\n[[zh:阿贝尔·马格维奇]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Deceased characters]]\n[[Category:One-off characters]]\n[[Category:Male characters]]\n[[Category:Adult characters]]\n[[Category:British characters]]\n[[Category:Characters voiced by Trey Parker]]\n[[Category:Characters with deviations]]"
