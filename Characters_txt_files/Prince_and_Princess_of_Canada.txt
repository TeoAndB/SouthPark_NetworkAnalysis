"{{South Park character\n|name = Prince and Princess of Canada\n|image = Prince-and-princess-of-canada.png\n|gender = '''Prince''': Male<br>'''Princess''': Female\n|race = Caucasian\n|hair = '''Prince''': Blond<br>'''Princess''': Brown\n|job = Canadian Royalty\n|voice = '''Princess''': [[Trey Parker]]<ref name=imdb>{{cite web|url=http://www.imdb.com/title/tt1913934/|title=\"South Park\" Royal Pudding (TV episode 2011)|publisher=IMDb.com}}</ref><br>\n'''Prince''': [[Trey Parker]]<ref name=imdb/>\n|appearance = \"[[Royal Pudding]]\"\n}}\nThe '''Prince and Princess of Canada''' were seen in [[Season Fifteen]] episode, \"[[Royal Pudding]]\". They are both parodies of the real-life [[wikipedia:Prince William, Duke of Cambridge|Prince William]] and [[wikipedia:Catherine, Duchess of Cambridge|Catherine]], the Duke and Duchess of Cambridge in the United Kingdom. The princess returns in \"[[South Park: The Stick of Truth]]\", with the prince having a bigger role. The prince returns as well in [[Season Eighteen]] episode, \"[[Freemium Isn't Free]]\".\n\n==Background==\nThe prince and princess were seen performing the traditional Canadian Royal Wedding ceremony in \"[[Royal Pudding]]\"; the ceremony is a parody of the British Royal Wedding. During the first part of the ceremony, the princess was abducted by an energy cube that crashed the wedding. [[Ike Broflovski|Ike]], [[Scott the Dick]], and [[Ugly Bob]] go after her, only to find out that the culprit was a hideous monster known as [[Tooth Decay]]. In the end, the princess was rescued and the ceremony was resumed.\n\nThe prince gets a major role in \"[[Freemium Isn't Free]]\", where he allows the [[Canadian Minister of Mobile Gaming]] to create a mobile phone app that helps pay to rebuild Canada better by making a game free and addicting. He finds out this was caused by the deeds of the Canadian Devil, in which [[Satan]] helped rescue Canada. He tells the people of Canada that he learns his lesson and promises to never make Freemium games.\n\n==Appearance==\n[[File:Prince and Princess of Canada in TSOT.jpg|thumb|The Prince and Princess of Canada in ''The Stick of Truth''.]]\n===Prince===\nThe prince wears a military dress uniform, which consists of a red coat with lots of buttons, a large silver medal, a yellow sash, a blue belt, and black pants with gray stripes. He has combed, blond hair.\n===Princess===\nThe princess wears a traditional white wedding dress, a white veil, sterling tear-drop earrings, and a small white vest. She wears a conservative amount of makeup and can be seen holding a bouquet of daisies. She has short brown hair. At the end of \"[[Royal Pudding]]\", one of her arms is removed by the prince.\n\nIn ''[[South Park: The Stick of Truth]]'', she appears with a lilac silk dress, a pearly-white headband, a gold necklace with three emeralds, a white bracelet and black pumped heels. She has curly-tipped hair with a bang and her arm is back into place.\n\n==Prominence==\n*\"[[Royal Pudding]]\" - Both are seen at their Royal Canadian Wedding.\n*\"[[Freemium Isn't Free]]\" - The prince allows the [[Canadian Minister of Mobile Gaming]] to create a freemium app based on [[Canada]].\n\n===''[[South Park: The Stick of Truth]]''===\nThe prince and princess appear in the game, when the [[Douchebag|New Kid]] arrives in Ottawa, Canada. The prince has imprisoned the Minister of Montreal in a cave and sends the New Kid to get the [[Bishop of Banff]]'s testicles to convince him to release the Minister. After obtaining the testicles, or a Dire Pig's to deceive, he goes back on the deal and instead friends the New Kid on [[Facebook]].
