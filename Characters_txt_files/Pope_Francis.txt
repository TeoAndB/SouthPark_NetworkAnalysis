Following the resignation of [[Pope Benedict XVI]] in 2013, Pope Francis became promoted as the head of the Vatican church.\n\n==Background==\nIn \"[[The Hobbit]]\", Pope Francis appeared to receive the honor of ''Time'' magazine's \"Person of the Year\" for 2013. Upon receiving the award, he is interrupted by [[Kanye West]], who tries to convince the audience that [[Kardashians|Kim Kardashian]] is not a hobbit. As the Pope tries to correct him, Kanye pushes him aside.\n\n==Appearance==\nPope Francis has thinning, gray hair and a wrinkled face.
