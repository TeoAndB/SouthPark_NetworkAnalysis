"{{South Park character\n|name = Sia\n|image = Sia.png\n|aliases = Blonde Girl\n|gender = Female\n|hair = Blonde\n|job = Student\n|voice = [[Mona Marshall]]<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.imdb.com/title/tt4080138/|title=\"South Park\" The Cissy (TV episode 2014)|publisher=IMDb.com}}</ref>\n|appearance = \"[[The Cissy]]\"\n}}\n'''Sia''' is a student at [[South Park Elementary]] who first appears in the [[Season Eighteen]] episode, \"[[The Cissy]]\". Although no other character refers to her by name in the episode, her voice credit is listed as \"Sia\".\n\n==Background==\nShe appears in \"[[The Cissy]]\", holding her nose and begging [[Eric Cartman]] to leave the [[South Park Elementary Bathrooms|girls' bathroom]].\n\n==Appearance==\nShe has very pale short blonde hair with bangs, and wears a short black jacket over a black-and-white striped shirt, with a red skirt. Her hairstyle (and name) may be a homage to singer Sia, who provides [[Lorde]]'s singing voice in the same episode.\n\n==Personality==\nSia exhibits frustration and anger at Cartman's refusal to leave the girls' bathroom.\n\n==References==\n{{Reflist}}\n{{Minor Characters from Season Eighteen}}\n{{South Park Children}}\n[[zh:希雅]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Female characters]]\n[[Category:School children]]\n[[Category:One-off characters]]"
