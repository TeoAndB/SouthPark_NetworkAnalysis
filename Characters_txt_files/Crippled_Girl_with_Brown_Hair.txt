"{{South Park character\n|name = Crippled Girl with Brown Hair\n|image = special_education_lisa.png\n|gender = Female\n|hair = Brown\n|job = Student\n|appearance = \"[[Up the Down Steroid]]\"\n}}\nThe '''Crippled Girl with Brown Hair''' is a handicapped girl who studies at [[South Park Elementary]]. She made her first appearance on \"[[Up the Down Steroid]]\" and later appeared on the episodes \"[[Crippled Summer]]\" and \"[[Moss Piglets]]\". For the time being she has played no notable role.\n\n==Appearance==\nShe has long brown hair, brown eyebrows and is usually seen looking up. She also uses a gray wheelchair. In \"[[Crippled Summer]]\" she wore a standard red Tardicaca Camp shirt, a purple skirt and pink sandals, outside the camp she appears to be wearing a light pink dress. She seems to have exotropy.\n\n==Prominence==\n*\"[[Up the Down Steroid]]\" - Seen training for the special Olympics.\n*\"[[Crippled Summer]]\" - Seen as a member of the [[Red Team]].\n*\"[[Moss Piglets]]\" - Seen at the science fair.\n{{South Park Children}}\n[[fr:Crippled Girl with Brown Hair]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Unnamed characters]]\n[[Category:Female characters]]\n[[Category:Characters with deviations]]\n[[Category:School children]]\n[[Category:Townsfolk]]\n[[Category:Tardicaca campers]]\n[[Category:Background characters]]"
