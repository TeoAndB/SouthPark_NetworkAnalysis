"{{South Park character\n|name = Dr. Schwartz\n|image = Rabbi-schwartz.png\n|gender = Male\n|hair = Brown\n|job = Jewish Rabbi\n|religion = Judaism\n|voice = [[Trey Parker]]<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0705934/|title=\"South Park\" Ike's Wee Wee (TV episode 1998)|publisher=IMDb.com}}</ref>\n|appearance = \"[[Ike's Wee Wee]]\"\n}}\n'''Dr. Schwartz''' is a well-meaning Jewish Rabbi, who debuts in the [[Season Two]] episode, \"[[Ike's Wee Wee]]\", as the rabbi that performed [[Ike Broflovski|Ike]]'s bris.\n\n==Background==\nDr. Schwartz appears again in \"[[The Passion of the Jew]]\" and \"[[The Death of Eric Cartman]]\", although with no speaking parts. He also appears during a crowd scene in \"[[Britney's New Look]]\".\n\nIn \"[[Chickenpox]]\", [[Sheila Broflovski|Kyle's mom]] calls [[Kyle Broflovski|Kyle]]'s doctor \"Dr. Schwartz\", suggesting they may be the same person. It is unknown if Dr. Schwartz is related in any way to [[Kyle Schwartz]].\n\n==Appearance==\nDr. Schwartz is overweight and wears gold rimmed glasses. He has brown hair with a beard. He wears a dark blue suit with green trousers. Underneath his sweater, he wears a white shirt and a black tie.\n\n==Prominence==\n*\"[[Ike's Wee Wee]]\" - Comes to Ike's bris to perform his circumcision.\n*\"[[The Passion of the Jew]]\"\n*\"[[The Death of Eric Cartman]]\" - Seen at the Synagogue, when Cartman gives them a fruit basket.\n*\"[[Britney's New Look]]\" - Seen among the crowd taking pictures of [[Britney Spears]].\n*\"[[Jewpacabra]]\" (mentioned only)\n\n==References==\n{{reflist}}\n{{Minor Characters from Season Two}}\n[[zh:施瓦兹拉比]]\n[[Category:Jewish characters]]\n[[Category:Male characters]]\n[[Category:Adult characters]]\n[[Category:Townsfolk]]\n[[Category:Minor characters]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Characters voiced by Trey Parker]]\n[[Category:Overweight characters]]\n[[Category:Middle Eastern characters]]\n[[Category:Religious characters]]"
