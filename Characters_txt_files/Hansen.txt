"{{South Park character\n|name = Rescuer Hansen\n|image = Rescuer-hansen.png\n|gender = Male\n|race = Caucasian\n|hair = Brown\n|job = SWAT Rescuer\n|appearance = \"[[Fourth Grade]]\"\n}}\n'''Hansen''' is a SWAT member who appears in [[Season Four]] episode \"[[Fourth Grade]]\".\n\n==Background==\nIn an attempt to travel back to third grade, [[The Boys|the boys]] seek help from the [[Star Trek Nerds]] to build a time travelling device onto [[Timmy Burch]]'s wheelchair, but the plan backfired when the device went out of control, causing Timmy's wheelchair to speed up. Knowing that the device would explode if the wheelchair slows down, a SWAT helicopter was deployed with Hansen attempting to disable the device, only to have accidentally carried a woman on board. Despite the difficulties, Hansen managed to fix the device at the last few seconds, creating a time portal as designed, but sending Timmy back to pre-history. \n\n==Appearance==\nHansen appears to have brown hair, and wears the SWAT uniform with black headgear and harness.\n\n{{Minor Characters from Season Four}}\n[[zh:营救员汉森]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Male characters]]\n[[Category:Adult characters]]\n[[Category:One-off characters]]"
