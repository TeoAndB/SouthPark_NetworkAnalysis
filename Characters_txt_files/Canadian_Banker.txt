"{{South Park character\n|image = Canadian Banker.png\n|name = Canadian Banker\n|gender = Male\n|hair = Brown\n|job = Banker\n|appearance = ''[[South Park: The Stick of Truth]]''\n}}\nThe '''Canadian Banker''' is a minor character in ''[[South Park: The Stick of Truth]]''.\n\n==Appearance==\nLike most Canadians, he has beady eyes and a flapping head.
