"{{South Park character\n|name = Sister Hollis\n|image = SH.png\n|gender = Female\n|race = Caucasian\n|hair = Brown\n|job = Missionary\n|religion = Christian\n|voice = Michelle Ann Young<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0705963/|title=\"South Park\" Starvin' Marvin in Space (TV episode 1999)|publisher=IMDb.com}}</ref>\n|appearance = \"[[Starvin' Marvin in Space]]\"\n}}\n'''Sister Hollis''' is a Christian missionary seen in the [[Season Three]] episode, \"[[Starvin' Marvin in Space]]\".\n\n==Background==\nSister Hollis is a member of [[CBC Network|Pat Robertson's 600 Club]], and is a Christian missionary in Africa. She uses food to bribe and convert the native [[Ethiopian Tribe]] to the Christian faith. When [[Starvin' Marvin (Character)|Starvin' Marvin]] takes his people to [[Marklar (Planet)|Marklar]] in his Marklar spaceship, she pursues them with the 600 Club's XT-9000 spaceship.\n\n==Appearance==\nSister Hollis wears black shoes, a black skirt, and a white shirt. She has long brown hair, which has been pulled back. Her jewelry includes a black crucifix necklace.\n\n==Personality==\nSister Hollis is determined to convert the Ethiopians to Christianity, even going so far as chasing then in outer space. She is devoted to [[Pat Robertson]] so much, she is willing to murder for him.\n\n==References==\n{{Reflist}}\n{{Minor Characters from Season Three}}\n[[zh:霍利斯修女]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:One-off characters]]\n[[Category:Female characters]]\n[[Category:Adult characters]]\n[[Category:Unnamed characters]]\n[[Category:Religious characters]]"
