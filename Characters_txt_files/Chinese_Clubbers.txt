On the news, scientists have pinpointed the virus to a certain bat in Wuhan, [[China]], but do not understand is how the virus could have spread from a bat to humans. Upon seeing this news report, Randy has a flashback to his time in China in the [[Band in China|previous season]]. He and [[Mickey Mouse]] went clubbing, during which they sat at a table with female Chinese clubbers. There were male and female Chinese clubbers at other tables. Mickey saw a [[bat]] at another table and exclaimed how \"hot\" she was.
