Her first appearance is in the [[Season Fifteen]] episode, \"[[Ass Burgers]]\", replacing [[Nurse Gollum]]. \n\n==Background==\nIn \"[[Ass Burgers]]\", [[Eric Cartman]] visits her office because he feels he has [[Asperger Syndrome|Asperger syndrome]] when in reality, he mistakes it for \"Ass Burgers\" syndrome. Much to Nurse Bonski's dismay, she finds wrapped hamburgers in Eric's underwear and promptly sends him back to class. \n\n==Appearance==\nNurse Bronski's attire is much different than that of Nurse Gollum's. She wears a gray-blue shirt over a white nurse's coat. Her coat has a pocket, which contains various medical tools, and it also has red medical cross on the right side. She is slightly overweight and has long, straight brown hair.\n\n==Prominence==\n*\"[[Ass Burgers]]\" - Examined Cartman in her office and finds hamburgers in his underwear.\n*\"[[Bass to Mouth]]\" - Voice heard in the background.\n*\"[[Taming Strange]]\" - Seen briefly in the background.\n*\"[[SUPER HARD PCness]]\" - Seen in the faculty lounge when [[Strong Woman]] introduces herself.\n\n==References==\n{{Reflist}}\n{{Minor Characters from Season Fifteen}}\n[[zh:布朗斯基女士]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Minor characters]]\n[[Category:Female characters]]\n[[Category:Overweight characters]]\n[[Category:Adult characters]]\n[[Category:Townsfolk]]\n[[Category:School faculty]]\n[[Category:Characters voiced by April Stewart]]\n[[Category:Medics]]"
