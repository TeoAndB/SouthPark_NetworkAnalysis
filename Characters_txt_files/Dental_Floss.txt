"{{South Park character\n|image = Dental Floss.png\n|name = Dental Floss\n|gender = Male\n|race = Caucasian\n|hair = Black\n|job = Student\n|grade = [[Kindergartners|Kindergartner]]\n|voice = Alexandra Meyer<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.imdb.com/title/tt1913934/fullcredits?ref_=tt_cl_sm#cast|title=\"South Park\" Royal Pudding (2011)|publisher=IMDb.com}}</ref>\n|appearance = \"[[Royal Pudding]]\"\n}}\n'''Dental Floss''' is an alter-ego of an unnamed kindergartener. He appears in [[Season Fifteen]] episode \"[[Royal Pudding]]\".\n\n==Background==\nDental Floss was part of a concert on tooth decay. When [[Mr. Mackey]] asked on the whereabouts of [[Ike Broflovski]], Dental Floss replied that Ike was watching the royal wedding of the [[Prince and Princess of Canada]] at [[Broflovski Residence|home]].\n\nIke was eventually replaced by his brother [[Kyle Broflovski|Kyle]]. However, in one rehearsal, when Dental Floss along with the rest of the crew were blaming Kyle for the alleged poor performance, Mr. Mackey vented his anger onto Dental Floss for being a star of the show instead.\n\n==Appearance==\nDental Floss has short black hair and appears to be wearing a red shirt, small green mittens, and black shoes. As part of his costume, he wears a large green box with a headcover on his head, and a string of rope as thin filaments attached on the side of the costume, imitating a box of dental floss. The name is capitalized and printed in black Comic Sans font at the front.\n\n==References==\n{{reflist}}\n\n[[zh:牙线]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:School children]]\n[[Category:Male characters]]\n[[Category:Kindergartners]]\n[[Category:One-off characters]]\n[[Category:Unnamed characters]]"
