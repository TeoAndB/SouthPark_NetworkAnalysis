After [[Butters Stotch]] attacks [[Dr. Mehmet Oz|Dr. Oz]], Jabs becomes angry at [[Stan Marsh|Stan]] for giving an impression that Butters was a violent psychopath.\n\nHe dismisses Stan so he can use the restroom, only to find [[Jesus Christ]] waiting for him, who talks about hell until Jabs starts to cry. Jabs later backed out of the bully video project, due to the immense criticism involved after Butters was deemed to be a violent psychopath.\n\n==Appearance==\nNick Jabs wears black shoes, tan pants with a brown belt, a teal button-up shirt, and a gray business coat.
