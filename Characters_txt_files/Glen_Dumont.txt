"{{South Park character\n|name = Glen Dumont\n|image = Glen-dumont.png\n|aliases = Mike from Lakewood\n|gender = Male\n|race = Caucasian\n|hair = Red\n|job = Negotiator\n|voice = [[Trey Parker]]<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0759312/|title=\"South Park\" Fun with Veal (TV Episode 2002)|publisher=IMDb.com}}</ref>\n|appearance = \"[[Fun with Veal]]\"\n}}\n'''Glen Dumont''' is an [[Federal Bureau of Investigation|F.B.I.]] negotiator who appears in the [[Season Six]] episode, \"[[Fun with Veal]]\". His job was to negotiate with [[The Boys|the boys]] to have them give back the stolen calves. He gives the boys weapons, changes the meaning of \"veal\" to \"tortured baby cow\", and gave them a cattle truck to escape to Mexico driven by [[Michael Dorn]] in full Worf makeup.\n\n==Appearance==\nHe has red hair and red eyebrows. He wears glasses and an F.B.I. jacket over a red vest, a white collared shirt with a teal tie, and dark blue pants.\n\n==Personality==\nHe seems confident in his negotiation skills, thinking he will be able to earn the boys trust with meeting [[Eric Cartman]]'s demands.
