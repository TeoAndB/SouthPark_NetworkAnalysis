"{{South Park character\n|name = Mr. McArthur\n|image = Mr. McArthur.png\n|gender = Male\n|race = Caucasian\n|hair = Dark Brown\n|job = [[City Council]] Member\n|voice=[[Matt Stone]]<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.imdb.com/title/tt6647832/|title=\"South Park\" Christmas Snow (TV Episode 2019)|publisher=IMDb.com}}</ref>\n|relation1 = Wife\n|relativename1 = [[Moira McArthur]]\n|relation2 = Daughter\n|relativename2 = [[Red McArthur]]\n|appearance = \"[[The China Probrem]]\"\n|speaking = \"[[Christmas Snow]]\"\n}}\n'''Mr. McArthur''' is [[Red McArthur]]'s father. He makes his first appearance in [[Season Twelve]]'s \"[[The China Probrem]]\".\n\n==Background==\nHe first appears as a background character who makes several cameos, starting with \"[[The China Probrem]]\" when he appears in [[Kyle Broflovski|Kyle]]'s nightmare. He is one of many in a line seen watching [[Tiger Woods]] play golf in \"[[Sexual Healing]]\". In \"[[The Problem with a Poo]]\", he appears in this episode at a [[City Council]] meeting where the council decides to cut the budget of [[Mr. Hankey]]'s Christmas pageant by half.\n\n\"[[Christmas Snow]]\" marks the first time Mr. McArthur speaks. He tells [[Santa Claus|Santa]] that they are trying to have fun when he talks about the dangers of drunk driving at the tree lighting ceremony.\n\nHe is revealed to be Red's father \"[[The Pandemic Special]]\". While the students are quarantined in [[South Park Elementary|school]] for two weeks, they are only given five minutes to have Zoom calls with their parents in the computer lab. Red is seen on a call with her parents. Mr. McArthur tells her that they miss her and cannot wait for quarantine to be over.\n\nOnly [[Moira McArthur|his wife]] attends the parent-teacher Zoom call with [[Mr. Mackey]] earlier in the episode.\n\n==Appearance==\nMr. McArthur has dark brown hair. He wears a brown v-neck pullover with a light brown neckline, under which he wears a brown collared shirt.
