"{{South Park character\n|name = Nobunaga Hiroichi\n|image = Nobunaga-hiroichi.png\n|gender = Male\n|race = Japanese\n|hair = Black and Gray\n|job = SNN Japanese Correspondent\n|voice = [[Trey Parker]]<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0766077/|title=\"South Park\" A Ladder to Heaven (TV episode 2002)|publisher=IMDb.com}}</ref>\n|appearance = \"[[A Ladder to Heaven]]\"\n}}\n'''Nobunaga Hiroichi''' is a Japanese correspondent for the SNN news channel who appears in the [[Season Six]] episode, \"[[A Ladder to Heaven]]\".\n\n==Background==\nWhen [[Eric Cartman|Cartman]], [[Stan Marsh|Stan]], and [[Kyle Broflovski|Kyle]] build a massive ladder to [[Heaven]] so they can get tickets to [[Lolly's Candy Factory]] from [[Kenny McCormick|Kenny]], the Japanese claim to have already reached Heaven with their own ladder to poke fun of the United States, which turns out to be a studio in Japan decorated to look like Heaven. Despite this, the boys parents still believe the Japanese reached Heaven before them.\n\n==Appearance==\nNobunaga has combed black hair that appears to be graying, thin black eyebrows, and a small black mustache. He wears a dark blue suit, a white undershirt, a red tie, black pants, and black shoes.
