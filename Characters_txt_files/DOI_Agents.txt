"{{South Park character\n|name = DOI Agents\n|image = Dept-of-the-interior.png\n|gender = Male\n|job = DOI Workers\n|voice = [[Trey Parker]]<br>[[Matt Stone]]<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0705937/|title=\"South Park\" Jakovasaurs (TV Episode 1999)|publisher=IMDb.com}}</ref>\n|appearance = \"[[Jakovasaurs]]\"\n}}\nThe '''DOI Agents''' are representatives of the United States Department of Interior who appear in [[Season Three]] episode \"[[Jakovasaurs]]\".\n\n==Background==\nUpon discovery of a female [[Jakovasaur]], it was taken to the [[City Hall]] and presented to three Department of Interior agents. The agents claimed that a live Jakovasaur had never been seen, and requested that it gets a chance at repopulation, granting a Jakovasaur couple to a [[Jakov Residence|new home]] where it can produce offspring.\n\nUpon realizing that the Jakovasaur population had grown out of control, the agents decided to flee [[South Park (Location)|South Park]], but not before appointing [[Eric Cartman]] an official member of the DOI, granting him authority over all wildlife in South Park.\n\n==Appearance==\nEach of the agents wear working suits of different colors, and a pair of black square-framed glasses.\n\n==References==\n{{Reflist}}\n\n{{Minor Characters from Season Three}}\n[[zh:美国内政部]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Male characters]]\n[[Category:One-off characters]]\n[[Category:Adult characters]]\n[[Category:Characters voiced by Matt Stone]]"
