He was on the [[Blue Team]] along with [[Jimmy]], [[Timmy]], [[Lenora]], [[Elmer Fudd]], [[Droopy Dawg]], and [[Pete Puma]]. His disability makes him look and sound like the Beaky Buzzard character.\n\n==Appearance==\nHe wears a blue shirt with the Camp Tardicaca logo,gray pants and black shoes. He has a long nose and a swelled head.
