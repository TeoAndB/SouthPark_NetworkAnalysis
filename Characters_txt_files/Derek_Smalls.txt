"{{South Park character\n|name = Derek Smalls\n|image = Derek-smalls.png\n|gender = Male\n|hair = Brown\n|job = News Reporter\n|voice = [[Trey Parker]]<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0705980/|title=\"South Park\" Two Guys Naked in a Hot Tub (TV episode 1999)|publisher=IMDb.com}}</ref>\n|appearance = \"[[Merry Christmas Charlie Manson!]]\"\n}}\n'''Derek Smalls''' (formerly '''Kevin McCarthy''') is a reporter that appears in some episodes of the first seasons. He first appears in the [[Season Two]] episode \"[[Summer Sucks]]\". Derek was not seen with the other reporters in \"[[Sponsored Content]]\".\n\n==Appearance==\nDerek Smalls wears a white shirt under a black suit with a blue tie, gray pants and black shoes. He has combed brown hair, a long head and small eyebrows.\n\n==Prominence==\n*\"[[Summer Sucks]]\" - Debut.\n*\"[[Merry Christmas Charlie Manson!]]\" - He announces that [[Charles Manson]] has managed to escape from prison.\n*\"[[Chef Aid]]\"\n*\"[[Rainforest Shmainforest]]\"\n*\"[[Sexual Harassment Panda]]\" - He announces the case of sexual harassment. He is named \"Kevin McCarthy\" in this episode.\n*\"[[Two Guys Naked in a Hot Tub]]\" - His name became \"Derek Smalls\". He reports the case of \"religious cult\" in the house throughout the episode. At the end of the episode he with the help of [[Stan Marsh|Stan]], [[Butters Stotch|Butters]], [[Dougie]], and [[Pip]], proves to [[Danny Ganz]] (ATF Commander) that there was no religious cult.
