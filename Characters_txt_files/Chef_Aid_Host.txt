The boys then decide to hold a concert as an alternative. Their concert eventually attracted several other celebrities such as [[Elton John]] and [[Rancid]], turning into a large-scale concert with a massive crowd. An MC would appear several times to introduce each singer or group that is due to perform in the concert.\n\n==Appearance==\nThe Chef Aid host has a messy brown hair tied with a blue headband.
