They appear in the [[Season Six]] episode \"[[The Return of the Fellowship of the Ring to the Two Towers]]\".\n\n==Background==\nAs [[the boys]] were playing ''Lord of the Rings'', they were tasked to return [[Back Door Sluts 9|a pornographic tape]] to the [[Two Towers Video Store|video store]]. Along the way, they encounter a group of kids playing ''Harry Potter'' and casting spells on one another, with [[Eric Cartman]] taunting them for being \"fags\".\n\nAs the boys were being chased by the [[The 6th Graders|6th graders]], Cartman suggested to cross a nearby river as he claims that \"6th graders can't stand water\". [[Craig Tucker]] opposed to this and decided to join the Harry Potter kids instead.\n\n==Appearance==\nThe kids are dressed up as students and faculty staff of the Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry (Severus Snape, Ron Weasley, Harry Potter).\n{{Minor Characters from Season Six}}\n[[zh:哈利·波特小孩]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Male characters]]\n[[Category:School children]]\n[[Category:Groups]]\n[[Category:Unnamed characters]]\n[[Category:One-off characters]]"
