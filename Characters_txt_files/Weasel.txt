"{{South Park character\n|name = Weasel\n|image = The-weasel.png\n|gender = Male\n|hair = Black\n|appearance = \"[[The Tooth Fairy Tats 2000]]\"\n}}\n'''Weasel''' is a character who appears in the [[Season Four]] premiere episode \"[[The Tooth Fairy Tats 2000]]\".\n\n==Background==\nWeasel brings [[Eric Cartman|Cartman]] four hundred pounds of teeth from [[China]] to pay [[Loogie]] for the tooth trade. Cartman kicks him out of his house when he finds out that Weasel was scamming him with four hundred pounds of teeth that were actually cat teeth instead of human teeth, causing Weasel to make monkey noises before leaving.\n\n==Appearance==\nWeasel has a unique appearance from most other child characters. He has an egg-shaped head and very large eyes. He has small strands of black hair on his head, thick black eyebrows, and a large nose. When not speaking, his large buck teeth are visible. He wears a black coat with a white undershirt, a light brown belt, dark brown pants, and black shoes.
