She first appear in the [[Season Eighteen]] episode, \"[[REHASH|#REHASH]]\".\n\n==Background==\nIn her debut, she performed \"[[Missy Who, Who]]\" at the \"Women of Rock\" concert with the [[Michael Jackson Hologram]]. Before the performance, she got into a fight with [[Lorde]].\n\nShe returns in \"[[HappyHolograms|#HappyHolograms]]\", where she performs with [[Elvis Presley]]'s [[Elvis Presley Hologram|hologram]] in the ''[[Washington Redskins: Go Fuck Yourself Holiday Special]]''.\n\n==Appearance==\nIggy Azalea has blonde hair and wears a light blue sleeveless jumpsuit, yellow high-heeled shoes, and much makeup.
