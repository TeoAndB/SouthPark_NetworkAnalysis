"{{South Park character\n|name = Mr. Harris\n|image = Mr. Harris.png\n|gender = Male\n|hair = Brown\n|job = [[South Park Elementary]] Staff Member\n|voice = [[Trey Parker]]<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0769424/|title=\"South Park\" Cartman Joins NAMBLA (TV Episode 2000)|publisher=IMDb.com}}</ref>\n|appearance = \"[[Cartman Joins NAMBLA]]\"\n}}\n'''Mr. Harris''' is a faculty member at [[South Park Elementary]] who has been seen at multiple assemblies. He first appears as a member of the [[NAMBLA|North American Man-Boy Love Association]] in [[Season Four]]'s \"[[Cartman Joins NAMBLA]]\".\n\n==Background==\nMr. Harris first appears at a meeting of the North American Man-Boy Love Association when they visit [[South Park (Location)|South Park]], protesting the treatment of the pedophiles arrested in the town. He is at the banquet later in the episode, claiming he had found the \"boy of the decade\", describing him as \"sexy, sassy, and full of spunk\", and is shown sitting with an uncomfortable [[Timmy Burch]].\n\nIn \"[[Erection Day]]\", he is seen with the South Park Elementary school faculty at the [[South Park Elementary Talent Show|talent show]], though his exact role there is unknown.\n\nWhen the faculty and students were taken hostage by the Germans in \"[[Funnybot]]\", he was also present.\n\nHe can be seen directing students to safety in \"[[SUPER HARD PCness]]\" during the assembly in the [[South Park Elementary Gymnasium|gymnasium]] after the nuclear strike on [[Canada]].\n\n==Appearance==\nThis man wears a white shirt with two pockets on each side and the sleeves up, with a tie that is initially tan and brown, and with brown pants and black shoes. His left side pocket has what appears to be a black pen and a pocket protector. His tie is purple in his later appearances. He has thinning brown hair, slightly combed over his head, with thick eyebrows and a prominent chin. His face is quite wrinkled.\n\n<gallery widths=\"160\" spacing=\"small\" caption=\"Images of Mr. Harris\" >\nCartmanJoinsNAMBLA077.png|Mr. Harris commenting on his attraction to Timmy.\nCartmanJoinsNAMBLA078.png|Mr. Harris sitting with Timmy.\nErectionDay_(57).png|Seen next to [[Richard Adler]] at the talent show in \"[[Erection Day]]\".\nFunnybot036.png|Held hostage by the Germans in \"[[Funnybot]]\".\nSPSHP_(94).png|Helping students to safety in \"[[SUPER HARD PCness]]\".\n</gallery>\n\n==Prominence==\n*\"[[Cartman Joins NAMBLA]]\" - first seen as a member of NAMBLA; finds Timmy Burch \"spunky and attractive\".\n*\"[[Erection Day]]\" - Seen during the school talent show with other teachers.\n*\"[[Funnybot]]\" - Seen when the school is held hostage by the Germans.\n*\"[[SUPER HARD PCness]]\" - Seen later in the gymnasium helping direct students to safety.\n\n==Trivia==\n*He, like some other NAMBLA members, is based on [[Randy Marsh]]'s character model.\n\n==References==\n{{reflist}}\n{{South Park Elementary Staff}}\n{{Minor Characters from Season Four}}\n[[zh:哈里斯先生]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Adult characters]]\n[[Category:Male characters]]\n[[Category:School faculty]]\n[[Category:Townsfolk]]\n[[Category:Minor characters]]\n[[Category:Unnamed characters]]\n[[Category:Characters voiced by Trey Parker]]"
