He appears in the [[Season Eight]] episode, \"[[Douche and Turd]]\", where he was seen competing against [[Turd Sandwich]].\n\n==Background==\nThe idea for having a giant douche as the school mascot was invented by [[Kyle Broflovski]], who attempted to persuade many students into writing 'giant douche' on the ballot.\n\nThe \"[[Douche and Turd]]\" concept is redone in \"[[Member Berries]]\". In the election, [[Herbert Garrison]] is the Giant Douche and [[Hillary Clinton]] is the [[Turd Sandwich]]. Giant Douche eventually wins the election.\n\n==Appearance==\nGiant Douche is a blue douche with \"South Park\" written in black.
