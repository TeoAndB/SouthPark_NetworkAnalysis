He later appeared in the episode \"[[Eat, Pray, Queef]]\" and told Terrance and Philip why their show was replaced with the [[Queef Sisters]] and later fired them.\n\n==Appearance==\nHe appears to be either very stocky or very obese. He has gray hair and a dark gray mustache. He wears a white, long sleeve shirt, gray suspenders, gray pants, and a red tie.\n<gallery widths=\"160\" spacing=\"small\" caption=\"Images of Brian\">\nCartoonWarsII011.png|Brian at his desk.\n</gallery>\n\n==Personality==\nIn \"[[Eat, Pray, Queef]]\", he appears to be very business-minded, ignoring his disgust with queefing in order to make more money.
