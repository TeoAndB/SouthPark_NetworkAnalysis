She wears a black navy-style jacket, grey pants, and pink lipstick. She has long brown hair and a small mole on the left side of her face.\n\n==Personality==\nShe seems coy and shy about showing her 'wormhole' to anyone and she apparently knows about stuffing.\n\n==Powers and Abilities==\nNatalie is the portal between the worlds Earth and [[Plymouth (Planet)|Plymouth]], called the wormhole.\n\n==Relationships==\nShe was taken on a date by [[Kyle Broflovski|Kyle of DeVry Institute]] to an expensive restaurant in order to persuade her to open her 'wormhole' per orders of the History Channel.\n\n==Quotes==\n*'''Natalie Portman:''' \"Hmmmm... no!\" from \"[[A History Channel Thanksgiving]]\"\n*'''Natalie Portman:''' \"I have dual [[City People|citizenship]] in the United States and Israel\" from \"[[A History Channel Thanksgiving]]\".\n\n==Trivia==\n*Natalie Portman played an identical role in the movie ''Thor'' released in the summer of 2011.\n*Natalie Portman claims to be taking courses at \"The University of Jerusalem\".
