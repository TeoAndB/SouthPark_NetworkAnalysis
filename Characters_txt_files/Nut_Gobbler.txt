"{{South Park character\n|name = Nut Gobbler\n|image = Nut-gobbler.png\n|gender = Female\n|hair = Brown\n|job = Prostitute\n|voice = [[Mona Marshall]]<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0763047/|title=\"South Park\" Erection Day (TV episode 2005)|publisher=IMDb.com}}</ref>\n|appearance = \"[[Erection Day]]\"\n}}\n'''Nut Gobbler''' is a prostitute who appears in the [[Season Nine]] episode, \"[[Erection Day]]\". \n\n==Background==\n[[File:Whore of jimmy.png|thumb|200px|left|Nut Gobbler in \"[[Britney's New Look]]\"]]\nIn \"[[Erection Day]]\", she is hired by [[Jimmy Valmer|Jimmy]], who thought having intercourse with her would prevent his erections long enough for him to perform in the school talent show without embarrassing himself. She has a pimp named [[Q-Money]] who, upon seeing Nut Gobbler at a fancy restaurant with Jimmy, tries to kill her under the belief that she was looking for another pimp. Jimmy ends up saving Nut Gobbler with his comedy, and he has sex with her.\n\nIn \"[[Britney's New Look]]\", Nut Gobbler is seen among the crowd of people taking pictures of [[Britney Spears]], in order to kill her.\n\n==Appearance==\nNut Gobbler appears to be in her 30's. She wears a nurse's outfit, a nurse's cap, a black leather jacket, red high heels and a red thong. She wears purple eyeshadow and red lipstick. She is overweight with large front teeth. Nut Gobbler is a brunette and often has a scowl on her face, though this could be because of her teeth. \n\nShe has a thick New England accent and apparently has a medical condition that causes her to bleed out of her urethra.\n\n==Prominence==\n*\"[[Erection Day]]\" - Debut appearance.\n*\"[[Britney's New Look]]\" - Seen in the crowd taking pictures of [[Britney Spears]].\n\n==References==\n{{reflist}}\n{{Minor Characters from Season Nine}}\n[[zh:疯火鸡]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Female characters]]\n[[Category:Adult characters]]\n[[Category:Overweight characters]]\n[[Category:Minor characters]]\n[[Category:Characters voiced by Mona Marshall]]\n[[Category:Unnamed characters]]"
