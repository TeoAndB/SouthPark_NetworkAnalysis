He first appears in the [[Season Three]] episode \"[[Chinpokomon]]\".\n\n==Background==\nWhen the children get hooked on Chinpokomon, they go to [[Luau's Toys]] to buy [[Chinpokomon (Brand)|Chinpokomon dolls]]. At closing time, Red picks up a doll to hear it explain how to stop the American government. Red goes to [[Japan]] to complain about Chinpokomon, but the creators confuse him by talking about how big his penis is compared to theirs, making him forget why he came, so he leaves.\n\n==Appearance==\nAt work, Red wears a white t-shirt and a green and pink jacket with a patch saying \"Luau's Toys\".
