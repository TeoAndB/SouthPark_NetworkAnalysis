"{{South Park character\n|name = Joseph Smith\n|image = Joseph-smith.png\n|gender = Male\n|hair = Blond\n|dob = December 23, 1805\n|age = 206\n|died = June 27, 1844 (in real life)\n|job = Superhero\n|religion = Mormonism\n|voice = [[Trey Parker]]<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0705972/|title=\"South Park\" Super Best Friends (TV episode 2001)|publisher=IMDb.com}}</ref><br>[[Matt Stone]]<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0705893/fullcredits#cast|title=\"South Park\" All About Mormons (2003)|publisher=IMDb.com}}</ref>\n|wikipedia = [[wikipedia:Joseph Smith|Joseph Smith]]\n|appearance = \"[[Super Best Friends]]\"\n}}\n'''Joseph Smith''' is the founder of Mormonism, and a member of the [[Super Best Friends (Group)|Super Best Friends]]. His powers include the ability to blow cold air and ice from his mouth. He is also featured in the episode, \"[[All About Mormons]]\". He appears briefly, with some other religious characters in \"[[Imaginationland]]\", when the boys are arriving with the [[Mayor of Imaginationland|Mayor]]. He is the founder of the only correct religion in the South Park universe.\n\n==Background==\n===Powers and Abilities===\nIn all appearances (except for \"[[All About Mormons]]\"), he is shown to have the power of flight and cryokinesis, which is shown in his ability to project super-cold vapor from his mouth.\n\n==Appearance==\nJoseph has short blond hair, he also has thick, yellow eyebrows. He wears a blue coat, a gold-colored vest, a white blouse, and brown pants.\n\n==Personality==\nIn \"[[All About Mormons]]\", he appeared to be manipulative, easily tricking people into thinking he was a prophet.\n\nIn \"[[Super Best Friends]]\", he is a member of the [[Super Best Friends (Group)|Super Best Friends]], making him a valid religious figure.\n\n==Prominence==\n*\"[[Super Best Friends]]\"\n*\"[[I'm a Little Bit Country]]\"\n*\"[[All About Mormons]]\"\n*\"[[Imaginationland]]\"\n*\"[[200]]\"\n*\"[[201]]\"\n* \"[[The Pandemic Special]]\" - Appears on [[Stan Marsh|Stan]]'s lunchbox with the other [[Super Best Friends (Group)|Super Best Friends]] including uncensored [[Muhammad]].\n\n==References==\n{{Reflist}}\n{{HistoricalCelebrities}}\n{{Minor Characters from Season Seven}}\n[[es:Joseph Smith]]\n[[ja:ジョセフ・スミス]]\n[[zh:约瑟夫·史密斯]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Religious Figures]]\n[[Category:Immortals]]\n[[Category:Characters with superpowers]]\n[[Category:Male characters]]\n[[Category:Adult characters]]\n[[Category:Super Best Friends]]\n[[Category:Characters voiced by Trey Parker]]\n[[Category:Imaginationland characters]]\n[[Category:Characters voiced by Matt Stone]]\n[[Category:Mormon characters]]\n[[Category:Historical celebrities]]\n[[Category:Celebrities]]\n[[Category:Minor characters]]"
