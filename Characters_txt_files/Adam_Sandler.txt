"{{South Park character\n|name = Adam Sandler\n|image = Adamsandler1.png\n|gender = Male\n|race = Caucasian\n|hair = Brown\n|age = {{age|1966|09|09}}\n|dob = September 9, 1966\n|job = Actor<br>Comedian\n|religion = Judaism\n|voice = [[Matt Stone]]<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.imdb.com/title/tt1884035/|title=\"South Park\" HUMANCENTiPAD (TV episode 2011)|publisher=IMDb.com}}</ref>\n|wikipedia = [[Wikipedia:Adam Sandler|Adam Sandler]]\n|appearance = \"[[Funnybot]]\"\n}}'''Adam Richard Sandler''' is an American actor and comedian famous for his comedic roles in ''Wedding Singer'', ''Little Nicky'' and ''Click''. He was first mentioned in the [[Season Eight]] episode \"[[AWESOM-O]]\", and made his first appearance on ''[[South Park]]'' in the [[Season Fifteen]] episode \"[[Funnybot]]\".\n\n==Background==\nAdam Sandler is mentioned in \"[[AWESOM-O]]\" when [[A.W.E.S.O.M.-O 4000]] is giving out movie ideas to executives for future Adam movies.\n\nIn \"[[You're Getting Old]]\", an Adam Sandler movie trailer for \"Jack and Jill\" is seen at the [[Bijou Cinema]]. [[Stan Marsh]] goes to see this movie in \"[[Ass Burgers]]\". Even if he previously ridiculed the trailer of it, he enjoys it while under the influence of alcohol.\n\nAdam Sandler makes an appearance as an angry comedian that holds the [[South Park Elementary]] hostage with other comedians in \"[[Funnybot]]\" for putting them out of work by the German-built [[Funnybot (Character)|Funnybot]].\n\n==Appearance==\nSandler is seen wearing a grey hoodie and a white 'NYC' (New York City) T-shirt, which is where he was born. He also wears blue jean-shorts and dark brown loafers.
