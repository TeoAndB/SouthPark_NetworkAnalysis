He appeared in ''[[South Park: Bigger, Longer & Uncut]]'' during the song, \"[[What Would Brian Boitano Do?]]\", in which [[Brian Boitano]] defeats Khan while building the pyramids.\n\n==Appearance==\nKublai Khan had long black hair and a long black mustache. He also had a black goatee and black eyebrows. He wore brown pants with a red tint, black boots with fur lining, a brown and blue vest, a long black cape, and a metal helmet.
