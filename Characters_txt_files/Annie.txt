"{{South Park character\n|image = Annie-ButtersBottomBitch.png\n|name = Annie\n|gender = Female\n|race = Caucasian \n|hair = Orange\n|age = 9\n|job = Student\n|grade = [[The 4th Grade|4th Grade]]\n|voice = [[April Stewart]]<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.imdb.com/title/tt1526694/fullcredits#cast|title=\"South Park\" Butters' Bottom Bitch (TV Episode 2009)|publisher=IMDb.com}}</ref>\n|appearance = \"[[Butters' Bottom Bitch]]\"\n|speaking = \"[[Hummels & Heroin]]\"\n}}\n{{LinkDisambig|Annie (Disambiguation)}}\n'''Annie''' is a [[List of Female 4th Graders|female fourth grader]] at [[South Park Elementary]]. She joined [[Butters Stotch|Butters]]' \"kissing company\" in \"[[Butters' Bottom Bitch]]\", and has since become a background character.\n\n==Background==\nAnnie debuted in \"[[Butters' Bottom Bitch]]\", where she joined Butters' kissing company later than his first worker [[Sally Darson]]. She did not speak but was shown being trained by Butters and referred by name.\n\nShe made her first speaking appearance in \"[[Hummels & Heroin]]\", telling [[Marcus Preston]] about the death of Peppa Pig.\n\n==Appearance==\nAnnie has short, curly orange hair with a cyan clip with a yellow flower on it. She wears a light pink-rimmed pink shirt with a yellow flower at its center, a cyan jacket, and blue pants. She has a more detailed appearance than most fourth graders.\n<gallery widths=\"160\" spacing=\"small\" caption=\"Images of Annie\">\nAnnie-ButtersBottomBitch2.png|Annie kissing [[Butters Stotch|Butters]].\nMinor cameo.jpeg|Annie's cameo in \"[[Tweek x Craig]]\".\n</gallery>\n\n==Personality==\nShe seems to be a nice girl with enough time on her hands for a second job, and seems content being included in this company.
