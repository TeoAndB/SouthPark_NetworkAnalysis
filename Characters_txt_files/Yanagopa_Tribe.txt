"{{Group\n|name = The Yanagopa Tribe\n|image = Adults-natives-yanagapa-tribe.png\n|type = Tribe\n|gender = Male\n|first = \"[[Rainforest Shmainforest]]\"\n|times = 1\n}}\nThe '''Yanagopa Tribe''' is a tribe native to [[Costa Rica]] that appears in the [[Season Three]] episode, \"[[Rainforest Shmainforest]]\".\n\n==Background==\nThe Yanagopa tribe lives in the jungles of [[Costa Rica]]. They capture the [[Getting Gay With Kids]] choir and plan to kill them while also sacrificing [[Ms. Stevens]] to their leader. [[Eric Cartman|Cartman]] and some bulldozers intervene and kill a few, causing the others to disperse.\n\n==Appearance==\nThey have light-brown skin with very large brown lips. They also have black hair and grey loincloths. They each have a spear in their hands.\n\n==Video Games==\n===[[South Park Let's Go Tower Defense Play!]]===\nThey are one of the enemies in later stages of the game, where the Laser tower works best against them. They appear in massive waves to overwhelm the player.
