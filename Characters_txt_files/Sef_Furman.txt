"{{South Park character\n|name = Josef \"Sef\" Furman\n|image = <gallery>\n4th-graders-josef-cc.png|Current\nSefFurmanWearingMask.png|Mask\n</gallery>\n|gender = Male\n|hair = Grayish Brown\n|age = '''South Park:''' 9-10 <br>'''Real life:''' 18 (deceased)<ref name=\"JF\">{{cite web|url=https://www.jewishfuneralsusa.com/obituary/523129/Josef-Furman/|title=Sef Furman obituary|publisher=Jewish Funeral USA}}</ref>\n|birthday = February 27, 2002<ref name=\"JF\" />\n|died = November 26, 2020<ref name=\"JF\" />\n|grade = [[The 4th Grade|4th grade]]\n|religion = Judaism<ref name=\"JF\" />\n|appearance = \"[[The Pandemic Special]]\"\n}}\n'''Josef \"Sef\" Furman''' was an 18-year-old ''South Park'' fan from Plano, Texas who suffered from brain cancer. With the help of the Make-A-Wish Foundation and the creators of the show, he made a guest appearance in \"[[The Pandemic Special]]\".<ref name=\"KRLD\">{{cite web|url=https://www.radio.com/krld/entertainment/local/north-texas-teen-stars-in-south-park-episode|title=North Texas teen stars in 'South Park' episode|publisher=KRLD 1080}}</ref>\n\nSef passed away on November 26, 2020,<ref name=\"JF\" /> almost two months after \"[[The Pandemic Special]]\" aired.\n\n==Background==\nSef Furman appears in [[Mrs. Nelson]]'s 4th-grade Zoom class and is later seen in quarantine with the rest of the school.\n\n==Appearance==\nSef Furman has black glasses and wears a black short-sleeved shirt, red shorts, and black shoes.
