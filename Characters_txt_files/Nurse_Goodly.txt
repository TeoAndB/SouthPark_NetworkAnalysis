She worked in [[Hells Pass Hospital]] with [[Dr. Doctor]].\n\n==Background==\nShe first appears in \"[[Cartman's Mom is Still a Dirty Slut]]\" as the only nurse at [[Hells Pass Hospital]]. She helps keep the kids informed of what's going on while the Doctor works. This is her only major appearance, but she can be seen with (likely prosthetic) arms in ''[[South Park: Bigger, Longer & Uncut]],'' and \"[[Cartman Joins NAMBLA]]\" in Kenny's dream sequence.\n\n==Appearance==\nShe has red hair and long eyelashes.
