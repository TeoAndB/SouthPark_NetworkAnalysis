"{{South Park character\n|name = Anonymous821\n|image = Anonymous821-0.png\n|gender = Male\n|race = Caucasian\n|hair = Brown\n|job = Internet Troll\n|appearance = \"[[Wieners Out]]\"\n|voice = [[Trey Parker]]\n}}\n'''Anonymous821''', whose real name is unknown, was an internet troll who first appeared in the [[Season Twenty]] episode, \"[[Wieners Out]]\". \n\n==Background==\nIn \"[[Wieners Out]]\", he is seen briefly at a once undisclosed location when [[Dildo Shwaggins]] brings [[Gerald Broflovski]] to the secret troll meeting.\n\nIn \"[[Douche and a Danish]]\", the meeting is revealed to be taking place at [[Airport Hilton]]. After Shwaggins and Gerald coordinate the troll, Anonymous821 joins the trolls and signs in to multiple online accounts.\n\nIn \"[[Fort Collins]]\", he is only seen briefly at the impromptu troll party at the [[Broflovski Residence]]. He later leaves after Gerald insists the trolls leave.\n\nIn \"[[Oh, Jeez]]\", he is captured by [[TrollTrace.com]] after believing he had been hired by [[Hillary Clinton]] to infiltrate and sabotage the service. He then spends the rest of Season Twenty imprisoned in their headquarters.\n\n===Office===\nAnonymous821's office is decorated with masks and Anonymous posters. To his right are several masks and a book case. In front of him are two computers, and to his left is a large panel television. He sits in high-back gaming chair.\n\n==Appearance==\nAnonymous821 is overweight, and is most noted by his Guy Fawkes mask. He typically wears a Minneapolis Millers jersey over a black, long sleeve tee shirt.\n\n<gallery widths=\"160\" spacing=\"small\" caption=\"Images of Anonymous821\">\nAnonymous821-1.png|Anonymous821 at the Airport Hilton trolls conference in \"[[Wieners Out]]\".\nAnonymous821-2.png|Anonymous821 at [[Gerald Broflovski]]'s home in \"[[Fort Collins]]\".\nAnonymous821-3.png|Anonymous821 captured by [[TrollTrace.com]] in \"[[Oh, Jeez]]\".\n</gallery>\n\n==Prominence==\n*\"[[Wieners Out]]\" - Seen briefly at the secret troll meeting.\n*\"[[Douche and a Danish]]\" - Seen at the secret troll meeting and joining the big troll against [[Denmark]].\n*\"[[Fort Collins]]\" - Seen at the troll party at [[Gerald Broflovski]]'s home.\n*\"[[Oh, Jeez]]\" - Seen at [[TrollTrace.com]] headquarters in a locked, secure room with other internet trolls.\n*\"[[Members Only]]\" - Seen being held by TrollTrace.com.\n*\"[[Not Funny]]\" - Seen at [[TrollTrace.com]] being humiliated by [[Lennart Bedrager]].\n*\"[[The End of Serialization as We Know It]]\" - Seen at [[TrollTrace.com]]'s headquarters locked in the control room.\n\n==References==\n{{Reflist}}\n\n{{Internet Trolls}}\n{{Minor Characters from Season Twenty}}\n[[es:Anonymous821]]\n[[fr:Anonymous821]]\n[[zh:匿名821]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Male characters]]\n[[Category:Overweight characters]]\n[[Category:Adult characters]]\n[[Category:Internet trolls]]\n[[Category:Bullies]]\n[[Category:Unnamed characters]]\n[[Category:Minor characters]]"
