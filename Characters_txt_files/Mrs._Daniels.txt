After her father teases her for dating the only other African American student, Mrs. Daniels supports Nichole and is very happy for her. \n\n==Appearance==\nMrs. Daniels wears black shoes, a red v-neck dress, and a light-yellow shirt. She has long, black hair and wears a gold necklace and maroon lipstick. She is slightly overweight.\n\nHer hair has been largely trimmed since \"[[The Pandemic Special]]\".\n\n<gallery widths=\"160\" caption=\"Images of Mrs. Daniels\">\nCartmanFindsLove047.png|Mrs. Daniels and her family eating dinner.\nSPPDS52.png|Mrs. Daniels in a zoom conference with Mr. Mackey and oher parents\n</gallery>\n\n==Prominence==\n*\"[[Cartman Finds Love]]\" - Seen with her  family eating dinner at her home.\n*\"[[Member Berries]]\" - Seen watching the football game.\n*\"[[Splatty Tomato]]\" - Seen at the end of the episode in the crowd.\n*\"[[Turd Burglars]]\" - Seen at the women's meeting at [[Park County Community Center]].\n*\"[[The Pandemic Special]]\" - Appeared in a Zoom conference with [[Mr. Mackey]] and other parents.\n\n==References==\n{{Reflist}}\n[[zh:丹尼尔斯太太]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Female characters]]\n[[Category:Adult characters]]\n[[Category:Parents]]\n[[Category:Minority characters]]\n[[Category:African-American characters]]\n[[Category:Characters voiced by Mona Marshall]]\n[[Category:Minor characters]]\n[[Category:Townsfolk]]\n[[Category:Characters in a relationship]]\n[[Category:Overweight characters]]"
