However, news reporter [[Nobunaga Hiroichi]] reports that [[Japan]] has reached \"Heaven\" (that was actually a studio decorated to look like heaven) and claimed it as their own.\n\nNobunaga then turns to the Natsako Semu for the weather, who reports \"Heaven\" to be partly cloudy.\n\n==Appearance==\nNatsako Semu has a long, black hair, and wears a white suit with light green tie, a gray skirt and white shoes.
