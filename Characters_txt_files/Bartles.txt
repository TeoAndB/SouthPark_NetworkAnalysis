Although appearing in the show many times, he was unnamed until the [[Season Twelve]] episode, \"[[Britney's New Look]]\", where he was referred to as \"Dave\". He was later revealed to be known as \"Bartles\" in ''[[South Park: The Fractured But Whole]]''.\n\n==Appearance==\nHe has large ears and light-brown messy hair. He wears an orange and blue striped shirt and blue jeans.
