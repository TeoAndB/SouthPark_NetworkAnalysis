"{{South Park character\n|name = Mr. Wyland\n|image = Mr-wyland.png\n|gender = Male\n|race = Caucasian\n|hair = Brown\n|job = Substitute Teacher\n|voice = Bruce Howell<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0705910/|title=\"Cherokee Hair Tampons\" on IMDB}}</ref>\n|appearance = \"[[Cherokee Hair Tampons]]\"\n|last = \"[[Chef Goes Nanners]]\"\n}}\n'''Mr. Wyland''' was the 3rd-grade substitute teacher for [[Herbert Garrison|Mr. Garrison]] in two episodes after Mr. Garrison was caught attempting to molest a child, [[Eric Cartman]], in \"[[Cartman Joins NAMBLA]]\". He was first shown in \"[[Cherokee Hair Tampons]]\", where he made the class make a get well card for [[Kyle Broflovski|Kyle]], who had kidney failure. He also appears in \"[[Chef Goes Nanners]]\" to have the children debate over the [[South Park Flag]].\n\n==Appearance==\nWyland wears a blue jacket, similar to that of [[Randy Marsh]], with a red bow tie. He has a double chin, wrinkles on his forehead, and brown hair.\n\n==Prominence==\n*\"[[Cherokee Hair Tampons]]\" - His first appearance.
