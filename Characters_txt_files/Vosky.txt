"{{South Park character\n|name = Vosky\n|image = Vosky.png\n|gender = Male\n|hair = Brown\n|job = Thief\n|voice = [[Trey Parker]]\n|appearance = \"[[Super Fun Time]]\"\n}}\n'''Vosky''' was a member of the [[The Burger King Bandits|Burger King Bandits]], who appears in the [[Season Twelve]] episode, \"[[Super Fun Time]]\".\n\n==Background==\nWhen the group seized control of Pioneer Village, Franz told Vosky and [[Domino]] to search for anyone who was hiding. He happened to came upon [[Eric Cartman|Cartman]] and [[Butters Stotch|Butters]], who were sneaking back in, and ordered them first to put their hands up, and for Butters to let go of Cartman's hand, or else he would shoot them. Butters refused, and used Cartman as a blunt object to bludgeon Vosky, knocking him down. The two boys ran away as Vosky recovered and shot at them.\n\n==Appearance==\nVosky is tall and slender and wears black pants and a heavy, black jacket. He has light brown hair. He can be seen holding an assault rifle with an underbarrel grenade launcher. He also appears to be wearing an earpiece to communicate with other members of Franz's gang.\n\n==Trivia==\n*In one scene, Franz mistakenly calls him [[Orlich]].\n{{Minor Characters from Season Twelve}}\n[[zh:沃斯基]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Male characters]]\n[[Category:One-off characters]]\n[[Category:Adult characters]]\n[[Category:Characters voiced by Trey Parker]]\n[[Category:Criminals]]"
