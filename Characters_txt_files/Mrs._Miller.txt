His mother is seen entering their base, looking upset and calling out Billy.\n\nCartman's addiction to KFC gets the better of him, keeping him from doing proper work for [[Colonel Sanders]]. In response, the Colonel mobilizes his goon squad to deal with Cartman's faction, who in turn face interception by the police, resulting in a massive gun fight.
