"{{South Park character\n|name = Tricia Tucker\n|image = Craigs-sister-0.png\n|gender = Female\n|race = Caucasian\n|hair = Strawberry Blonde\n|age = 5-6\n|job = Student\n|relation1 = Grandmother\n|relativename1 = [[Grandma Tucker]]\n|relation2 = Father\n|relativename2 = [[Thomas Tucker]]\n|relation3 = Mother\n|relativename3 = [[Laura Tucker]]\n|relation4 = Brother\n|relativename4 = [[Craig Tucker]]\n|appearance = \"[[Tweek vs. Craig]]\"\n|speaking = \"[[Major Boobage]]\"\n}}\n'''Tricia Tucker''' is [[Craig Tucker]]'s younger sister, who first appeared in the [[Season Three]] episode, \"[[Tweek vs. Craig]]\" seated at the dinner table with her family. She later joined in as the rest of her family flipped each other off.\n\n==Background==\nShe also appeared in the [[Season Twelve]] episode \"[[Major Boobage]]\", where she was shown resisting the confiscation of a grey kitten, crying, \"No! No!\" after the ban on cats was enforced by the towns bureaucracy, in attempt to stop the cat urine drug abuse known as \"cheesing\".\n\nIn \"[[Pandemic 2: The Startling]]\", she is seen in a photograph of Craig's tenth birthday. \n\nIn \"[[Butterballs]]\", she can be seen sitting in front of [[Kyle Broflovski|Kyle]] and [[Eric Cartman|Cartman]] at the anti-bullying assembly.\n\n==Appearance==\nShe wears a light blue shirt with a white flowering collar along with red pants. She appears to be about the age of five or six.\n\nShe closely resembles [[Millie Larsen]], one of Craig's classmates. She has the same color patterns as Millie, though her hair is in long, higher-up pigtails with red hair bands, as opposed to Millie's short, drooping ones.\n\n==Prominence==\n*\"[[Tweek vs. Craig]]\" - Seen eating dinner at the table with rest of family.\n*\"[[Major Boobage]]\" - Seen playing with her pet cat, prior to cats being outlawed.\n*\"[[Pandemic 2: The Startling]]\" - Seen in a photograph at her brother's tenth birthday party. \n*\"[[Butterballs]]\" - Seen sitting in front of Kyle and Cartman at the anti-bullying assembly. \n*\"[[The Magic Bush]]\" - Tricia is seen with the rest of her family at her mother's ceremony.\n\n{{South Park Families}}\n[[es:Tricia Tucker]]\n[[fr:Tricia Tucker]]\n[[it:Tricia Tucker]]\n[[ru:Триша Такер]]\n[[zh:翠西亚·塔克]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Female characters]]\n[[Category:Minor characters]]\n[[Category:Gingers]]\n[[Category:Siblings]]\n[[Category:Characters with unknown voice actor]]\n[[Category:School children]]"
