"{{South Park character\n|name = Jason Voorhees\n|image = Monster-jason-voorhees.png\n|gender = Male\n|race = Caucasian\n|hair = Brown\n|job = Murderer/Dream Expert\n|voice = [[Trey Parker]]<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0995578/|title=\"South Park\" Imaginationland: Episode II (TV episode 2007)|publisher=IMDb.com}}</ref>\n|appearance = \"[[Hell on Earth 2006]]\"\n}}\n{{LinkDisambig|Jason (Disambiguation)}}\n'''Jason Voorhees''' is a fictional mass murderer from the ''Friday the 13th'' film series.\n\n==Background==\nIn \"[[Hell on Earth 2006]]\", a man dressed as Jason is seen arriving at Satan's Halloween Party. Later, in \"[[Imaginationland, Episode II]]\", Jason is one of the members of the evil side of [[Imaginationland (Location)|Imaginationland]] who attacks the good side. He is shown with a feminine-like voice, declaring killing [[Strawberry Shortcake]] as \"super hardcore\". He is killed during the third episode but resurrected and put back behind the wall.\n\n==Appearance==\nJason Voorhees wears black pants, black/grey torn up shirts, black gloves, a green coat, and a white hockey mask. His clothes are severely torn and are covered in blood. He also wears filthy brown shoes, which are in poor condition.\n\n==Trivia==\n*In \"[[Imaginationland, Episode II]]\", Voorhees does not resemble ''South Park'''s traditional character style. Instead, his model features a more stylistic look about him, akin to rival [[Freddy Krueger]], who also appears.\n*In \"[[Broadway Bro Down]]\", a Jason hockey mask is seen being displayed in Creepy City Costumes & Novelties.\n*In \"[[Bring the Crunch]]\", [[Mimsy]] wears Jason's outfit.\n*The mask is based on his Part 7 hockey mask.\n*In the Halloween themed episode \"[[Sons A Witches]]\", [[Herbert Garrison|Mr. Garrison]] is seen in the intro wearing a hockey mask which strongly resembles Jason's mask.\n\n==Prominence==\n*\"[[Hell on Earth 2006]]\" (mentioned only)\n*\"[[Imaginationland, Episode II]]\" - Member of the bad side of Imaginationland attacking the good side.\n*\"[[The Scoots]]\"\n\n==References==\n{{Reflist}}\n[[ja:ジェイソン・ボーヒーズ]]\n[[zh:杰森·沃赫斯]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Imaginationland characters]]\n[[Category:Male characters]]\n[[Category:Adult characters]]\n[[Category:Serial killers]]\n[[Category:Immortals]]\n[[Category:Characters voiced by Trey Parker]]\n[[Category:Monsters]]\n[[Category:Minor characters]]\n[[Category:Crossover characters]]"
