He provides incredibly passionate and over-enthusiastic commentary for all \"Cock Magic\" games, both at [[City Wok]] and at [[Panda Express]]. He is voiced by Peter Serafinowicz.\n\n==Appearance==\nThe Cock Magic Ring Announcer wears a blue business suit, which consists of black shoes, blue pants, a blue coat, a purple shirt, and a purple tie.
