"{{Group\n|name = Chinese Court Judges\n|image = Outside-southpark-chinese-judges.png\n|job = High-ranking officials, court judges\n|aliases = 审判长 (presiding judges)\n|first = \"[[Band in China]]\"\n}}\nThe '''Chinese Court Judges''' are a trio of high-ranking Chinese officials who came to judge [[Randy Marsh]]'s crime of illegal possession of marijuana into [[China]]. They appear in the [[Season Twenty-Three]] episode, \"[[Band in China]]\". They reappeared in [[Season Twenty-Four]]'s episode, \"[[The Pandemic Special]]\".\n\n==Background==\nWhen Randy is arrested by the Chinese police for illegal possession of marijuana, he is brought into the Chinese Court where he meets the judges. They have just sentenced a prisoner to another 10 years in prison for disgracing the country. Randy successfully persuades the judges to put him on parole.\n\nIn \"[[The Pandemic Special]]\", they reappeared as a flashback of what Randy and [[Mickey Mouse]] did in China in \"[[Band in China]]\", with some previously unrevealed details on what they did there.\n{{Minor Characters from Season Twenty-Three}}\n[[fr:Chinese Court Judges]]\n[[zh:中国法官]]\n[[Category:Groups]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Minor characters]]\n[[Category:Adult characters]]\n[[Category:Unnamed characters]]\n[[Category:Chinese characters]]\n[[Category:Elderly people]]\n[[Category:Minority characters]]\n[[Category:Asian characters]]\n[[Category:Characters with unknown voice actor]]"
