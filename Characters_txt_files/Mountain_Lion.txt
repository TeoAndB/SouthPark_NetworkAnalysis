"{{South Park character\n|image = Mountain-lion.png\n|name = Mountain Lion\n|gender = Female\n|race = Mountain Lion\n|hair = Light Brown\n|relation1 = Children\n|relativename1 = [[Mountain Lion Cubs]]\n|appearance = \"[[Woodland Critter Christmas]]\"\n}}\nThe '''Mountain Lion''' is a legendary animal seen in [[Season Eight]] episode \"[[Woodland Critter Christmas]]\".\n\n==Background==\nIn \"[[Woodland Critter Christmas]]\", [[Stan Marsh|Stan]] is forced into looking for and killing the mountain lion that the [[Woodland Critters]] say keeps eating the virgin critter every Christmas. Stan tricks the lion into falling off a cliff. After Stan kills the lion, [[Mountain Lion Cubs|Three Cubs]] walk up to her body begging for her not to leave them and begin to cry. Stan realizes what he had done and feels extremely guilty.\n\nLater it is discovered that the critters' \"savior\" is the [[Anti-Christ]], the Son of [[Satan]], and is using [[Kyle Broflovski|Kyle]] as a vessel. However, with the help of the lion cubs, they carry out an abortion on Kyle, removing the Anti-Christ which is destroyed by [[Santa Claus]]. Santa then goes to the mountain lions' lair and brings their mother back to life. The cubs are happy at the end with their mother being alive once more and Stan goes home for Christmas.\n\n{{Minor Characters from Season Eight}}\n[[zh:山顶圣狮]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:One-off characters]]\n[[Category:Animals]]\n[[Category:Made-up characters]]\n[[Category:Female characters]]\n[[Category:Revived characters]]"
