"{{South Park character\n|name = Mr. Yelman\n|image = mr-yelman.png\n|gender = Male\n|died = \"[[Insheeption]]\"\n|job = Sheep Herder\n|appearance = \"[[Insheeption]]\"\n|final = \"[[Insheeption]]\"\n}}\n'''Mr. Yelman''' appeared in the [[Season Fourteen]] episode, \"[[Insheeption]]\". Throughout the episode, he was believed to be a hoarder but in reality, was a sheep herder. After the group finds out [[Mr. Mackey]]'s hoarding was caused by [[Woodsy Owl]], Woodsy becomes angered and kills Mr. Yelman. After everyone awoke, Mr. Yelman was seen decapitated in his chair.\n\n==Appearance==\nMr. Yelman is a sheep herder. He wears a bandanna on his head with a black strap. He wears a beige robe with a teal border. He also wears a long brown sweater.
