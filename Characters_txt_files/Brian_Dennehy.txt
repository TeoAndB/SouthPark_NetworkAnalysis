His lines parody those of [[Brian Boitano]] in the original ''[[The Spirit of Christmas#|Jesus vs. Santa]]'' cartoon. He later reappeared in the background of the [[Season Fourteen]] episode, \"[[200]]\", as one of celebrities suing [[South Park (Location)|South Park]].\n\n==Appearance==\nDennehy had short blond hair. He also appeared to be very stocky. He wore a brown suit, light blue tie, white shirt, and a white \"dress\" shirt. In \"[[200]]\", he had been redesigned in many ways. His blond hair has turned grey, he is wearing a black tie, he is shorter, and has a redesigned face.\n\n==Personality==\nBrian seemed to be very calm, not showing the slightest bit of anger when [[The Boys|the boys]] indirectly insult him in ''[[South Park: Bigger, Longer & Uncut]]''. Despite not affected from the insult, he was still with many of the other celebrities brought together by [[Tom Cruise]] to sue South Park and get [[Muhammad]].\n\n==Trivia==\n*The way the face of Brian Dennehy's current look is drawn bears a strong resemblance to [[Herbert Garrison|Garrison]]'s father, [[Mr. Garrison Senior]].\n\n==Prominence==\n*''[[South Park: Bigger, Longer & Uncut]]''\n*\"[[200]]\" - Seen as one of the angry celebrities suing South Park.\n*\"[[201]]\" - Seen as one of the angry celebrities suing South Park.\n\n==References==\n{{Reflist}}\n{{Minor Characters Movie}}\n{{MovieCelebrities}}\n\n[[es:Brian Dennehy]]\n[[fr:Brian Dennehy]]\n[[zh:布莱恩·丹尼希]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Celebrities]]\n[[Category:200 and 201 celebrities]]\n[[Category:Male characters]]\n[[Category:Minor characters]]\n[[Category:South Park: Bigger, Longer & Uncut]]\n[[Category:Adult characters]]\n[[Category:Movie celebrities]]\n[[Category:Elderly people]]\n[[Category:Characters voiced by Trey Parker]]\n[[Category:Deceased characters]]"
