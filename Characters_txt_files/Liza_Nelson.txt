"{{South Park character\n|name = Liza Nelson\n|image = Liza.png\n|gender = Female\n|race = Asian-American\n|hair = Black\n|age = 9-10\n|job = Student\n|grade = [[The 4th Grade|4th Grade]]\n|relation1 = Mother\n|relativename1 = [[Margaret Nelson]]\n|relation2 = Brother\n|relativename2 = Unnamed Brother\n|appearance = \"[[The City Part of Town]]\"\n}}\n{{LinkDisambig|Nelson (Disambiguation)}}\n'''Liza Nelson''' is a [[The 4th Grade|4th grade]] student at [[South Park Elementary]], who appears in the [[Season Nineteen]] episode, \"[[The City Part of Town]]\".\n\n==Background==\nLiza is first seen during the Whole Foods montage in the episode \"[[The City Part of Town]]\".\n\nIn \"[[You're Not Yelping]]\", Liza is new at South Park Elementary, and is shown around by [[Wendy Testaburger]]. Wendy helps her get acquainted with the school, its facilities, and its students. In the [[South Park Elementary Cafeteria|cafeteria]], Wendy introduces her to the other girls.\n\n==Appearance==\nLiza wears a black skirt, a green long-sleeved shirt, and black shoes. She has black hair that is tied in a ponytail.
