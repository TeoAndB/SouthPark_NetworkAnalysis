"{{South Park character\n|name = Maury Povich\n|image = Maury-povich.png\n|gender = Male\n|race = Caucasian\n|hair = Gray\n|dob = January 17, 1939\n|age = {{age|1939|1|17}}\n|job = Talk Show Host\n|religion = Judaism\n|voice = [[Trey Parker]]<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0705926/|title=\"South Park\" The Tale of Scrotie McBoogerballs (TV episode 2010)|publisher=IMDb.com}}</ref>\n|wikipedia = [[Wikipedia:Maury Povich|Maury Povich]]\n|appearance = \"[[Freak Strike]]\"\n}}\n'''Maury Povich''' is a talk show host who appears in the [[Season Six]] episode, \"[[Freak Strike]]\", as the host of ''The Maury Povich Show'', which had [[Eric Cartman]] and [[Liane Cartman|his mother]] as guests.\n\nBefore his initial appearance, Maury was mentioned in several prior episodes. In the episode, \"[[Spookyfish]]\", Cartman asks [[Stan Marsh]] and [[Kyle Broflovski]], \"Who did you expect, Maury Povich?\" In \"[[City on the Edge of Forever (Flashbacks)]]\", a talent agent says, \"I haven't seen a funnier person since Maury Povich\", referring to [[Veronica Crabtree]].\n\n==Appearance==\nMaury Povich wears a black collared shirt and black pants. He has wavy gray hair and a thick gray mustache. His face shows visible signs of aging as exaggerated by his eyes and cheeks.\n\n==Personality==\nMaury exploits his talk show guests purely for entertainment purposes, implying he is quite cynical and immoral, and that he is willing to do show anything outrageous on his show simply to get high ratings. \n\n==Prominence==\n*\"[[Spookyfish]]\" (mentioned) - Cartman references him.\n*\"[[City on the Edge of Forever (Flashbacks)]]\" (mentioned) - He is mentioned by a talent agent.\n*\"[[Freak Strike]]\" - Hosts ''The Maury Povich Show'' with [[Eric Cartman|Eric]] and [[Liane Cartman|Liane]] as guests.\n\n==References==\n{{Reflist}}\n{{TelevisionCelebrities}}\n{{Minor Characters from Season Six}}\n[[zh:莫里·波维奇]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Celebrities]]\n[[Category:Male characters]]\n[[Category:Adult characters]]\n[[Category:One-off characters]]\n[[Category:Characters voiced by Trey Parker]]\n[[Category:Elderly people]]\n[[Category:Jewish characters]]\n[[Category:Television celebrities]]"
