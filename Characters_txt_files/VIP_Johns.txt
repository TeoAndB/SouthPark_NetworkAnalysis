Just then, they were approached by two drunk men who mistake them as strippers, taking them into the VIP room. The men became dissatisfied with the lap dance performed by the New Kid and fought the superheroes.\n\n==Appearance==\nThe VIP Johns appeared in loosened business suits, with their faces drunk red.
