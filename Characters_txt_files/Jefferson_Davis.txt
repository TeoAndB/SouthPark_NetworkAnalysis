"{{South Park character\n|name = Jefferson Davis\n|image = Jefferson Davis Stan.png\n|gender = Male\n|age = 81 (deceased)\n|dob = June 3, 1808\n|died = December 6, 1889\n|job = Former President of the Confederate States of America<br>Former Secretary of War\n|religion   = Episcopal<ref>{{cite web|url=http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jefferson_Davis|title=Jefferson Davis on Wikipedia}}</ref>\n|appearance = \"[[The Red Badge of Gayness]]\" (Played by [[Stan Marsh]])\n}}\n'''Jefferson Davis''' was the first President of the Confederate States of America from 1861 to 1865. He was also a Representative, Secretary of War, and Senator for the United States prior to this.\n\n[[Stan Marsh|Stan]] appears dressed as Davis in \"[[The Red Badge of Gayness]]\", in order to end the Civil War reenactment.\n\n==Appearance==\nJefferson Davis was never drawn like his celebrity likeness and instead appeared in the form of Stan Marsh dressing up as him. Stan simply wore a black tuxedo with a white shirt, and a black vest.
