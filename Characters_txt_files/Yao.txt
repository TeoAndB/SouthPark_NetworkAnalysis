"{{South Park character\n|name = Yao\n|image = Yao.png\n|gender = Male\n|race = Asian\n|hair = Black\n|age = 11-13\n|job = Student; Gamer\n|voice = Ming Yao<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0705986/|title=\"South Park\" You Got F'd in the A (TV episode 2004)|publisher=IMDb.com}}</ref>\n|appearance = \"[[You Got F'd in the A]]\"\n}}\n'''Yao''' is an Asian boy who took part in [[Stan Marsh|Stan]]'s dance group in the [[Season Eight]] episode, \"[[You Got F'd in the A]]\". Stan meets him at the [[South Park Arcade]] after hearing he's really good at ''Dance Dance Revolution'' from [[Michael]].\n\n==Appearance==\nYao has long, black, hair. He wears a red shirt, with an alien from ''Space Invaders'' on the front and the words \"Game Over\" on the back. He is also seen wearing a watch on his left wrist, as well as a necklace. He also wears a blue cap as well as red-framed glasses. He appears to have abnormally large ears as well as problems with facial acne. He also has stereotypical characteristics of Asians seen in the series such as small eyes.\n\n<gallery spacing=\"small\" caption=\"Images of Yao\" widths=\"160\">\nYGFTA31.png\nYGFTA54.png\n</gallery>\n\n==Personality==\nHe appears to have an obsession with Dance Dance Revolution as he plays it everyday after school and has, so far, spent about $6000 on it. As for the rest of his personality, he appears to be kind and supportive, agreeing to give his time up to help Stan with very little coercion. He also appears to care about his reputation, becoming upset when he realized that he might \"get served\" at the Dance Off in \"[[You Got F'd in the A]]\" .\n\n==Relationships==\n===[[Stan Marsh]]===\nHe joined Stan's dance troop in \"[[You Got F'd in the A]]\" and they presumably befriended each other during the short time they were seen together.\n\n==References==\n{{Reflist}}\n{{Minor Characters from Season Eight}}\n{{South Park Children}}\n[[es:Yao]]\n[[zh:å§š]]\n[[Category:School children]]\n[[Category:Male characters]]\n[[Category:Minority characters]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:One-off characters]]\n[[Category:Asian characters]]\n[[Category:The South Park Diggities]]"
