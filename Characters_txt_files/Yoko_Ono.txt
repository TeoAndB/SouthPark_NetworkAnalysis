She is the widow of former [[The Beatles|Beatle]] and peace activist, John Lennon.\n\nShe is also briefly seen in \"[[Fat Butt and Pancake Head]]\".\n\n==Appearance==\nYoko Ono wears a black jumpsuit. She is also seen wearing semi-transparent sunglasses with light red lenses. She has short, spiky, black hair, and a uniquely shaped nose. She also has stereotypical characteristics of Asians seen in the series such as small eyes.\n\n<gallery widths=\"160\" spacing=\"small\" captionalign=\"left\" caption=\"Images of Yoko Ono\">\nYoko-Ono-angry.png|Yoko angrily addressing the children at the Recorder Concert.\nYoko-Ono-ow.png|Yoko clutching her ears after hearing the terrible music coming from the children at the Recorder Concert.\nFBPF_-_Yoko.png|An image of Yoko featured on the cover of ''The National Inquisition'' in \"[[Fat Butt and Pancake Head]]\"\n</gallery>\n\n==Personality==\nIn \"[[World Wide Recorder Concert]]\", she easily became angry over the fact that four million 3rd graders did not know the song they were supposed to be playing.\n\n==Prominence==\n*\"[[World Wide Recorder Concert]]\"\n*\"[[Fat Butt and Pancake Head]]\" - Seen on an image on the cover of The National Inquisition.\n\n==Trivia==\n*In reality, Yoko Ono is much older than she was shown, she is currently {{age|1933|02|18}}, which would have made her 66 when this episode aired.\n*On ''The National Inquisitor'', the text next to her picture reads: \"BREAKUP!: 'STILL NOT MY FAULT!'\".
