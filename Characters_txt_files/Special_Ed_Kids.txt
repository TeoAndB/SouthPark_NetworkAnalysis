"{{Group\n|name = Special Ed Kids\n|image = Kid-groups-special-ed-kids-in-lab-coats.png\n|job = Students\n|first = \"[[World Wide Recorder Concert]]\"\n|times = 9\n}}\nThe '''Special Ed Kids''' are a group of children that appear on several episodes of the show and in the ''[[South Park: The Fractured But Whole]]'' DLC, [[Bring the Crunch]].\n\n==Background==\nTheir first appearance is in \"[[Up the Down Steroid]]\" where several of them are seen training for the special Olympics. They later appear in \"[[Crippled Summer]]\" where they are seen on [[Lake Tardicaca]], many of them are seen on the [[Red Team]] in this episode. For the time being their last appearance is in \"[[Moss Piglets]]\" where they are seen as participants in the science fair.\n\nAlthough they appear in a good number of episodes, they don't have as big a role as [[Jimmy Valmer|Jimmy]] and [[Timmy Burch|Timmy]] and are mostly used as background characters.\n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery widths=\"160\" spacing=\"small\" caption=\"Images of the Special Ed Kids\">\nSpecialEdKidsHandicar.png|The Special Ed kids in \"[[Handicar]]\".\n</gallery>\n\n==Prominence==\n*\"[[Up the Down Steroid]]\"\n*\"[[The Death of Eric Cartman]]\"\n*\"[[Crippled Summer]]\"\n*\"[[Funnybot]]\"\n*\"[[Black Friday]]\"\n*\"[[A Song of Ass and Fire]]\"\n*\"[[Titties and Dragons]]\"\n*\"[[Handicar]]\"\n*\"[[Moss Piglets]]\"\n\n==Members==\n*[[Jimmy Valmer]]\n*[[Timmy Burch]]\n*[[Nathan]]\n*[[Mimsy]]\n*[[Francis (Special Ed)|Francis]]\n*[[Michael (Special Ed)|Michael]]\n*[[Libby Perkins]]\n*[[Reggie Burke]]\n*[[Chad Handler]]\n*[[Dennis Murray]]\n*[[Colette Francis]]\n[[zh:残疾小孩]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Groups]]\n[[Category:Tardicaca campers]]\n[[Category:Male characters]]\n[[Category:Female characters]]\n[[Category:Minor characters]]\n[[Category:School children]]\n[[Category:Characters with deviations]]"
