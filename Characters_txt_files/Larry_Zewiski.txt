He is the winner of all bike parades as shown in \"[[Bike Parade]]\".\n\n==Appearance==\nLarry has long blonde hair that is trimmed back at the fringe and sides that curves at the back to come out under the ears as sideburns. He wears a white tee and blue jacket with a tri-prong ornament necklace and standard jeans. He is usually grinning with a cocky smile.\n\n<gallery widths=\"160\" spacing=\"small\" caption=\"Images of Larry Zewiski\">\nLarryZewiski01.png|Larry with his bike.\nLarryZewiski02.png|Larry accompanied by [[Porsche]] and [[Mercedes]]\nLarryZewiski03.png|Larry preparing for the bike parade.\n</gallery>\n\n==Background==\nIn \"[[Unfulfilled]]\", [[Butters Stotch|Butters]] tells [[the boys]] that he will win the big prize at the bike parade and will finally beat Larry Zewiski. Larry arrives, mocking Butters, and then is accompanied by two Raisins girls, [[Porsche]] and [[Mercedes]], which admire him and his bike. Larry continues to boast about how his bike is better, upsetting Butters, and leaves with the girls to show them his blue ribbons.\n\nIn \"[[Bike Parade]]\", Larry gets intimidated by Butters' bike and tells his [[Mr. Zewiski|father]], who was part of the [[Amazon]] workers' strike, to start working again, so he can have a better bike at the bike parade.\n\n==Prominence==\n*\"[[Unfulfilled]]\"\n*\"[[Bike Parade]]\"\n\n==References==\n{{Reflist}}\n{{South Park Children}}\n[[zh:拉里·泽维斯基]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Male characters]]\n[[Category:Other 4th graders]]\n[[Category:Minor characters]]\n[[Category:Townsfolk]]\n[[Category:4th graders]]"
