"{{South Park character\n|name = Hoffman and Turk Attorney\n|image = Adults-unamed-townsfolk-hoffman-n-turk-attorney.png\n|gender = Male\n|race = Caucasian\n|hair = Brown\n|job = Attorney\n|appearance = \"[[Reverse Cowgirl]]\"\n}}\nThe '''Attorney''' from [[Hoffman and Turk Law Offices]] appears in the [[Season Sixteen]] episode, \"[[Reverse Cowgirl]]\".\n\n==Background==\nIn \"[[Reverse Cowgirl]]\", he helps [[the Boys]] perform a \"sue-ance\", in order to try to sue the inventor of the toilet, [[Sir John Harington]]. He begins these \"sue-ances\" by first getting into contact with a \"greaser\" spirit. In order to get the greaser spirit to divulge information, the attorney asks the boys to tip him $100 for each bit of information. At the end of the episode, it is revealed by the ghost of [[Betsy Donovan|Clyde's mother]] that the attorney is fraudulent, and had been extorting money from [[Clyde Donovan|Clyde]] and the boys.\n\nIn “[[World War Zimmerman]]”, he appears as Zimmerman's lawyers at the quick trail scene.\n\n==Appearance==\nThe attorney wears a black suit consisting of black pants, a black coat, and a white shirt with a red tie.
