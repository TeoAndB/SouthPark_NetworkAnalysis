"{{South Park character\n|name = Mr. Biggle\n|image = MrBiggle.png\n|gender = Male\n|race = Caucasian\n|hair = Brown\n|voice = [[Trey Parker]]<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.imdb.com/title/tt3169686/|title=\"South Park\" Goth Kids 3: Dawn of the Posers (TV Episode 2013)|publisher=IMDb.com}}</ref>\n|relation1 = Wife\n|relativename1 = [[Harriet Biggle]]\n|relation2 = Adopted Son\n|relativename2 = [[Bradley Biggle]]\n|relation3 = Daughter\n|relativename3 = [[Henrietta Biggle]]\n|appearance = \"[[Goth Kids 3: Dawn of the Posers]]\"\n}}\n'''Mr. Biggle''' is the biological father of [[Henrietta Biggle]], the adoptive father of [[Bradley Biggle]] and husband of [[Harriet Biggle]]. He only appears in \"[[Goth Kids 3: Dawn of the Posers]]\", where he and Mrs. Biggle sent Henrietta to a camp for [[Troubled Acres|troubled kids]].\n\n==Appearance==\nMr. Biggle is almost bald, he has brown hair on the sides of his head, a brown mustache, small light eyebrows, and a double chin. He wears an aqua-blue shirt under a gray jacket with white details, a black belt with a white buckle, blue pants, and black shoes. He is slightly overweight.\n\n==Family==\n===[[Bradley Biggle]]===\nBradley is his adopted son. Nothing is known about their relationship, although Bradley does not appear in any of the family portraits the Biggle family has in their living room.\n\n===[[Henrietta Biggle]]===\nHenrietta is his biological daughter.
