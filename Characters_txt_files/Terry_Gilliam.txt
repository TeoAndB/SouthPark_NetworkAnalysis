"{{South Park character\n|name = Terry Gilliam\n|image = Gilliam.JPG\n|gender = Male\n|race = Caucasian\n|age = {{age|1940|11|22}}\n|dob = November 22, 1940\n|job = Actor\n|wikipedia = [[Wikipedia:Terry Gilliam|Terry Gilliam]]\n|appearance = \"[[Dead Friend Sketch]]\"\n}}\n'''Terry Gilliam''' is a filmmaker and member of the acclaimed British comedy group [[Monty Python]].\n\n==Background==\nGilliam appears in [[Trey Parker]] and [[Matt Stone]]'s short film, the \"[[Dead Friend Sketch]]\", which is a homage to Monty Python's famous \"Dead Parrot Sketch\". In the sketch, Gilliam unexpectedly appears and manipulates cutouts of [[Eric Cartman|Cartman]] and two sculptures before being crushed by a giant foot. It is later revealed that Parker and Stone are holding his mother for ransom until Gilliam makes a cartoon for ''[[South Park]]''.\n\nParker and Stone have cited Gilliam as an influence on ''[[South Park]]'s'' animation style.\n\n==Appearance==\nIn the sketch, Gilliam appears with a real-life photograph cutout for a head, as well as realistic hands, of which he has three.\n{{MovieCelebrities}}\n[[zh:特瑞·吉列姆]]\n[[Category:Celebrities]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Male characters]]\n[[Category:One-off characters]]\n[[Category:Adult characters]]\n[[Category:Realistic characters]]\n[[Category:Movie celebrities]]"
