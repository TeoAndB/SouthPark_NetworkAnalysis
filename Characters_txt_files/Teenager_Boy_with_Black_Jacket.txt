"{{South Park character\n|name = Teenager Boy with Black Jacket\n|image = Teen-boy-Medicinal-Fried-Chicken.png\n|gender = Male\n|hair = Brown\n|appearance = \"[[Eek, A Penis!]]\"\n|speaking = \"[[Medicinal Fried Chicken]]\"\n}}\nThe '''[[Teenagers|Teenager]] Boy with Black Jacket''' is a [[South Park (Location)|South Park]] [[City People|citizen]] who has appeared in a few episodes. For now his only major role was in the [[Season Fourteen]] episode, \"[[Medicinal Fried Chicken]]\".\n\n==Background==\nHe made his first appearance on the episode \"[[Eek, A Penis!]]\", Where he is seen as one of the minority students who participate in Cartman's class.\n\nAfter making some background appearances he finally had his first speaking role in \"[[Medicinal Fried Chicken]]\". In this episode he is seen leaving [[KFC]] after buying a bag of weed. He tells [[Randy Marsh|Randy]] that the KFC has moved to a Medical Marijuana Dispensary. He is later seen at the end of the episode watching [[Dr. Doctor]]'s speech.\n\n==Appearance==\nIn his current appearance, he has thick, messy brown hair, brown eyebrows and wears a black striped hooded jacket with a lime green shirt underneath, gray pants and brown and white shoes.\n\nIn his previous appearances his appearance was almost identical, only with a bright blue shirt and blue shoes.\n\nIn his appearances on \"[[Eek, A Penis!]]\" and at the end of \"[[Medicinal Fried Chicken]]\", he had dark skin.\n\n==Trivia==\n*He is one of the few characters in the show that has more than one look, along with the [[Truck Driver]] and the [[Teenager Girl with Striped Shirt]].\n*There is an unnamed citizen in a blue shirt who looks a lot like him, only with black hair and eyebrows and appears to be an adult.\n\n==Prominence==\n*\"[[Eek, A Penis!]]\" - Seen as one of Cartman's students.\n*\"[[Dead Celebrities]]\"\n*\"[[The F Word]]\" - Seen in the library.\n*\"[[Pandemic]]\" - Seen leaving a store with his girlfriend.\n*\"[[Butters' Bottom Bitch]]\" - Seen at the [[Alpha Tau Omega]] fraternity house.\n*\"[[Medicinal Fried Chicken]]\" - Tell Randy that KFC has become a Medical Marijuana Dispensary.\n[[zh:黑夹克少年]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Unnamed characters]]\n[[Category:Teens]]\n[[Category:Townsfolk]]\n[[Category:Male characters]]"
