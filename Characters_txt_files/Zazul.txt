"{{South Park character\n|name = Zazul\n|image = <gallery>\nZazul-normal.png|Current\nZazul-girl.png|Schoolgirl\n</gallery>\n|gender = Male\n|voice = [[Matt Stone]]<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0887071/|title=\"South Park\" Hell on Earth 2006 (TV episode 2006)|publisher=IMDb.com}}</ref>\n|appearance = \"[[Best Friends Forever]]\"\n}}\n'''Zazul''' is a demon from [[Hell]] and one of [[Satan]]'s minions who makes his first appearance as a background character in the [[Season Nine]] episode \"[[Best Friends Forever]]\".\n\n==Background==\nIn \"[[Hell on Earth 2006]]\", Zazul appears among more of Satan's minions when he tells them about a party he is having on Earth. He later introduces the Three Killers, [[Ted Bundy]], [[John Wayne Gacy]], and [[Jeffrey Dahmer]] and tells them to pick up Satan's [[Ferrari Cake]] for the party. He also appears wearing a schoolgirl costume but is told to take it off by Satan because Satan himself wants to wear the schoolgirl costume. He appears when an Acura Cake is unveiled due to a problem with the Ferrari Cake and is smacked away by an angry Satan.\n\n==Appearance==\nZazul has dark red skin, large wings, two small horns on his head, small yellow eyes, and sharp teeth. His normal outfit consists of black and gray body armor. As a schoolgirl, he wears a blonde wig with pink bows in it, a gray and white top that is tied up high, a plaid skirt, white leggings, and black high-heeled shoes.\n\n==Prominence==\n*\"[[Best Friends Forever]]\" - Seen having his body armor put on him for the battle.\n*\"[[Make Love, Not Warcraft]]\" - A model of him can be seen on top of [[Jenkins]]' television.\n*\"[[Hell on Earth 2006]]\" - He appears as a schoolgirl but is told to take the costume off so Satan can wear it.\n\n===''South Park: The Fractured But Whole''===\n[[File:SPTFBW_Zazul.png|thumb|left|Zazul's appearance in ''South Park: The Fractured But Whole''.]]\nZazul also makes an appearance in the video game ''[[South Park: The Fractured But Whole]]''. In the side mission [[Touch the Sky]], the player must help the [[Kanye West|Gay Fish]]'s mother get into [[Heaven]]. To do this, the player takes control of his mother riding a unicorn that farts rainbows. They must avoid columns while doing so, but towards the end, Zazul will appear and start shooting out fireballs that will hurt the player if they touch them.\n\n{{Clear}}\n\n==References==\n{{Reflist}}\n{{Minor Characters from Season Nine}}\n[[es:Zazul]]\n[[ja:ゼイザル]]\n[[pl:Zazul]]\n[[zh:扎祖尔]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Male characters]]\n[[Category:Minor characters]]\n[[Category:Characters voiced by Matt Stone]]\n[[Category:Characters from Hell]]\n[[Category:Demons]]\n[[Category:South Park: The Fractured But Whole]]"
