"{{South Park character\n|name = Human Pete Puma\n|image = Pete Puma.png\n|gender = Male\n|hair = Brown\n|age = 9-10\n|job = Student\n|voice = [[Trey Parker]]<ref>Credited as \"Disabled Kid #4\" in {{cite web|url=https://www.imdb.com/title/tt1644897/|title=\"South Park\" Crippled Summer (TV episode 2010)|publisher=IMDb.com}}</ref>\n|appearance = \"[[Crippled Summer]]\"\n}}\n'''Pete Puma''' is a character who appears in [[Season Fourteen]] episode, \"[[Crippled Summer]]\".\n\n==Background==\nHe is a student at [[Lake Tardicaca|Tardicaca Camp]], which is a camp for children with disabilities. He was on the [[Blue Team]] with [[Jimmy]], [[Timmy]], [[Lenora]], [[Droopy Dawg]], and [[Elmer Fudd]]. He later appears at the end of the episode saying he never saw a child being raped by a shark, which was what had happened to [[Nathan]]. His disability makes him look and sound like the Pete Puma character\n\n==Appearance==\nHe wears a blue long-sleeved shirt with the Lake Tardicaca logo and dark purple pants. He is almost always smiling and has a wide nose and a big mouth, his eyes are almost closed and he also has big hands.
