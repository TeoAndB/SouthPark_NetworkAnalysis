Gardner''' is a [[List of Female 4th Graders|female fourth grader]] at [[South Park Elementary]]. She is voiced by [[April Stewart]] and first appears in the [[Season Fifteen]] episode, \"[[Funnybot]]\".\n\n==Background==\nShe first appeared in the episode, \"[[Funnybot]]\", where she was nominated for the \"Funniest Kid in School\" award. Ultimately, she, along with [[Clyde Donovan|Clyde]], lost to [[Jimmy Valmer|Jimmy]].\n\nShe appeared in \"[[Faith Hilling]]\" as part of a group of children \"Taylor Swifting\" in front of [[Café Monet]].\n\nShe also appeared in \"[[Raising the Bar]]\", where she was seen in the background at [[Disneyland]].\n\nShe can be seen disgusted at Craig for \"breaking up\" with Tweek in \"[[Tweek x Craig]]\".\n\n==Appearance==\nKelly wears a pink shirt, a blue skirt and has short, brown hair. She also has two bows in her hair, either blue or yellow.\n\n<gallery widths=\"160\" spacing=\"small\" caption=\"Images of Kelly Gardner\">\nKellyWhaleWhores.JPG|Kelly in her debut appearance\nFunnybot05.png|Kelly was nominated for the \"Funniest Kid in School\" award in \"[[Funnybot]]\".\nFaithHilling00023.png|Kelly with her friends in \"[[Faith Hilling]]\".\n</gallery>\n\n==Personality==\nConsidering she was nominated for \"Funniest Kid in School\", we can assume she is a very funny, or at least, a very humorous person.
