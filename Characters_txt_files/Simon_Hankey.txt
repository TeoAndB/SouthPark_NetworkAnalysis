He is of low intelligence, due to him being born with a peanut in his head. He appears in the [[Season Four]] episode, \"[[A Very Crappy Christmas]]\", and ''[[South Park: The Stick of Truth]]''.\n\n==Appearance==\nSimon is a talking piece of excrement that has a peanut stuck in it, and like the rest of his family has gloves on.\n\n==Personality==\nIn his debut appearance, Simon seems to be mentally challenged as a result of being born with a peanut lodged in his head. However, by ''South Park: The Stick of Truth'', Simon is now speaking in full sentences and seems to be a bit more intelligent.\n\n==''[[South Park: The Stick of Truth]]''==\nAs part of Mr. Hankey's quest in the sewer, the New Kid must rescue Simon from a group of rats trying to eat his nut. Here, it is shown that he is capable of complete speech.\n\n==Prominence==\n*\"[[A Very Crappy Christmas]]\" - First appearance.
