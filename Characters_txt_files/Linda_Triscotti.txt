"{{South Park character\n|name = Linda Triscotti\n|image = Linda.png\n|gender = Female\n|hair = Dull Brown\n|job = Student\n|grade = [[The 4th Grade|4th Grade]]\n|religion = Roman Catholic\n|relation1 = Parents\n|relativename1 = [[Mr. and Mrs. Triscotti]]\n|appearance = \"[[Go God Go]]\"\n}}{{LinkDisambig|Linda (Disambiguation)}}\n'''Linda Triscotti''' is a character who appears in the [[Season Ten]] episode \"[[Go God Go]]\". She comes from a strict Catholic family who reprimanded the school for trying to teach her and the other [[The 4th Grade|4th grade]] students evolution. The only known information about her name is her last name, Triscotti.<ref>Minutes 5:40-5:45 in \"[[Go God Go]]\"</ref>\n\n==Appearance==\nShe has dull brown hair with a purple clip in it. She appears to be a combination of [[Sally Turner]] and [[Heidi Turner]]. She wears a green coat with red trim and dark blue pants.\n<gallery widths=\"120\" captionalign=\"left\" caption=\"Images of Linda Triscotti\">\nGirlwithGreenCoat_desk.png|Linda at her desk.\nGirlwithGreenCoat_Parents.png \nGirlwithGreenCoat_parents2.png|Linda being taken out of the Principal's office by [[Mr. and Mrs. Triscotti|her parents]].\n</gallery>\n==Personality==\nShe seems to have a compassion to learn, telling her parents that she wants to learn evolution despite their strict devotion to Catholicism. \n==Family==\n===[[Mr. and Mrs. Triscotti]]===\n[[File:GoGodGo045.png|thumb|130px|[[Mr. and Mrs. Triscotti]] yelling at [[Principal Victoria]] for allowing their daughter to learn evolution.]]\nIn \"[[Go God Go]]\", her parents learned that [[South Park Elementary]] was teaching evolution, which angered them due to them being strict Catholics.
