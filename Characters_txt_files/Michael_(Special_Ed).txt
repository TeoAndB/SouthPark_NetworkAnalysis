He is on the [[Red Team]] with [[Nathan]], [[Mimsy]], [[Francis (Special Ed)|Francis]], [[Chad Handler]], [[Reggie Burke]], [[Dennis Murray]], and [[Colette Francis]].\n\nMichael has unspecified motor disabilities and an eye disorder as well as learning difficulties, along with many of the other special education class members.\n\n==Appearance==\nMichael wears a gray short-sleeved shirt, black pants, black shoes, and a red cap with a blue border. He has brown hair and is almost always seen with his eyes closed.
