"{{South Park character\n|name = Canadian Door Guard\n|image = Canadian-doorman.png\n|gender = Male\n|hair = Brown\n|race = Canadian\n|voice = [[Matt Stone]]<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0705936/|title=\"South Park\" It's Christmas In Canada (TV Episode 2003)|publisher=IMDb.com}}</ref>\n|appearance = \"[[It's Christmas in Canada]]\"\n}}\nThe '''Canadian Door Guard''' is a minor character who appears in [[Season Seven]] episode \"[[It's Christmas in Canada]]\".\n\n==Background==\n[[The Boys]], [[Rick the Mountie]], [[Steve (Canadian)|Steve]] and the [[French Canadian Mime]] arrived in [[Ottawa]] to see the Prime Minister. They were turned away by the door guard who told the group that the Prime Minister is in [[China]] on an official trip. After seeing the boys cry, the guard admitted he was lying and the Prime Minister is in the building.\n\n==Appearance==\nThe door guard appears with a brown hair and a large brown mustache. He wears a royal guard suit and is armed with a rifle.
