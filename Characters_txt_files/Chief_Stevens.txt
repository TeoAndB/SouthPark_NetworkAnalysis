"{{South Park character\n|name = Chief Stevens\n|image = Chief-stevens.png\n|gender = Male\n|hair = Gray\n|job = Police Chief\n|voice = [[Matt Stone]]<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0705942/|title=\"Merry Christmas Charlie Manson!\" on IMDB}}</ref>\n|appearance = \"[[Merry Christmas Charlie Manson!]]\"\n}}\n'''Chief Stevens''' is a police chief who first appeared in the [[Season Two]] episode, \"[[Merry Christmas Charlie Manson!]]\".\n\n==Background==\nChief Stevens was seen with the other cops at the end of the episode outside of [[Mabel Cartman]]'s house, waiting for [[Charles Manson]] to surrender. When Charles, [[Howard Cartman|Howard]] and Cartman's other relatives finally leave the house, Charles says he really deserves to be arrested and sings a song about the importance of the holidays, which makes Stevens and all the other cops happy. Then he finally arrests Charles Mansons.\n\nChief Stevens later appeared in \"[[The Wacky Molestation Adventure]]\", where he is seen arresting the parents of children who claimed to have molested them.\n\n==Appearance==\nChief Stevens has messy light gray hair and light gray eyebrows. He wears a gray jacket with elbow sleeves, a black tie, a brown belt with a yellow buckle, black pants, and black shoes.\n\n==Prominence==\n*\"[[Merry Christmas Charlie Manson!]]\" - Debut\n*\"[[The Wacky Molestation Adventure]]\" - He arrests parents accused of molesting their children.\n*\"[[Cartman's Silly Hate Crime 2000]]\" - (Cameo)\n*\"[[Die Hippie, Die]]\" - Seen as a guard at City Hall.\n\n==References==\n{{reflist}}\n[[fr:Chief Stevens]]\n[[zh:史蒂文斯警长]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Male characters]]\n[[Category:Adult characters]]\n[[Category:Police officers]]\n[[Category:Minor characters]]"
