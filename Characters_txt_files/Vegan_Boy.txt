His name was not revealed. When [[Nichole Daniels|Nichole]] and [[Bebe Stevens|Bebe]] argued with [[Eric Cartman]], wanting healthy food to be served in school, he immediately joined the discussion. He joined the girls' side, asking for vegan food in school.\n\n==Appearance==\nThe Vegan Boy has dark brown hair. He wears a large yellow long-sleeved shirt with \"VEGAN\" in green written on it, blue pants, and black shoes.
