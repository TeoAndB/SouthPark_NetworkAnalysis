"{{South Park character\n|image = <gallery>\nAdults-mrs-malkinson.png|Current\nFuture Ellen.png|Elderly\n</gallery>\n|gender = Female\n|hair = Brown\n|relation1 = Son\n|relativename1 = [[Scott Malkinson]]\n|relation2 = Husband\n|relativename2 = [[Clark Malkinson]]\n|appearance = \"[[Basic Cable]]\"\n|last = \"[[South ParQ Vaccination Special]]\"\n}}\n'''Ellen Malkinson''' is the mother of [[Scott Malkinson]] who appears in [[Season Twenty-Three]] episode, \"[[Basic Cable]]\".\n\n==Background==\nAt the beginning of the episode, she serves breakfast to her son. Later when she and [[Clark Malkinson|her husband]] are watching television, Scott comes home and tells his parents about [[Sophie Gray|the diabetic girl]] he met at [[South Park Elementary|school]]. She is proud of him.\n\nIn \"[[South ParQ Vaccination Special]]\", she argues with her husband over letting their son go to school with [[Herbert Garrison]] returning as teacher. She thinks Scott needs to go to school while her husband thinks Garrison is a terrible teacher and does not want their son to taught by him. Her first name is revealed as \"Ellen\" in this episode. She is seen celebrating at Ms. Nelson’s funeral at the end of the episode.\n\n==Appearance==\nEllen Malkinson has short combed brown hair and is overweight. She wears a pale red sweater, green pants and black shoes. She also wears round gray glasses and very small earrings.\n\nIn the future timeline set in ''[[South Park: Post Covid]]'', her hair has significantly faded. She wears a slightly darker shade of green pants, and a pair of round gold glasses.\n\n==Family==\n===[[Scott Malkinson]]===\nScott is the son of Ellen Malkinson. They appear to have a good relationship, as she was glad Scott had met a girl with diabetes at school.\n\n===[[Clark Malkinson]]===\nClark is the husband of Ellen Malkinson. It is not clear what her relationship with Clark is. Clark was seen talking to her while watching television. In \"[[South ParQ Vaccination Special]]\", they are seen arguing over whether they should let their son go to school.\n\n==Prominence==\n*\"[[Basic Cable]]\" - Debut appearance.\n*\"[[The Pandemic Special]]\" - Appears in a Zoom conference with [[Mr. Mackey]] and other parents.\n*\"[[South ParQ Vaccination Special]]\" - Argues with her husband about letting their son go to school.\n*''[[South Park: The Fractured But Whole]]'' - Mentioned.\n\n===Movies===\n*''[[South Park: Post Covid]]'' - Appears in the [[Shady Acres]] retirement home.\n\n{{Minor Characters from Season Twenty-Three}}\n{{South Park Families}}\n[[ja:エレン・マルキンソン]]\n[[zh:艾伦·马尔金森]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Adult characters]]\n[[Category:Female characters]]\n[[Category:Overweight characters]]\n[[Category:Minor characters]]\n[[Category:Townsfolk]]\n[[Category:Parents]]"
