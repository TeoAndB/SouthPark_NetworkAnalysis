His first appearance was in \"[[Tweek vs. Craig]]\" as one of the students viewing the fight between [[Tweek Tweak|Tweek]] and [[Craig Tucker|Craig]].\n\n==Background==\nLouis also made an appearance in \"[[Super Fun Time]]\" as a member of [[The Boys|the Boys']] class on a field trip to the [[Pioneer Village]]. In \"[[Chinpokomon]]\", he received his first speaking role, and he later received his second speaking role twelve years later in \"[[Funnybot]]\", when he screams, \"It's the Germans!\" His religion is revealed to be Jewish in \"[[Jewbilee]]\".\n\n==Appearance==\nLouis wears black shoes, gray pants, a dark blue coat, and a red scarf with black stripes.
