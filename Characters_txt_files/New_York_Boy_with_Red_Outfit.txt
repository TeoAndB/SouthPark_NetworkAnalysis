He is also one of several cut characters from ''[[South Park: The Stick of Truth]]'' and he is seen on a poster at [[Raisins (Location)|Raisins]] in ''[[South Park: The Fractured But Whole]]''.\n\n==Appearance==\nHe has combed Black hair and wears a White shirt under a bright Red outfit with White stripes on the sides and a Gold chain. He has Black skin in the episode, but in his game appearance, he has Tanned skin.\n\n==Prominence==\n*\"[[World Wide Recorder Concert]]\" - He and the other [[New York Kids]] antagonize the boys during the episode.\n*\"[[Raisins]]\" - Seen on a poster at Raisins.\n*\"[[You Got F'd in the A]]\" - Seen on a poster at [[Raisins (Location)|Raisins]].\n\n==Trivia==\n*Like Smitty, he also appears in the [[Raisins (Location)|Raisins Restaurant]] on a poster.\n*In the ''[[South Park: The Stick of Truth]]'' game files, his character model is described as \"newyork_2\".\n[[Category:3rd graders]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Male characters]]\n[[Category:School children]]\n[[Category:Minor characters]]\n[[Category:Unnamed characters]]"
