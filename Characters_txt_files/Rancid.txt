They were one of the bands featured in the [[Season Two]] episode, \"[[Chef Aid]]\", as well as on the soundtrack ''[[Chef Aid: The South Park Album]]''.\n\n==Background==\nThe song featured on the show and on the album, called \"[[Brad Logan]]\", is about a friend named Brad Logan who has been in many bands and toured as a 'roadie' on many occasions for Rancid. This song is not available on any of Rancid's regular releases.\n\nThe band makes a guest appearance on \"[[Chef Aid]]\" performing the song. They also appear in the mockumentary, [[Chef Aid: Behind the Menu]].\n\n==Appearance==\nThe band appears as typical punk rock band with t-shits, denim jean pants, and spiky hair.\n<gallery widths=\"160\" captionalign=\"left\" caption=\"Images of Rancid\">\nCaliforniaSunk.png|''\"California Sunk!\"''\n</gallery>\n\n==Personality==\nThey are very altruistic, as shown when they play for free to raise money for [[Chef]] in \"[[Chef Aid]]\".\n\n==Prominence==\n*\"[[Chef Aid]]\"\n*\"[[Christian Rock Hard]]\"\n\n==References==\n{{Reflist}}\n{{MusicCelebrities}}\n[[es:Rancid]]\n[[ja:ランシド]]\n[[zh:腐臭乐队]]\n[[Category:Music celebrities]]\n[[Category:Bands]]\n[[Category:Celebrities]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Minor characters]]\n[[Category:Male characters]]\n[[Category:Groups]]\n[[Category:Adult characters]]"
