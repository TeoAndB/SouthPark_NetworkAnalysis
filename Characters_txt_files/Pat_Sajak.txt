He appears in the [[Season Eleven]] episode, \"[[With Apologies to Jesse Jackson]]\", hosting ''Wheel of Fortune''. \n\n==Background==\nWhen [[Randy Marsh]] blurts out the wrong word instead of \"Naggers\", Pat Sajak is visibly disappointed and abruptly cuts the show to a commercial break. He also makes a brief cameo appearance in the [[Season Twelve]] episode \"[[Britney's New Look]]\", among the crowd of people taking pictures of [[Britney Spears]] at the end of the episode.\n\n==Appearance==\nPat Sajak can be seen wearing a gray, formal suit with a white, thick collared shirt and a red tie.
