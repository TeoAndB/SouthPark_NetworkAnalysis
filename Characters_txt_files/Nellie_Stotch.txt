She lives in [[Los Angeles]], California with her husband, [[Budd Stotch|Budd]]. Her only appearances are in \"[[AWESOM-O]]\" and \"[[Britney's New Look]]\".\n\n==Appearance==\nShe wears a pink, sleeveless button-up shirt, blue pants, and white shoes. She has wavy, blonde hair and small navy blue earrings.\n\n==Personality==\nShe is very kind, being very loving to Butters in \"[[AWESOM-O]]\". She is pretty much the only family member who is entirely kind to Butters.\n\n==Family==\n===[[Budd Stotch]]===\nShe is married to [[Budd Stotch]], they have not been seen fighting, so it is assumed they have a fairly good marriage. She is oblivious to the fact that Budd molested Butters.\n\n===[[Butters Stotch]]===\nShe is Butters' aunt, it is assumed she loves him very much, judging by her reaction when he first arrived from the airport in \"[[AWESOM-O]]\".\n\n==Prominence==\n*\"[[AWESOM-O]]\" - Greets Butters and [[Eric Cartman]], when they arrive from airport.\n*\"[[Britney's New Look]]\" - Seen taking photos of [[Britney Spears]].\n\n==References==\n{{Reflist}}\n{{Stotch Family}}\n{{South Park Families}}\n{{Minor Characters from Season Eight}}\n[[zh:内莉·斯多奇]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Female characters]]\n[[Category:Minor characters]]\n[[Category:Adult characters]]\n[[Category:Characters in a relationship]]\n[[Category:Characters voiced by April Stewart]]\n[[Category:Stotch Family]]"
