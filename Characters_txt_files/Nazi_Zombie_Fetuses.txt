"{{South Park character\n|name = Nazi Zombie Fetuses\n|image = Nazi-zombie-fetuses.png\n|race = Zombies\n|appearance = ''[[South Park: The Stick of Truth]]''\n}}\nThe '''Nazi Zombie Fetuses''' are enemies that appear in ''[[South Park: The Stick of Truth]]''.\n\n==Background==\nThe fetuses are first encountered during the \"[[Unplanned Parenthood]]\" quest, when green goo was leaked into the [[Unplanned Parenthood Clinic]]. In the clinic, the government agents struggled to contain the situation, with most being killed by the fetuses. However [[The New Kid]] fought their way through the clinic, before being approached by the [[Giant Nazi Zombie Fetus]].\n\n==Abilities==\n*'''Ranged Attack''' - Spews four balls of vomit on both the New Kid and buddy (two vomit balls per character).\n*'''Brain Damage''' - Leaps at target dealing medium damage and inflicts Defense Down if not blocked.\n*'''Projectile Vomit''' - Vomits on the New Kid or buddy, deals Ability Down if not blocked.\n[[zh:纳粹僵尸胎儿]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:The Stick of Truth: Enemies]]\n[[Category:The Stick of Truth: Characters]]\n[[Category:Nazi Zombies]]\n[[Category:Video Game Exclusive characters]]"
