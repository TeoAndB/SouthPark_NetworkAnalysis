I'm Scared''. This prank involves Howard taking up a job as a gardener at [[Troubled Acres]], caring for plants that \"talks\" and has the ability to turn [[Goth Kids|goths]] and [[South Park Vampire Society|vamp kids]] into [[Emo Kids|emos]].\n\nAt the end of the prank, Sarah appears beside Howard in front of the crew's camera. The couple laughs off the prank, while the goths and vamp kids could only watch in bewilderment.\n\n==Appearance==\nSarah Flannigan has a long, light brown hair and large nose.
