After the shootout, he was carried off into an ambulance while [[Franz]] was arrested.\n\n==Appearance==\nHe has a dark brown, blood-splattered Mohican haircut and wears a wireless headset, a black sweater, dark grey pants, and a shoulder bag slung over his left shoulder.
