The nuke kills thousands of Canadians, and heavily injured many, including Justin Trudeau. While on a live news report, Trudeau angrily threatens retaliation against [[Herbert Garrison|Garrison]] and the entire [[United States]].\n\n==Appearance==\nTrudeau is a flappy headed Canadian with smoldering brown hair, and ashes on his face.
