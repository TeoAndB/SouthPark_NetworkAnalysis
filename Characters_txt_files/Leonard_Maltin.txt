"{{South Park character\n|name = Leonard Maltin\n|image = Leonard-maltin.png\n|gender = Male\n|race = Caucasian\n|hair = Brown\n|age = {{age|1950|12|18}}\n|dob = December 18, 1950\n|job = Film Critic<br>Food Critic\n|religion = Judaism\n|voice = [[Trey Parker]]<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0705941/|title=\"South Park\" Mecha-Streisand (TV episode 1998)|publisher=IMDb.com}}</ref>\n|wikipedia = [[Wikipedia:Leonard Maltin|Leonard Maltin]]\n|appearance = \"[[Mecha-Streisand]]\"\n}}\n'''Leonard Maltin''' is an American film critic who appears in the episode, \"[[Mecha-Streisand]]\", in which he assists the town of [[South Park (Location)|South Park]] in defeating [[Barbra Streisand]] by transforming into a Japanese ''kaiju'' form. Although both he and [[Sidney Poitier]] fail in their efforts, [[Robert Smith]] manages to destroy Streisand in the end. Maltin transforms into an Ultraman-type character in order to fight Mecha-Streisand. He has yet to make another appearance on the show.\n\n==Appearance==\nLeonard Maltin, as he appears as a human being, wears a black suit with a white shirt and a black tie. He has brown hair, brown eyebrows, and a big gray beard. He also wears red framed glasses.\n\nAs he appears as an Ultraman-type fighter, he is a gray robot with red trim, yellow eyes, and his initials, LM, in red text on his chest.\n<gallery widths=\"160\" caption=\"Images of Leonard Maltin\">\nMecha-Streisand086.png|Leonard Maltin fighting [[Barbra Streisand|Mecha-Streisand]].\n</gallery>\n\n==Personality==\nHe is brave and heroic, as he is willing to battle Mecha-Streisand by transforming into an Ultraman-like being.
