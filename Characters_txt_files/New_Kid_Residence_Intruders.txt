Through a tutorial on the selected [[Classes|class]], the New Kid takes down the intruders in each of the four missions. It was revealed in [[Farts of Future Past]] that the intruders' true intention was to obtain a selfie with the New Kid.\n\n==Appearance==\nPercy (also known as the Alpha Intruder while in combat) wears a large red cap, and brown jacket with a navy blue shirt underneath, black gloves, a pair of black pants and black sports shoes. Percy has a large scar running along the left side of his face.\n\nJames wears a navy blue cap, grey jacket, black gloves, a pair of denim, and black shoes.\n\nJesse is usually seen carrying a crowbar. He wears a black beanie, black collared shirt, grey pants and black boots.\n\n==Abilities==\n*'''Wicked Crowbar''' - Jesse flings a crowbar onto the New Kid.\n*'''Vicious Crowbar''' - James flings a crowbar onto the New Kid.\n*'''Home Invader Special''' - Exclusive to Percy.
