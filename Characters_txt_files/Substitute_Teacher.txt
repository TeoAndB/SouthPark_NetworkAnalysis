"{{South Park character\n|name = Substitute Teacher\n|image = Substitute-teacher.png\n|gender = Female\n|hair = Brown\n|job = [[South Park Elementary]] Teacher\n|voice = [[April Stewart]]<ref>Credited as \"Teacher\" in {{Cite web|url=https://www.imdb.com/title/tt4063638/|title=\"South Park\" Gluten Free Ebola (TV Episode 2014)|publisher=IMDb.com}}</ref>\n|appearance = \"[[With Apologies to Jesse Jackson]]\"\n}}\nThe '''Substitute Teacher''' is an unnamed teacher at [[South Park Elementary]]. She is first seen acting as a substitute for the kindergarten class, but continues to appear as a minor faculty member throughout the show.\n\n==Background==\nThe teacher is first seen at South Park Elementary in \"[[With Apologies to Jesse Jackson]]\" at a school assembly introducing [[Dr. David Nelson]] to the school. She has since made frequent background appearances in assemblies or meetings involving the school teachers.\n\nShe is seen teaching the [[Kindergartners|kindergarten class]] in \"[[Fatbeard]]\" during the morning announcements, but a new teacher takes over that class later that season and she continues to appear.\n\nIn \"[[Gluten Free Ebola]]\", she makes a frustrated remark that they are running behind because of [[Mr. Mackey]] once again, and the other teachers begin discussing his gluten-free diet.\n\nShe is also present at the larger staff meeting in \"[[SUPER HARD PCness]]\", where she is one of the teachers who overhears \"[[Hold My Hand]]\" by Hootie and the Blowfish playing in the background.\n\n==Appearance==\nThe substitute teacher has bright brown parted hair with visible eyelashes and white earrings. She wears a pink buttoned shirt with a collar and a pearl necklace under a pale yellow jacket. She also wears a long purple skirt and has deep purple shoes.\n\n==Prominence==\n*\"[[With Apologies to Jesse Jackson]]\" - Introduces Dr. Nelson at the school assembly.\n*\"[[Eek, A Penis!]]\" - Seen when Garrison returns to the school.\n*\"[[Fatbeard]]\" - Seen teaching the kindergarten class.\n*\"[[Funnybot]]\" - Seen when the school is held hostage by the Germans.\n*\"[[Sarcastaball]]\" - Seen attending the [[Sarcastaball PTA Meeting]] in the library.\n*\"[[Taming Strange]]\" - Seen with other teachers.\n*\"[[Gluten Free Ebola]]\" - Complains about [[Mr. Mackey]]'s gluten-free diet.\n*\"[[Stunning and Brave]]\" - Present when [[PC Principal]] is first introduced.\n*\"[[Put It Down]]\" - Seen singing [[Put It Down (Song)|the song]] at the climax.\n*\"[[Hummels & Heroin]]\" - Seen in the [[South Park Elementary Cafeteria|school cafeteria]] during a meeting.\n*\"[[SUPER HARD PCness]]\" - Seen with other teachers at a school meeting and later in the [[South Park Elementary Gymnasium|gymnasium]].\n\n==References==\n{{reflist}}\n{{South Park Elementary Staff}}\n{{Minor Characters from Season Eleven}}\n[[zh:代课老师]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Adult characters]]\n[[Category:Female characters]]\n[[Category:School faculty]]\n[[Category:Townsfolk]]\n[[Category:Minor characters]]\n[[Category:Unnamed characters]]\n[[Category:Characters voiced by April Stewart]]"
