After their last show at the [[Cirque Du Cheville]] in the U.S., she attempted to help the Quintuplets stay in the U.S. due to their mother's wishes. She later asked the Marshes if she could stay in their [[Marsh Residence|house]] for a night. That night, she had sex with [[Marvin Marsh]], which resulted in her death.\n\n==Appearance==\nShe has gray hair, various facial wrinkles, and a double chin.
