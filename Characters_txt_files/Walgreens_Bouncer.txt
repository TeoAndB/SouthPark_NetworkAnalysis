"{{South Park character\n|name = Walgreens Bouncer\n|image = Buinesss-Walgreens-bouncer.png\n|gender = Male\n|race = Caucasian\n|hair = Brown\n|job = Security Guard\n|appearance = \"[[South ParQ Vaccination Special]]\"\n}}\nThe '''Walgreens Bouncer''' is a security guard who appears in [[Season Twenty-Four]] episode \"[[South ParQ Vaccination Special]]\".\n\n==Background==\nIn a vaccination program at [[Walgreens]], the [[COVID-19 Vaccine]]s are reserved to elderly people aged 55 and above and first responders. Despite attempts by [[Mr. Mackey]], [[Richard Adler]], the [[Underpants Gnomes]] and few other characters to get the vaccines, the bouncer turns them away, even escorting Mackey off when the latter attempts to get in dressed as a fireman.\n\nLater, as [[The Boys|the boys]] plan to steal the vaccines for [[Margaret Nelson]] and the other teachers, they show up at Walgreens with a [[Elderly Woman in Wheelchair|wheelchair-bound elderly woman]]. They claim to be a non-profit organization known as the [[Kommunity Kidz]] who escort elderly to the vaccination locations. The bouncer lets them in.\n\n==Appearance==\nThe bouncer is well-built with tattoos on both arms. He has brown hair with a five o' clock shadow. He wears a black shirt, pants and shoes, an earphone on his left ear, a pair of sunglasses and a mask bearing the words \"SECURITY\".\n\n{{Minor Characters from Season Twenty-Four}}\n[[ja:ウォルグリーンの用心棒]]\n[[zh:沃尔格林药房保安]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Male characters]]\n[[Category:Adult characters]]\n[[Category:One-off characters]]\n[[Category:Unnamed characters]]"
