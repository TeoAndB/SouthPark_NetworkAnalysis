During the actual heist, Neely is stationed outside the [[Smithsonian Natural History Museum]] with [[John McCain]], awaiting Barack Obama to open the museum's northwest exit from inside. After Barack Obama successfully retrieves the Hope Diamond, Neely works with [[Brad Davis]] in patching up the hole which Barack broke through. At the end of the episode, he, along with the other members of the thief club, fake their deaths and leave for paradise.\n\n==Appearance==\nTimothy Neely has dark brown hair with light brown highlights, brown eyebrows, and a five o' clock shadow.\n\nHe wears two different outfits during the episode. For most of the episode, he wears a brown suit over a black shirt, brown pants, black shoes, and a pair of glasses.\n\nAt the airport at the end of the episode, he wears a green/gray vest over a white shirt, blue shorts, white sneakers, and a pair of sunglasses.\n\n==References==\n{{Reflist}}\n[[zh:提莫希·尼利]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Male characters]]\n[[Category:Adult characters]]\n[[Category:One-off characters]]\n[[Category:Characters voiced by Matt Stone]]\n[[Category:Criminals]]"
