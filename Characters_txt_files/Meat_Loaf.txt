"{{South Park character\n|name = Meat Loaf\n|image = Meat-loaf.png\n|aliases = Marvin Lee Aday\n|gender = Male\n|race = Caucasian\n|hair = Brown\n|age = {{age|1947|9|27}}\n|dob = September 27, 1947\n|job = Actor, Musician\n|religion = Christian\n|voice = Meat Loaf<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0705907/|title=\"South Park\" Chef Aid (TV episode 1998)|publisher=IMDb.com}}</ref>\n|wikipedia = [[Wikipedia:Meat Loaf|Meat Loaf]]\n|appearance = \"[[Chef Aid]]\"\n}}\n'''Meat Loaf''' is the stage name of an American rock musician and actor named Marvin Lee Aday. He appears in the [[Season Two]] episode, \"[[Chef Aid]]\", where he donates to [[Chef]]'s cause.\n\n==Background==\nIn \"[[Chef Aid]]\" he revealed that he was performing under the name Couscous, until Chef brought him some meatloaf and suggested a name change.\n\nHe also appeared in the [[Season Seven]] episode, \"[[Christian Rock Hard]]\", as one of the musicians protesting illegal music downloading.\n\nMeat Loaf performed the song \"Tonight Is Right for Love\" with Chef on ''[[Chef Aid: The South Park Album]]''. He also appeared in the accompanying mockumentary, ''[[Chef Aid: Behind the Menu]]'', in which he talked about Chef's influence on his career.\n\n==Appearance==\nHe appears as a musician with long brown hair wearing gray tuxedo consisting of a gray coat, a white formal shirt, and a black bow tie.\n\nIn the mockumentary ''[[Chef Aid: Behind the Menu]]'', he appears with short brown, graying hair, wearing a black Hawaiian shirt with colored palm trees on it.\n\n<gallery widths=\"160\" caption=\"Images of Meat Loaf\">\nMeatloaf.JPG|Meat Loaf in ''[[Chef Aid: Behind the Menu]]''.\n</gallery>\n\n==Prominence==\n*\"[[Chef Aid]]\" - Performs with Chef.\n*\"[[Christian Rock Hard]]\" - Protests.\n\n==References==\n{{Reflist}}\n{{MusicCelebrities}}\n[[es:Meat Loaf]]\n[[ja:ミートローフ（キャラクター）]]\n[[zh:密特·劳弗]]\n[[Category:Celebrities]]\n[[Category:Minor characters]]\n[[Category:Music celebrities]]\n[[Category:Male characters]]\n[[Category:Adult characters]]\n[[Category:Characters]]"
