"{{South Park character\n|name = Matthew Klinner\n|image = Adults-townsfolk-matthew-klinner-contest-winner-cc.png\n|gender = Male\n|hair = Brown\n|appearance = \"[[Sexual Healing]]\" (intro)\n}}\n'''Matthew Klinner''' was the winner of the \"Become a South Park Citizen\" sweepstakes who appeared at the end of the [[South Park Title Sequence|title sequence]] specifically for the [[Season Fourteen]] premiere episode \"[[Sexual Healing]]\".\n\n==Background==\n[[File:Matthew-Klinner-Intro.png|thumb|left|140px|Matthew in intro.]]Matthew Klinner's character was animated into the opening title sequence in the ending crowd scene along with [[The Boys]], standing, waving, and smiling at the viewer next to the South Park sign. His character was created based on photographs that he sent to the South Park artists. Additionally, he was given a special poster signed by South Park creators [[Trey Parker]] and [[Matt Stone]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://southpark.cc.com/blog/2010/03/17/season-premiere-tonight|title=Season Premiere Tonight!|date=March 17, 2010}}</ref>\n\n==Appearance==\nMatthew's character has brown hair that is combed on the top and shaved short on the sides and brown eyebrows. He wears a green shirt with two white skull designs on it and blue jeans.\n\n==Trivia==\n* Matthew was the second of four ''[[South Park]]'' fans who have guest starred on the show. The other guests include:\n**[[Alex Glick]], who appeared in \"[[Red Man's Greed]]\".\n**[[Hugh]] who appeared in \"[[Dead Kids]]\" and \"[[Nobody Got Cereal?]]\".\n**[[Sef Furman]], who appeared in \"[[The Pandemic Special]]\".\n\n==References==\n{{reflist}}\n{{Minor Characters from Season Fourteen}}\n[[ja:マシュー・クリンナー]]\n[[zh:马修·科林纳]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Male characters]]\n[[Category:One-off characters]]\n[[Category:Background characters]]\n[[Category:Teens]]"
