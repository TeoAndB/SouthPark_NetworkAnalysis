She still appears in current episodes, but very rarely.\n\nIn ''[[South Park: The Fractured But Whole]]'', she appears as a retirement home employee.\n\n==Appearance==\nNurse Fran has brown hair with a ponytail and brown eyebrows and wears red lipstick. She wears a cyan uniform over a bright pink V-neck shirt with a badge on the right side and black shoes. In \"[[Tonsil Trouble]]\" she had blonde hair.
