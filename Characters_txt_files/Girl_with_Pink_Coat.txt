"{{South Park character\n|name = Girl with Pink Coat\n|image = Girl with Pink Coat.png\n|gender = Female\n|hair = Dark brown\n|age = 9-10\n|job = Student\n|grade = [[The 4th Grade|4th grade]]\n|appearance = \"[[Proper Condom Use]]\"\n}}\n'''Girl with Pink Coat''' is an unnamed [[List of Female 4th Graders|female fourth grader]] who first appears in \"[[Proper Condom Use]]\" and later appears frequently throughout [[Season Nineteen]], beginning with \"[[Where My Country Gone?]]\".\n\n==Background==\nShe was initially a background character, only appearing as just another student at school assemblies. However, in \"[[Where My Country Gone?]]\", she was seen chatting with [[Leslie Meyers]]. During the assembly, [[PC Principal]] caught them both talking, and ordered Leslie to stop talking. Throughout the rest of the season, she appears at school assemblies sitting next to Leslie.\n\n==Appearance==\nShe has long dark brown hair, purple pants, and a pink coat with a dark red collar. She appears to be a recolor of [[Esther]], who is also a background character.\n\n==Relationships==\n===[[Leslie Meyers]]===\nShe is friends with Leslie, and the two are usually seen talking together, even at school assemblies.\n\n==Prominence==\n[[File:Leslie's_friend.png|thumb|Girl with Pink Coat seen at the school assembly.]]\n*\"[[Proper Condom Use]]\" - Seen in the [[South Park Elementary Cafeteria]].\n*\"[[Butt Out]]\" - Seen at the school assembly.\n*\"[[ManBearPig]]\" - Seen at the assembly.\n*\"[[1%]]\" - Seen at the assembly.\n*\"[[Where My Country Gone?]]\" - Shown in the gym, chatting with Leslie.\n*\"[[Safe Space]]\" - Shown in the gym, again chatting with Leslie.\n*\"[[Tweek x Craig]]\" - Shown in the gym, not chatting but still sitting next to Leslie.\n*\"[[Naughty Ninjas]]\" - Shown in the gym, not chatting but still sitting next to Leslie.\n*\"[[Oh, Jeez]]\" - Seen in the school assembly.\n\n{{Minor Characters from Season Nineteen}}\n{{South Park Children}}\n[[zh:穿粉色外套的女孩]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Female characters]]\n[[Category:Female 4th graders]]\n[[Category:Unnamed characters]]\n[[Category:4th graders]]\n[[Category:Other 4th graders]]\n[[Category:Background characters]]\n[[Category:School children]]"
