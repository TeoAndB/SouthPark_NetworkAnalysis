"{{South Park character\n|name = Tommy Edwards\n|image = Tommy-edwards.png\n|gender = Male\n|hair = Strawberry-Blonde\n|job = Student\n|voice = [[Matt Stone]]\n|appearance = \"[[Bass to Mouth]]\"\n}}{{LinkDisambig|Thomas (Disambiguation)}}\n'''Tommy Edwards''' is a student at [[South Park Elementary]] who appears in the [[Season Fifteen]] episode, \"[[Bass to Mouth]]\".\n\n==Background==\n[[File:TommyEdwards.png|thumb|left]]\nWhen [[The Boys|the boys]] and other students from [[Herbert Garrison|Mr. Garrison's]] class discover and confront [[Wikileaks]] as the editor of [[Eavesdropper]], Wikileaks managed to escape. Tommy was in the library typing a text message to a girl named Marla Watkins, a crush of his, when Wikileaks stole his phone and posted his findings on Eavesdropper. [[Butters Stotch|Butters]] tried to catch and stop [[Wikileaks]], but was too late to prevent the story appearing on Eavesdropper.\n\n==Appearance==\nTommy has messy strawberry-orange hair. He wears a red long sleeve shirt with orange trimming and uses a black iPhone 4. His school supplies include a purple binder, a tan Broncos backpack, and a brown envelope.\n\n==Personality==\nFrom what is shown in the episode, it can be assumed that Tommy is a little shy and reserved, given that he decided to communicate his affections for his crush through text messaging.\n\n{{South Park Children}}\n[[zh:汤米·爱德华兹]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Male characters]]\n[[Category:One-off characters]]\n[[Category:School children]]\n[[Category:Characters in a relationship]]"
