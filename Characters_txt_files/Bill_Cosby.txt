He first appear in the [[Season Five]] episode, \"[[Here Comes the Neighborhood]]\", as one of the wealthy African-Americans who move to [[South Park (Location)|South Park]]. \n\nHe returned as a hologram in the [[Season Eighteen]] episode, \"[[HappyHolograms|#HappyHolograms]]\" where he is shown trying to molest pop star [[Taylor Swift]] during the ''[[Washington Redskins: Go Fuck Yourself Holiday Special|Go Fuck Yourself Holiday Special]]''. \n\n==Appearance==\nBill Cosby is elderly, having gray hair as well as various facial wrinkles.
