After the New Kid uses the shrink dust to enter a passageway under the floor of the abortion clinic, some gnomes are found along the way.\n\n== Appearance ==\nNazi zombie gnomes have light-green skin and smoldy green beards, they have a swastika and an eagle on their hats, except for one which has only the swastika.\n\n==Abilities==\n\n*'''Financial Arbitration''' - The gnome drops gold gnome coins that inflicts ability down if not blocked.\n*'''Fiscal Impact''' - The gnome summons fire balls that inflicts defense down if not blocked.\n*'''Gloomy Forecast Earnings''' - The gnome summons frost and snow that inflicts slowing debuff if not blocked.\n*'''Nazi Zombie Bite''' - The gnome attempts to bite the player, inflicting gross out if not blocked.\n\n[[zh:纳粹僵尸侏儒]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:The Stick of Truth: Characters]]\n[[Category:The Stick of Truth: Enemies]]\n[[Category:Groups]]\n[[Category:Nazi Zombies]]\n[[Category:Male characters]]\n[[Category:Video Game Exclusive characters]]"
