He appears in [[Season Eleven]] episode \"[[Guitar Queer-O]]\".\n\n==Background==\nWhen [[Stan Marsh|Stan]] and [[Kyle Broflovski|Kyle]] reached 100,000 points on Guitar Hero, they got the attention of [[Charles Kincade]], who offered to be the boys' manager. Charles brought them to Alex Hart, who was impressed with the boys' performance in Guitar Hero, signing them into a contract and inviting them to a party at his mansion. Charles would later replace Kyle with [[Thad Jarvis]].\n\nMeanwhile, Alex secure both Stan and Thad a Guitar Hero station in [[Gamecraft Arcade]] for practice, hoping for them to break one million points.\n\n==Appearance==\nAlex Hart wears a grey business suit, black shoes, and a pair of tinted glasses.\n\n==References==\n{{Reflist}}\n{{Minor Characters from Season Eleven}}\n[[es:Alex Hart]]\n[[fr:Alex Hart]]\n[[zh:亚历克斯·哈特]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Male characters]]\n[[Category:Adult characters]]\n[[Category:One-off characters]]\n[[Category:Characters voiced by Trey Parker]]"
