"{{South Park character\n|name = Pharaoh of Egypt\n|image = Historical-figures-ramesses-2.png\n|gender = Male\n|race = Middle Eastern\n|job = Pharaoh of Egypt\n|religion = Polytheism\n|appearance = \"[[Jewpacabra]]\"\n}}\nThe '''Pharaoh of Egypt''' appeared in the [[Season Sixteen]] episode, \"[[Jewpacabra]]\".\n\n==Background==\n[[Eric Cartman]] had a dream that he went back to [[Ancient Egypt]]. The Pharaoh of Egypt was his father during the events of the biblical account of the plagues prior to the [[Hebrews]]' Exodus.\n\nHe comforted his son by singing a [[Our Love Grows|song]] together and promising he would not die because their god is more loving. It was only when his son died, that he realized he was wrong.\n\n==Appearance==\nThe Pharaoh of Egypt wore white pants, a blue and red belt, a gold belt buckle with a sapphire, a blue and red cloak with gold trimming, a gold crown, and a gold device on his chin. He wore a white headdress, and also wore gold bracers.
