He appears in the [[Season Two]] episode \"[[City on the Edge of Forever (Flashbacks)]]\".\n\n==Background==\n[[Veronica Crabtree]], who is trying to look for help rescuing schoolchildren from her school bus, ends up performing in a talent show instead. An agent takes her to Marty Jonus, and the latter becomes impressed with her insults. Marty then offers her a million-dollar deal, allowing her to appear in ''The Tonight Show with [[Jay Leno]]''.\n\n==Appearance==\nMarty Jonus has balding brown hair with gray highlights.
