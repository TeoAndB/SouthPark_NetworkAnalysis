"{{South Park character\n|name = Bob Summers\n|image = Adults-townsfolk-bob-summers.png\n|gender = Male\n|hair = Gray\n|appearance = \"[[Britney's New Look]]\"\n}}\n'''Bob Summers''' is an elderly man who first appear in the [[Season Twelve]] episode, \"[[Britney's New Look]]\".\n\n==Background==\nIn \"[[Britney's New Look]]\" he was seen as a resident of a small town that was among the many people in the country who wanted to make the \"Britney sacrifice\", he explains to [[Kyle Broflovski|Kyle]] that in ancient times they used to choose a lovely girl and treat her as a goddess and then watch her die. He is finally seen at the end of the episode with the crowd taking pictures of [[Britney Spears]] until she dies.\n\nHe later appeared in \"[[Over Logging]]\" where he is seen talking to Randy and others about the lack of internet.\n\nHe apparently moved to [[South Park (Location)|South Park]], as he is seen in the retirement home in some recent episodes like \"[[Hummels & Heroin]]\" and \"[[Time To Get Cereal]]\".\n\n==Appearance==\nBob Summers has gray hair, thick gray eyebrows, big ears and a lot of wrinkles on his face. He wears a light blue coat with two side pockets, a white shirt underneath, a black belt with a gray buckle, brown pants and dark brown shoes. In \"[[Over Logging]]\" he wore a unbuttoned jacket over his coat and a cowboy hat with a black belt.\n\n==Prominence==\n*\"[[Britney's New Look]]\"\n*\"[[Over Logging]]\"\n*\"[[Oh, Jeez]]\" - Seen in an airport of [[Denmark]].\n*\"[[Hummels & Heroin]]\"\n*\"[[Time To Get Cereal]]\"\n*\"[[South ParQ Vaccination Special]]\" - He is seen celebrating the end of the pandemic with the crowd and later seen with the other elderly people, returning to retirement home.\n[[fr:Bob Summers]]\n[[ja:ボブ・サマーズ]]\n[[zh:鲍勃·萨默斯]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Male characters]]\n[[Category:Adult characters]]\n[[Category:Elderly people]]\n[[Category:Townsfolk]]\n[[Category:Minor characters]]"
