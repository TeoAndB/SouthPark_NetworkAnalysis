He appears in the [[Season Seven]] episode \"[[All About Mormons]]\".\n\n==Appearance==\nDave has combed strawberry-blond hair. He wears a collared white shirt under a pair of blue overalls and black shoes.\n\n==Personality==\nDave's personality appears to be shaped largely by his family and religion, Mormonism. He is overly friendly and optimistic, like the rest of his family.\n\n==Family==\n===Parents===\nDave is very close to his parents and they are the people who taught him The Book of Mormon.\n\n===Siblings===\nDave has two brothers and two sisters. They all share a strong bond and are friendly to each other. They never fight and enjoy spending time together.
