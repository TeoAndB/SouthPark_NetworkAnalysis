"{{South Park character\n|name = Danny Ray Johnson\n|image = 4th-graders-other-4th-graders-danny-ray-johnston.png\n|gender = Male\n|hair = Blonde/Light Brown\n|age = 10\n|job = Student\n|grade = [[The 4th Grade|4th grade]]\n|appearance = \"[[Shots]]\"\n}}\n'''Danny Ray Johnson''' is a male [[The 4th Grade|4th grader]] who appears in the [[Season Twenty-Three]] episode \"[[Shots]]\".\n\n==Background==\nDanny appears as the first child to be immunized at the CDC Child Immunization Challenge. According to the announcer of the event, Danny has not been immunized since he was in kindergarten and that he just does not like needles. It is also stated that he is from Bailey, Colorado. He is chased around by the wranglers and tied up to be given his shot by Dr. Chete Mollins. He is then seen taking a toy from the toy chest while his parents wait for him.\n\n==Appearance==\nDanny has blonde/light brown hair that has a few spikes on the top. He wears a dark blue and light blue button-up shirt, blue pants, and black shoes.
