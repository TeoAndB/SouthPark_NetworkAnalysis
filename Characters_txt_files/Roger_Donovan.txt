He was mentioned in \"[[Terrance and Phillip: Behind the Blow]]\", and appeared in \"[[Red Hot Catholic Love]]\" at the book meeting. He was usually seen with [[Betsy Donovan|his wife]] until her death. His name was confirmed in \"[[Reverse Cowgirl]]\".\n\nHe is the widower of [[Betsy Donovan]].\n\n==Background==\n===Occupation===\nHe is first mentioned as a geologist in \"[[Terrance and Phillip: Behind the Blow]]\" but in \"[[The List]]\" it is revealed he owns a shoe store, which is the basis of the episode's plotline, as the girls corrupt the list of cutest boys so they can date [[Clyde Donovan|Clyde]] for free shoes.\n\n==Appearance==\nHe has dark brown hair, a large chin, and glasses. He also wears a dark green coat over a white buttoned shirt, with the collar over the coat, and dark blue pants.\n\n==Family==\n===[[Betsy Donovan]]===\nHe was Betsy Donovan's husband, however, he is now a widower as of \"[[Reverse Cowgirl]]\".\n\n===[[Clyde Donovan]]===\nHe is Clyde's father, however, their relationship has not been expanded upon, so not much is known about their relationship.\n\n==Prominence==\n{{Incomplete|item=list}}\n*\"[[City on the Edge of Forever (Flashbacks)]]\" - (mentioned only)\n*\"[[Red Hot Catholic Love]]\" - Questions the Catholic Church along with the other parents.\n*\"[[It's Christmas in Canada]]\" - Seen in the crowd of people at the Christmas tree lighting in South Park.\n*\"[[The Losing Edge]]\" - Cheers for the South Park little league team along with the other parents.\n*\"[[Ginger Kids]]\" - Finds Clyde missing from his bedroom.\n*\"[[Cartoon Wars Part I]]\" - Seen in the crowd at the town's meeting.\n*\"[[Mystery of the Urinal Deuce]]\" - Tells [[Mr. Mackey]] about Clyde's colostomy.\n*\"[[The List]]\" - It is revealed that he owns a shoe shop.
