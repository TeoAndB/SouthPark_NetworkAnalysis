"{{South Park character\n|name = Officer Bright\n|image = Officer-bright.png\n|gender = Male\n|race = Caucasian\n|hair = Brown\n|job = Police Officer\n|voice = [[Trey Parker]]\n|appearance = \"[[Splatty Tomato]]\"\n}}\n'''Officer Bright''' is a police officer who made his first appearance in the [[Season Twenty-One]] finale episode \"[[Splatty Tomato]]\".\n\n==Background==\nIn \"[[Splatty Tomato]]\", Officer Bright leads the search to find [[Herbert Garrison|President Garrison]] after he is given a \"splatty tomato\" for his approval ratings. He shows up at the [[Park County Community Center]] to explain to the townsfolk what to do if they see the president. Later on, he is shown to have set up a \"Fox trap\" for the president and vomits when it is discovered that [[PC Principal]] and [[Strong Woman]] were having a sexual relationship.\n\nOfficer Bright makes a second appearance in the [[Season Twenty-Three]] episode \"[[Basic Cable]]\", where he talks to [[Sophie Gray]]'s mother after a showed called \"[[Crab People]]\" comes on her TV while she tries to watch ''[[wikipedia:Stranger Things|Stranger Things]]''.\n\n==Appearance==\nOfficer Bright has brown hair cut into a buzzcut, brown eyebrows, and a brown mustache. He wears a tan cowboy hat, a police uniform consisting of a gray shirt with a tie, a black belt, gray pants with yellow lines on the sides, and black shoes.
