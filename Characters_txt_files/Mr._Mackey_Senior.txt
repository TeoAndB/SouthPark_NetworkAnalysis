Although Mr. Mackey Senior's name was not acknowledged, he refers to Mackey as \"Junior\", thus giving the idea that the two share a common first name.\n\nHe does not appear \"[[Insheeption]]\", despite the episode being set in Mackey's childhood. It is later revealed in \"[[Royal Pudding]]\" that Mr. Mackey Senior was taken and presumably killed by [[Tooth Decay]] two years prior.\n\n==Appearance==\nMr. Mackey Senior wears blue shoes, gray pants, and a red turtleneck shirt. He also wears a teal, button-up sweater and small-framed eyeglasses. Mackey is mostly bald, but has gray hair on the sides of his head. He has a large head, alike Mr. Mackey, and has large ears.\n\n==Prominence==\n*\"[[Child Abduction is Not Funny]]\" - Seen supporting Mr. Mackey during the child abduction crisis.\n*\"[[Royal Pudding]]\" - Mentioned to have been taken and killed by Tooth Decay.\n\n==References==\n{{Reflist}}\n{{South Park Families}}\n{{Minor Characters from Season Six}}\n[[zh:老麦奇先生]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Parents]]\n[[Category:Adult characters]]\n[[Category:Male characters]]\n[[Category:Deceased characters]]\n[[Category:Characters in a relationship]]\n[[Category:Characters voiced by Matt Stone]]\n[[Category:Elderly people]]\n[[Category:Unnamed characters]]"
