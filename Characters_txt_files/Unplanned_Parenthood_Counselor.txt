When the counselor asks when the child was conceived, Liane says that it was eight years ago. The counselor responds with this by saying that it is impossible to abort an eight-year-old child.\n\n==Appearance==\nThe Unplanned Parenthood Counselor is extremely obese. She wears a large blue dress with a circular pattern on it and black shoes.
