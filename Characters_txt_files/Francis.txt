He was formerly voiced by [[Matt Stone]], and then by [[Trey Parker]]. He first appeared in the [[Season One]] episode, \"[[Tom's Rhinoplasty]]\", and has been a common background character and classroom regular for several seasons.\n\n==Background==\nHis first appearance was in the background of the cafeteria in \"[[Tom's Rhinoplasty]]\". He was later shown to be Jewish while attending Jew Scouts in \"[[Jewbilee]]\".\n\nIn \"[[Timmy 2000]]\", he was on the cover of the 'alternative ADD treatment' booklet. He was ranked #14 on the rigged list in \"[[The List]]\", making this his official naming.\n\nHe also plays on the school's basketball team, as shown in \"[[Elementary School Musical]]\".\n\nFrancis' parents were shown in \"[[The Wacky Molestation Adventure]]\".\n\n===Criminal Record===\n* '''Indecent exposure''': During the Weiners Out Movement of [[Season Twenty]], he joins [[Butters Stotch|Butters]]' cause and frequently exposes his genitals.\n* '''False police report:''' In \"[[The Wacky Molestation Adventure|The Wacky Molestration Adventure]]\", he files a false police report that adults were 'molestering' kids.\n\n==Appearance==\nHe has short, medium brown hair, a lock of which hangs down his forehead, wears a light brown sweater with the \"Snacky S'mores\" beaver mascot roasting marshmallows on it, and darkish blue jeans.\n\nIn his appearance in ''[[South Park: Bigger, Longer & Uncut]]'', he wore the same outfit as [[Clyde Donovan]], a burgundy coat with blue trim, matching mittens, and a pair of brown trousers.\n\n<gallery widths=\"120\" caption=\"Images of Francis\">\nFrancis8.png|Francis wearing [[Clyde Donovan]]'s clothes in ''[[South Park: Bigger, Longer &
