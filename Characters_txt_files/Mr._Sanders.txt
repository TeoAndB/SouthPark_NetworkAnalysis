"{{South Park character\n|name = Mr. Sanders\n|image = horace-dad.png\n|gender = Male\n|hair = Black\n|voice = [[Matt Stone]]<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0705924/|title=\"South Park\" Fat Camp (TV Episode 2000)|publisher=IMDb.com}}</ref>\n|relation1 = Son\n|relativename1 = [[Horace Sanders]]\n|relation2 = Wife\n|relativename2 = [[Mrs. Sanders]]\n|appearance = \"[[Fat Camp]]\"\n}}\n'''Mr. Sanders''' is a character who appears in the [[Season Four]] episode \"[[Fat Camp]]\".\n\n==Background==\nMr. Sanders and his [[Mrs. Sanders|wife]] go to [[Hopeful Hills|fat camp]] to see if [[Horace Sanders|their son]] has lost weight. After discovering that he has not lost any weight, he decides to take him out of camp. He gets into an argument with the [[Rick and Susan Tyler|camp counselors]], saying that their camp is a fraud and he will call all the campers' parents to come for their children. When the rest of the parents come, [[Chad]] reveals that [[Eric Cartman]] was selling candy to the campers after lights out. Mr. Sanders, along with the other parents, decide to leave their child at camp, giving them another chance to lose weight.\n\n==Appearance==\nMr. Sanders wears an ill-fitting light blue jacket, dark green pants and black shoes. He has a thick mustache and curly black hair that is similar to his son's.
