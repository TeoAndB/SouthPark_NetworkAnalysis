"{{South Park character\n|name = Chet\n|image = Redneck-with-red-shirt.png\n|gender = Male\n|hair = Brown\n|appearance = \"[[Goobacks]]\"\n}}\n'''Chet''' is a male character who made his first appearance in the [[Season Eight]] episode, \"[[Goobacks]]\". He still appears in recent episodes, but usually as a background character. He is rarely seen without the presence of other rednecks.\n\n==Appearance==\nChet has long light brown hair with an almost straight top, thick brown eyebrows, three forehead wrinkles and also has a light brown mustache. He wears a red jacket with the sleeves going up over his elbows and with two side pockets with gold buttons and a black collar. He also wears a black belt with a golden buckle, shiny blue pants and black shoes. In some episodes he can be seen without a mustache.\n\n==Prominence==\n*\"[[Goobacks]]\" - Debut appearance.\n*\"[[Two Days Before the Day After Tomorrow]]\" - (Speaking role) Seen with other rednecks in the News.\n*\"[[Die Hippie, Die]]\"\n*\"[[Follow That Egg!]] - He tells Mr. Garrison to appeal to the governor.\n*\"[[Bloody Mary]]\" - Seen in the crowd at the end of the episode.\n*\"[[Margaritaville]]\" -  Seen in the News with the other rednecks.\n*\"[[W.T.F.]]\"\n*\"[[Cock Magic]]\"\n*\"[[White People Renovating Houses]]\" - Seen at the beginning of the episode, discussing with other rednecks about Alexa \"taking\" their jobs.\n[[fr:Chet]]\n[[zh:切特]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Male characters]]\n[[Category:Adult characters]]\n[[Category:Townsfolk]]\n[[Category:Rednecks]]\n[[Category:Background characters]]\n[[Category:Minor characters]]"
