"{{South Park character\n|name = Handicapped Boy with Blue Jumpsuit\n|image = Groups-special-education-chad-handler.png\n|gender = Male\n|hair = Blond\n|age = 9-10\n|job = Student\n|grade = 4th Grade\n|appearance = \"[[Up the Down Steroid]]\"\n}}\nThe '''Handicapped Boy with Blue Jumpsuit''' is a unnamed handicapped boy who appears in several episodes with other handicapped children. He made his first appearance on \"[[Up the Down Steroid]]\" where he is seen training for the special Olympics. He also appears in \"[[Crippled Summer]]\" where he is seen on Lake Tardicaca as a member of the [[Red Team]]. So far he has played no major role.\n\n==Appearance==\nHe has a tuft of messy blond hair and is usually seen with a smiling face. He is usually seen with his eyes closed. He wears a cyan blue shirt under a dark blue jumpsuit with two front pockets.\n\nIn \"[[Crippled Summer]]\" he wore the standard red uniform of Tardicaca Camp, and in \"[[Moss Piglets]]\" he wore a lab coat with a badge on the right side.\n\n==Prominence==\n*\"[[Up the Down Steroid]]\"\n*\"[[The Death of Eric Cartman]]\"\n*\"[[Crippled Summer]]\"\n*\"[[Handicar]]\"\n*\"[[Moss Piglets]]\"\n\n{{South Park Children}}\n[[zh:吊带裤残疾男孩]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Unnamed characters]]\n[[Category:Male characters]]\n[[Category:Minor characters]]\n[[Category:Background characters]]\n[[Category:4th graders]]\n[[Category:Male 4th graders]]\n[[Category:School children]]\n[[Category:Townsfolk]]\n[[Category:Tardicaca campers]]\n[[Category:Characters with deviations]]"
