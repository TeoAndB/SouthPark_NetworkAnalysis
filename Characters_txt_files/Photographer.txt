"{{South Park character\n|name = Photographer\n|image = Adults-workforce-photodojo-photographer.png\n|gender = Male\n|hair = Light Brown\n|voice = [[Trey Parker]]<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0705976/|title=\"South Park\" Tom's Rhinoplasty (TV Episode 1997)|publisher=IMDb.com}}</ref>\n|appearance = \"[[Damien]]\"\n}}\nThe '''Photographer''' is a minor character who debuted in the [[Season One]] episode \"[[Damien]]\". He later appears in \"[[Tom's Rhinoplasty]]\" and \"[[Cartman Joins NAMBLA]]\".\n\n==Background==\nHe is the main photographer of [[Photo Dojo]]; he takes photos of [[Herbert Garrison|Mr. Garrison]] in \"[[Tom's Rhinoplasty]]\" and in \"[[Cartman Joins NAMBLA]]\", he takes pictures of [[Eric Cartman]], who wants to be the poster boy for [[NAMBLA]]. He was replaced by a [[Cab Driver (\"Erection Day\")|generic background character]] in ''[[South Park: The Fractured But Whole]]''.\n\n==Appearance==\nThe photographer has long dirty blond hair, and eyebrows that are drawn similarly to Cartman's eyebrows. He wears a brown vest over a long-sleeved cyan shirt that reveals his upper chest hair, gray pants and black shoes. He wears glasses with orange lenses.\n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery widths=\"160\" caption=\"Images of the photographer\" spacing=\"small\">\nPhotographer3.png|The photographer taking pictures of [[Eric Cartman|Cartman]]\nPhotographer2.png\nPhotographer1.png|The Photographer taking pictures of [[Herbert Garrison|Mr. Garrison]]\nPhotographer4.png|Photographer's icon in ''[[South Park: The Stick of Truth]]'' (scrapped)\nPhotographer-cut-stick-of-truth.png|The photographer in a beta version of ''[[South Park: The Stick of Truth]]''.\n</gallery>\n\n==Prominence==\n*\"[[Damien]]\" - He is in the crowd when [[Satan]] first appears.\n*\"[[Tom's Rhinoplasty]]\" - He takes pictures of [[Herbert Garrison|Mr. Garrison]].\n*\"[[Cartman Joins NAMBLA]]\" - He takes pictures of [[Eric Cartman]].\n\n==Trivia==\n*His appearance is similar to the appearance of the [[cameraman]] with blond hair that appeared in the old episodes.\n*The Photographer was originally planned to appear in ''[[South Park: The Stick of Truth]]'', but was replaced by the [[Pedophile Cameraman]].\n\n==References==\n{{Reflist}}\n{{Minor Characters from Season One}}\n[[zh:摄影师]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Minor characters]]\n[[Category:Adult characters]]\n[[Category:Male characters]]\n[[Category:Unnamed characters]]\n[[Category:Townsfolk]]\n[[Category:Background characters]]"
