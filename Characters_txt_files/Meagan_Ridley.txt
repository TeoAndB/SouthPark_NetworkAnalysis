She is voiced by [[April Stewart]] and first appears in the [[Season Twelve]] episode, \"[[Super Fun Time]]\".\n\n==Background==\nHer first speaking role was in \"[[T.M.I.]]\", when [[Randy Marsh]] teaches the class about penis sizes and makes her read his measurements out loud.\n\nShe next spoke in \"[[Ass Burgers]]\", when she was surprised with how good [[Eric Cartman|Cartman's]] hamburgers were in the school hallway.\n\nShe was originally called Riley in \"[[Ass Burgers]]\", but was renamed Meagan Ridley in \"[[Member Berries]]\".\n\n==Appearance==\nShe has wavy, gray hair. She wears a teal jacket with dark blue trimming, dark blue pants, two light pink bows in her hair, and black shoes.\n\n== Gallery ==\n<gallery widths=\"160\" caption=\"Images of Riley\">\nMemberBerries00003.png|Meagan with [[Heidi Turner]], [[Wendy Testaburger]], and [[Nichole Daniels]] in \"[[Member Berries]]\".\n</gallery>\n\n==Personality==\nIn \"[[T.M.I.]]\", she seemed to take issue with talking about the sexually explicit T.M.I. formula, having to be told by [[Randy Marsh|Randy]] twice, although considering her age and gender, this is normal.\n\n==Prominence==\n*\"[[Super Fun Time]]\" - Seen holding hands with [[Jenny Simons]];
