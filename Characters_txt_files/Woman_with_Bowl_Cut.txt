"{{South Park character\n|name = Woman with Bowl Cut\n|image = Unamed-townsfolk-bowl-cut-woman.png\n|gender = Female\n|hair = Brown (though it varies between episodes)\n|voice = [[April Stewart]]\n|appearance = \"[[The China Probrem]]\"\n|speaking = \"[[Medicinal Fried Chicken]]\"\n}}\nThe '''Woman with Bowl Cut''' is an unnamed woman who appears as a background character in many episodes starting with [[Season Twelve]]. Her most notable appearance is in the [[Season Sixteen]] episode \"[[Reverse Cowgirl]]\", where she gets angry at the people attending [[Betsy Donovan]]'s funeral for being disrespectful, though her first speaking role was in the [[Season Fourteen]] episode \"[[Medicinal Fried Chicken]]\".\n\n==Appearance==\nAs described, she has medium length hair made into a bowl haircut on the front. While she is most commonly seen with brown hair, it varies in some episodes, such as the end of \"[[Medicinal Fried Chicken]]\", where she has blonde hair. She also usually wears a light green scarf, a white shirt, a navy green skirt, and black boots, though in some of her appearances she doesn't wear her scarf and she wears a black shirt.\n<gallery widths=\"160\" spacing=\"small\" caption=\"Images of Woman with Bowl Cut\">\nMedicinalFriedChicken029.png|The woman with [[Douglas]] as [[Randy Marsh|Randy]] pushes his balls in a wheelbarrow in \"[[Medicinal Fried Chicken]]\".\nMedicinalFriedChicken088.png|The woman with blonde hair in \"[[Medicinal Fried Chicken]]\".\nRC-Bowl-Cut-Woman.png|The woman getting angry at a funeral in \"[[Reverse Cowgirl]]\".\n</gallery>\n\n==Prominence==\n*\"[[The China Probrem]]\" -
