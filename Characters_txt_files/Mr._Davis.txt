"{{South Park character\n|name = Mr. Davis\n|image = Townsfolk-mr-davis.png\n|gender = Male\n|race = Caucasian\n|hair = Orange\n|appearance = \"[[The Entity]]\"\n}}\n'''Mr. Davis''' is a [[South Park (Location)|South Park]] [[City People|citizen]] who first appears in [[Season Six]] episode, \"[[The New Terrance and Phillip Movie Trailer]]\". \n\n==Background==\nMr. Davis first appeared in the episode \"[[The New Terrance and Phillip Movie Trailer]]\". When [[Russell Crowe]] is speaking, Mr. Davis walks in front of him while eating a sandwich, which makes Russell Crowe angry and starts beating him. He tries to talk to Russell, but he is still beaten.\n\nHe later appeared in \"[[Free Hat]]\", where he is seen with other people picking up a song for the governor, hoping that he will release [[Hat McCullough]].\n\nHe also appeared in \"[[Pre-School]]\" where he asks [[Miss Claridge]] if she wanted help crossing the street, he is answered negatively.\n\nHis latest speaking appearance was in \"[[You're Not Yelping]]\", where he appears as one of several [[Yelp]] reviewers who receives a badge from [[Mayor McDaniels]].\n\n==Appearance==\nMr. Davis wears a gray suit under a white shirt with a pink tie, gray pants, and black shoes.
