He is a retired basketball player who recovered from HIV. \n\n==Background==\n[[Eric Cartman]] and [[Kyle Broflovski]] go to see Johnson after Cartman infects Kyle with [[AIDS]] for teasing him about having the disease. He, unlike other victims of the virus, had not yet had the virus develop into full-blown AIDS. It turns out that this is because he sleeps with all of his money in his bedroom, which is discovered to be the [[cure for AIDS]].\n\n==Appearance==\nJohnson is bald and wears a purple jacket over white t-shirt and purple track pants. His jacket has his number, 32, with a star over it. He owns a mansion in front of which there is a large fountain with two sculptures of a lion on it.
