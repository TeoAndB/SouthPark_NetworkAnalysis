"{{South Park character\n|name = Kobe Bryant\n|image = Kobe-bryant.png\n|gender = Male\n|race = African-American\n|hair = Black\n|age = 41 (deceased)\n|dob = August 23, 1978\n|died = January 26, 2020\n|job = Basketball Player\n|religion = Roman Catholic\n|voice = [[Matt Stone]]<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0705931/|title=\"Here Comes the Neighborhood\" on IMDB}}</ref>\n|wikipedia= [[Wikipedia:Kobe Bryant|Kobe Bryant]]\n|appearance = \"[[Here Comes the Neighborhood]]\"\n|final = \"[[Sexual Healing]]\"\n}}\n'''Kobe Bean Bryant''' was an American professional basketball player, who spent his entire career with the Los Angeles Lakers in the NBA. He appeared in \"[[Here Comes the Neighborhood]]\", as one of the rich people who moved to [[South Park (Location)|South Park]]. He appeared again in \"[[Sexual Healing]]\", in which he was treated at the [[Karne Institute for Sex Addiction]] with [[Kyle Broflovski|Kyle]] and [[Butters Stotch|Butters]], and learned that his sex addiction was caused by a \"[[Wizard Alien]]\".\n\nHis real-life counterpart passed away along with his daughter Gianna \"Gigi\" Bryant in a helicopter crash in Los Angeles on January 26, 2020.\n\n==Appearance==\nKobe wears a purple \"warm-up\" suit with the number 8 on the left side. He also wears a spherical earring on his right ear.\n\nHis look is updated in \"[[Sexual Healing]]\", with him now having a shorter hairstyle and his face is clean shaven. He wears a maroon warm-up jacket with two white stripes on the sleeves and black track pants with a white stripe on the side.
