When the New Kid defeats the girls, Rebecca was called upon to fight the New Kid, delivering a body slam. However, Rebecca immediately discovered that she received a thousand dislikes on an Instagram photo of her in ballet class (and therefore losing all her followers), leaving the alley in a hurry and in panic.\n\nShe can be fought again in the [[Raisins (Location)|Raisins restaurant]] as part of \"[[A Perky Predicament]]\" mission.\n\n==Appearance==\nRebecca is the only overweight girl among all other Raisins Girl. She wears the Raisins uniform with an undersized shirt which exposes her belly.
