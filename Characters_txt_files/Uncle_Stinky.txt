Like most of the extended Cartman family, he hasn't made a recent appearance.\n\n==Appearance==\nHe wears a dark green sweater with a matching pair of grey trousers and black shoes. He also has depicted facial hair consisting of a mustache that runs down the side of his nose and a small centered goatee.
