"{{South Park character\n|name = Mr. McDonald\n|image = Fosses-father.png\n|gender = Male\n|hair = Black\n|relation1 = Son\n|relativename1 = [[Fosse McDonald]]\n|appearance = \"[[Mr. Hankey, the Christmas Poo]]\"\n|speaking = \"[[Damien]]\"\n}}\n'''Mr. McDonald''' is [[Fosse McDonald]]'s unnamed father who first appears in the [[Season One]] episode, \"[[Mr. Hankey, the Christmas Poo]]\".\n\n==Background==\nHe first appeared in \"[[Mr. Hankey, the Christmas Poo]]\" in the background at the [[South Park Elementary]] Christmas play. He later appeared in \"[[Damien]]\" at the bar with [[Mr. Allen]], calling [[Jesus Christ]] \"gay\" in the style of their sons' catchphrase. He appears to share the exact same characteristics as his son, with the exception of the purple shirt; he wears a turquoise shirt which resembles that of [[Bill Allen]]'s.\n\nHe is shown to be friends with [[Mr. Allen]] and was confirmed to be Fosse's father in the creator commentary for \"[[Damien]]\".\n\n==Appearance==\nHis appearance is pretty much the same as his son's, the only differences being height and the shirt; he wears a turquoise shirt rather than a purple one.\n\n==Personality==\nMuch like his son, he likes to call things \"gay\" whilst laughing in a deep voice.\n\n==Prominence==\n*\"[[Mr. Hankey, the Christmas Poo]]\" - Seen watching the [[South Park Elementary]] Christmas play.\n*\"[[Damien]]\" - Seen at the bar with Mr. Allen, laughing whilst using their sons' \"That's Gay!\" catch phrase.\n\n{{Minor Characters from Season One}}\n[[zh:麦克唐纳先生]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Male characters]]\n[[Category:Adult characters]]\n[[Category:Parents]]\n[[Category:Townsfolk]]\n[[Category:Characters with unknown voice actor]]\n[[Category:Unnamed characters]]\n[[Category:Minor characters]]"
