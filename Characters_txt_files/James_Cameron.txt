"{{South Park character\n|name = James Cameron\n|image = James cameron 2.3..png\n|gender = Male\n|race = Caucasian\n|hair = Gray\n|age = {{age|1954|8|16}}\n|dob = August 16, 1954\n|job = Director, Explorer\n|religion = Atheist\n|wikipedia = [[Wikipedia:James Cameron|James Cameron]]\n|appearance = \"[[Raising the Bar]]\"\n}}\n'''James Cameron''' is a Hollywood director and deep-sea explorer who appears in the [[Season Sixteen]] episode, \"[[Raising the Bar]]\". The plot was a reference to James's record-setting solo dive into the Mariana trench on March 26, 2012.\n\n==Background==\nWith the help of his crew of the [[USS James Cameron]], James travels to the lowest depths of the ocean to raise \"[[The Bar|the bar]]\" on society's acceptance of \"[[Here Comes Honey Boo Boo]]\" as well as obesity. The \"[[Ballad of James Cameron]]\" about his expedition plays twice in the episode.\n\n==Appearance==\nJames Cameron has gray hair and a gray beard. He wears a black shirt, a black and blue deep-sea diving suit, black gloves, black diving pants, and black shoes.\n\nUnlike other Canadian celebrities on the show, he has the traditional appearance of a South Park character rather than the beady-eyed look of many Canadian characters.\n\n{{Minor Characters from Season Sixteen}}\n{{MovieCelebrities}}\n[[pl:James Cameron]]\n[[zh:詹姆斯·卡梅隆]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Celebrities]]\n[[Category:Male characters]]\n[[Category:Adult characters]]\n[[Category:One-off characters]]\n[[Category:Canadians]]\n[[Category:Characters with unknown voice actor]]\n[[Category:Atheists]]\n[[Category:Movie celebrities]]"
