"{{South Park character\n|name = Mr. and Mrs. Triscotti\n|image = Mr-and-mrs-triscotti.png\n|gender = '''Mr. Triscotti:''' Male<br>'''Mrs. Triscotti:''' Female\n|hair = '''Mr. Triscotti:''' Brown<br>'''Mrs. Triscotti:''' Light Brown\n|religion = Roman Catholic\n|voice = '''Mr. Triscotti:''' [[Trey Parker]]<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0890648/|title=\"South Park\" Go God Go (TV episode 2006)|publisher=IMDb.com}}</ref>\n|relation1 = Daughter\n|relativename1 = [[Linda Triscotti]]\n|appearance = \"[[Go God Go]]\"\n}}\n'''Mr. and Mrs. Triscotti'''<ref>Minutes 5:40-5:45 in \"[[Go God Go]]\"</ref> appear in [[Season Ten]] episode \"[[Go God Go]]\". They are [[Linda Triscotti]]'s parents. They are strict Catholics who reprimanded [[South Park Elementary]] for teaching evolution to [[Girl with Green Coat|their daughter]].\n\n==Appearance==\n===Mr. Triscotti===\nMr. Triscotti has shiny, dark brown hair, facial wrinkles, and an abnormally large nose. He wears a brown coat, white shirt, gray tie, brown belt and black pants. He also wears black-framed, circular glasses. In the season 17 intro, he can be seen near the Catholic Church.\n\n===Mrs. Triscotti===\nMrs. Triscotti has long, light brown hair. She wears a light gray, button-up coat, white shirt, dark blue, ankle-length skirt, an orange scarf, and dark purple stockings.\n\n==Personality==\n===Mr. Triscotti===\nHe is a very strict Catholic. He was shown to react in anger when anything that challenged his faith was taught to his daughter.\n\n===Mrs. Triscotti===\nLittle is know about her personality, due to the fact that she had no speaking role, however, we can assume she feels similarly to Mr. Triscotti about evolution as she let him reprimand the school with no objection or argument.\n\n==Family==\n===[[Linda Triscotti]]===\nMr. and Mrs. Triscotti are [[Linda Triscotti]]'s parents. They seem to push their religion on her, with Mr. Triscotti telling her ''\"No you don't!
