Hannah's largest role was in \"[[Put It Down]]\", when she performed at the school talent show after [[Tweek Tweak|Tweek]]. She sang \"[[The Wheels on the Bus]]\" while crying because Tweek's performance before her had scared her.\n\n==Appearance==\nHannah is an adorable first grader with wavy black hair. She wears a yellow shirt with green sleeves, brown pants, and has a flower in her hair.\n\n==Personality==\nHannah is sensitive to heavy news.
