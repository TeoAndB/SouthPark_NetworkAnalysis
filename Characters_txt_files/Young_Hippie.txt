"{{South Park character\n|name = Young hippie\n|image = Hippie (Ike's Wee Wee).png\n|gender = Female\n|race = Caucasian\n|hair = Blond\n|voice = [[Mary Kay Bergman]]<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0705934/|title=\"South Park\" Ike's Wee Wee (TV Episode 1998)|publisher=IMDb.com}}</ref>\n|appearance = \"[[Ike's Wee Wee]]\"\n}}\nThe '''Young Hippie''' is a young woman whom [[Mr. Mackey]] encountered and married while on drugs in the [[Season Two]] episode, \"[[Ike's Wee Wee]]\". \n\n==Background==\nMr. Mackey is fired from [[South Park Elementary]] and then evicted from [[Mackey Residence|his home]] amid false allegations of drug abuse. He only after that turns to drugs and becomes a hippie. He meets this young hippie and they decide to fingerpaint at her house, and basking in the afterglow, they decide to get married and have a honeymoon in [[India]]. While on honeymoon, Mr. Mackey is captured by The A-Team and brought to the [[Betty Ford Clinic]] for rehab. It is unknown whether they are still married bu she has not appeared since.\n\n==Appearance==\nThe young hippie has long blond hair, long eyelashes, red lipstick, and visible ears. She wears a spaghetti strapped crop top that is dyed red and green, and a pair of blue bell-bottomed jeans with a belt and a heart patch.\n\n==References==\n{{Reflist}}\n\n{{Minor Characters from Season Two}}\n[[zh:嬉皮少女]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Adult characters]]\n[[Category:Female characters]]\n[[Category:Unnamed characters]]\n[[Category:One-off characters]]\n[[Category:Characters voiced by Mary Kay Bergman]]"
