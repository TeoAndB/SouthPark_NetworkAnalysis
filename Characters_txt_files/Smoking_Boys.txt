"{{South Park character\n|name = Smoking Boys\n|image = 5th-8th-graders-smoking-boys-drug-dealers-cc.png\n|gender = Male\n|hair = Black<br>Brown\n|age = 11-12\n|grade = 5th Grade\n|appearance = \"[[The Tooth Fairy Tats 2000]]\"\n}}\nThe '''Smoking Boys''' are three 5th graders who appear in [[Jerome \"Chef\" McElroy|Chef]]'s flashback in \"[[The Tooth Fairy Tats 2000]]\".\n\n==Background==\nChef tells [[The Boys|the boys]] a story from his childhood to convince them that drugs are not good; while he and his girl walk the streets, the smoking boys appear and offer drugs to them, Chef then refuses and sings a song about when he grows up he will make love to [[Amanda]] (the girl who was with him), the boys then interrupt Chef after that part.\n\n==Appearance==\nThe smoking boys are three tall boys with an unpleasant appearance and all wear black shoes.\n\nThe first boy has black hair, a wide nose and wears a simple blue shirt and light blue pants. The second boy has dark brown hair and wears a lime green shirt with a gray \"A\" and dark green pants. The third and last boy has thick black hair and wears a yellow shirt and blue pants, he also has darker skin than the other two boys.\n[[zh:吸烟男孩]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Unnamed characters]]\n[[Category:Male characters]]\n[[Category:5th graders]]\n[[Category:One-off characters]]"
