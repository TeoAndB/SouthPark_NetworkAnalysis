"{{South Park character\n|name = Mrs. Stoley\n|image = Mrs-stoley.png\n|aliases = Mrs. Brook\n|gender = Female\n|hair = Brown\n|relation1 = Husband\n|relativename1 = [[Mr. Stoley]]\n|relation2 = Son\n|relativename2 = [[Kevin Stoley]]\n|appearance = \"[[My Future Self n' Me]]\"\n}}\n'''Mrs. Stoley''', formerly '''Mrs. Brook''', is the mother of [[Kevin Stoley]], who first appears in [[Season Six]]'s episode, \"[[My Future Self n' Me]]\". She and her [[Mr. Stoley|husband]] hire a \"Future self\", similar to Kevin. After that episode, it remained a mystery whether she and her husband were really Kevin's parents until [[Season Nineteen]]'s episode, \"[[You're Not Yelping]]\", where she is seen eating at [[Whistlin' Willy's]] with her husband and Kevin.\n\n==Appearance==\nMrs. Stoley has short brown hair and brown eyebrows. She wears a light purple sweater, a black skirt and black shoes.
