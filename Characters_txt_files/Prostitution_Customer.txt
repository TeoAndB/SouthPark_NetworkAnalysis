"{{South Park character\n|name = Prostitution Customer\n|image = Adults-prostitution-customer-cc.png\n|gender = Male\n|race = Caucasian\n|hair = Brown\n|died = \"[[Coon 2: Hindsight]]\" {{s|(revived)}}\n|voice = [[Matt Stone]]<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.imdb.com/title/tt1526694/|title=\"South Park\" Butters' Bottom Bitch (TV episode 2009)|publisher=IMDb.com}}</ref>\n|appearance = \"[[Butters' Bottom Bitch]]\"\n}}\nThe '''Prostitution customer''' is an unnamed citizen of [[South Park (Location)|South Park]].\n\n==Background==\nHe was first seen in \"[[Butters' Bottom Bitch]]\", where he drives his rusty old model yellow car to the Red Light District opting to have sex with prostitutes, he accepts to have oral sex with [[Harrison Yates]] (dressed as \"Yolanda\") for twenty dollars, who frames and arrest him as the first culprit caught in his anti-prostitution operation; Yates said that he'd bring him for booking in the [[Park County Police Force]].\n\nHe was seen as a cameo amongst the zombie-like shoppers in \"[[A Song of Ass and Fire]]\" who are shocked to learn that [[Black Friday]] was delayed in a week by the advisory of [[George R. R. Martin]].\n\nHe can be seen in \"[[Tweek x Craig]]\", walking in town next to the [[Yaoi]] art market in the pouring rain with an umbrella.\n\nHe is seen as one of the men in [[The Peppermint Hippo]] strip club in ''[[South Park: The Fractured But Whole]]''.\n\nHe is seen again in \"[[Christmas Snow]]\" drinking beer and rooting for [[Mr. Mackey]] for his song in the town's Christmas' festival, and then listening to [[Santa Claus]]'s anti-drink-and-drive speech.\n\n==Appearance==\nHe has combed light brown hair and is slightly overweight. He wears a dark green jacket under a blue shirt.
