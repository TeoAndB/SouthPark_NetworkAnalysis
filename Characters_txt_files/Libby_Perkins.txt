"{{South Park character\n|name = Libby Perkins\n|image = <gallery>\nGroups-special-education-lenora.png|Current\nLenoraTardicaca.png|Tardicaca Camper\n</gallery>\n|aliases = Lenora\n|gender = Female\n|age = 10\n|job = Student\n|voice = [[April Stewart]]<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.imdb.com/title/tt1644897/\n|title=\"South Park\" Crippled Summer (TV episode 2010)|publisher=IMDb.com}}</ref>\n|appearance = \"[[Up the Down Steroid]]\"\n|speaking = \"[[Crippled Summer]]\"\n|last = \"[[Moss Piglets]]\"\n}}\n'''Libby \"Lenora\" Perkins''' is a [[List of Female 4th Graders|female fourth-grader]] at [[South Park Elementary]]. She first appears in the [[Season Eight]] episode, \"[[Up the Down Steroid]]\".\n\n==Background==\nShe is seen training for the Special Olympics in the episode \"[[Up the Down Steroid]]\", but appears prominently in the [[Season Fourteen]] episode, \"[[Crippled Summer]]\". She is in the Blue Team along with [[Jimmy]] and [[Timmy]], she also appears at the end of the episode singing at the talent show behind Jimmy. She was called \"Lenora\" by the [[Steve|Counselor Steve]] in this episode.\n\nLibby appears in ''[[South Park: The Fractured But Whole]]'' DLC [[Bring the Crunch]] and can take a selfie with the player. She was renamed \"Libby Perkins\" in the game and her voice is also different.\n\n==Appearance==\nLibby has blond hair with braids, thick, spiky eyebrows and a slack mouth. She wears a pale pink shirt with a flower design in the middle, blue pants and black shoes. She wore pink gloves in ''[[South Park: The Fractured But Whole]]''.\n\nIn \"[[Crippled Summer]]\" she wears the standard uniform of Lake Tardicaca.\n\nIn \"[[Sponsored Content]]\" Libby wears a green dress with a black belt.\n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery caption=\"Images of Libby Perkins\" spacing=\"small\" columns=\"dynamic\" widths=\"159\">\nLenora2.png|Libby Perkins in the blue Tardicaca Camp uniform.\nLenora.png|[[Towelie]] about to puke on Libby.\nSponsored Content 13.png|Libby with other disabled children on [[PC Delta]] in \"[[Sponsored Content]]\".\nSuperschoolnews.png|Libby in the school newspaper.\n</gallery>\n\n==Trivia==\n*Lenora is the only member of the blue team that appears in ''[[South Park: The Fractured But Whole]].''\n\n==Prominence==\n*\"[[Up the Down Steroid]]\" - Seen training for the Special Olympics.\n*\"[[Crippled Summer]]\" - Appears in [[Lake Tardicaca]] with the other handicapped children.\n*\"[[Black Friday]]\" - Part of [[Stan Marsh|Stan]]'s army to get the PlayStation 4.\n*\"[[Titties and Dragons]]\"\n*\"[[Sponsored Content]]\" - Seen at the [[PC Delta]], along with other disabled children.\n*\"[[Moss Piglets]]\" - Seen as a participant at the science fair.\n\n==References==\n{{Reflist}}\n{{South Park Children}}[[zh:莉比·珀金斯]]\n[[Category:4th graders]]\n[[Category:Female characters]]\n[[Category:Female 4th graders]]\n[[Category:School children]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Other 4th graders]]\n[[Category:Minor characters]]\n[[Category:Characters with deviations]]\n[[Category:Background characters]]\n[[Category:Characters with changed names]]\n[[Category:Tardicaca campers]]"
