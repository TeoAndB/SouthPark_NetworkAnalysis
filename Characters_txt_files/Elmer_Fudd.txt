"{{South Park character\n|name = Elmer Fudd\n|image = Elmer Fudd.png\n|gender = Male\n|race = Caucasian\n|age = 9-10\n|job = Student\n|voice = [[Trey Parker]]<ref>Credited as \"Disabled Kid #1\" in {{cite web|url=https://www.imdb.com/title/tt1644897/|title=\"South Park\" Crippled Summer (TV episode 2010)|publisher=IMDb.com}}</ref>\n|wikipedia = [[Wikipedia:Elmer Fudd|Elmer Fudd]]\n|appearance = \"[[Crippled Summer]]\"\n}}\n'''Elmer Fudd''' is a character who appears in the [[Season Fourteen]] episode, \"[[Crippled Summer]]\".\n\n==Background==\nHe is a student at [[Lake Tardicaca|Tardicaca Camp]], which is a camp for children with disabilities. He was on the [[Blue Team]] along with [[Jimmy]], [[Timmy]], [[Lenora]], and [[Droopy Dawg]]. His disability makes him look and sound like Elmer Fudd from Merrie Melodies.\n\n==Appearance==\nHe wears a blue cap with a gray border, a blue hood with the Camp Tardicaca logo, and light blue pants. He has a large nose and has a large and rather unusual head than other children.
