He has been repeatedly referenced in ''[[South Park]]'', usually preceded by the catchphrase \"What would Brian Boitano do?\" He is a fictionalized version of the celebrity, one of few to be portrayed in a positive light.\n\n==Appearance==\nBrian has small eyes, as well as a generally small head.
