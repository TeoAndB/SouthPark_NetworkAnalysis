"{{South Park character\n|name = Mrs. Zewiski\n|image = Adults-parents-mrs-zewiski.png\n|gender = Female\n|hair = Brown\n|relation1 = Husband\n|relativename1 = [[Mr. Zewiski]]\n|relation2 = Son\n|relativename2 = [[Larry Zewiski]]\n|appearance = \"[[Bike Parade]]\"\n}}\n'''Mrs. Zewiski''' is the wife of [[Mr. Zewiski]] and the mother of [[Larry Zewiski]]. She appears in the [[Season Twenty-Two]] episode \"[[Bike Parade]]\", though her son and husband were both introduced in \"[[Unfulfilled]]\".\n\n==Background==\nAfter Larry Zewiski sees [[Butters Stotch]]'s bicycle he made for the bike parade, he is seen yelling at his father to go back to work at the [[Amazon Fulfillment Center]]. Mrs. Zewiski is seen when he leaves to go to the center, asking him where he's going. This is her only spoken line in the show.\n\n==Appearance==\nMrs. Zewiski has short combed brown hair and brown eyebrows.
