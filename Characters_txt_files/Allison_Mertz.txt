"{{South_Park_character\n|image = allison-mertz.png\n|name = Allison Mertz\n|gender = Female\n|hair = Black with Purple Highlights\n|age = 5-6\n|job  = Student\n|grade = Kindergarten\n|appearance = \"[[The Ungroundable]]\"\n}}\n'''Allison Mertz''' is a female kindergartner who first appeared in the [[Season Twelve]] episode, \"[[The Ungroundable]]\". She is in the [[South Park Vampire Society]].\n\n==Background==\nIn \"[[The Ungroundable]]\", the is seen with [[Mike Makowski]] and another [[South Park Vampire Society|vampire kid]] in [[Principal Victoria]]'s office. Makowski set the record straight noting that vampires, unlike the goths, are very spiritual beings.\n\nShe was later seen in \"[[Goth Kids 3: Dawn of the Posers]]\" at the noon meeting for the [[South Park Vampire Society]]. She sat in the front row.\n\n==Appearance==\nAllison has black pig tails with purple highlights. She wears black shoes, black pants, and a black shirt with gray concentric circles.
