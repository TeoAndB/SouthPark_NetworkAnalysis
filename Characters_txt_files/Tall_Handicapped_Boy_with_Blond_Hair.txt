"{{South Park character\n|name = Tall Handicapped Boy with Blond Hair\n|image = Special education dan.png\n|gender = Male\n|hair = Blond\n|job = Student\n|appearance = \"[[Up the Down Steroid]]\"\n}}\nThe '''Tall Handicapped Boy with Blond Hair''' is a student at [[South Park Elementary]] who appears in several episodes with other disabled children. He made his first appearance on the [[Season Eight]] episode \"[[Up the Down Steroid]]\".\n\n==Appearance==\nHe is a tall boy with short blond curly hair, blond eyebrows, big ears and is usually seen with a smile in his mouth. He wears an orange short-sleeved shirt, purple pants (pale blue in \"[[Up the Down Steroid]]\") over the belly button and black shoes.\n\nIn \"[[Crippled Summer]]\" he was seen wearing the red Tardicaca uniform and in one scene he was wearing a green summer shirt, purple shorts and blue flip flops and in \"[[Moss Piglets]]\" he was wearing a lab coat over his shirt and safety goggles for the science fair.\n\n==Prominence==\n*\"[[Up the Down Steroid]]\"\n*\"[[Crippled Summer]]\"\n*\"[[A Song of Ass and Fire]]\"\n*\"[[Titties and Dragons]]\"\n*\"[[Moss Piglets]]\"\n\n{{South Park Children}}\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Unnamed characters]]\n[[Category:Male characters]]\n[[Category:Characters with deviations]]\n[[Category:Background characters]]\n[[Category:Townsfolk]]\n[[Category:School children]]\n[[Category:Teens]]\n[[Category:Tardicaca campers]]"
