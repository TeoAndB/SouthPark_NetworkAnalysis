"{{South Park character\n|name = Millard Anderson\n|image = <gallery>\nMillard Anderson.png|Current\nMillardAndersonWithHeadphones.png|Headphones \n</gallery>\n|gender = Male\n|race = Caucasian\n|hair = Black\n|relation1 = Wife\n|relativename1 = [[Mrs. Anderson]]\n|relation2 = Son\n|relativename2 = [[Filmore Anderson]]\n|appearance = \"[[It Hits the Fan]]\"\n|speaking = \"[[Child Abduction is Not Funny]]\"\n}}\n'''Millard Anderson''' is the father of [[Filmore Anderson]], who first appears in the [[Season Five]] episode \"[[It Hits the Fan]]\". His name was revealed in \"[[The Pandemic Special]]\".\n\n==Background==\nIn \"[[The Pandemic Special]]\", [[Mr. Mackey]] holds a Zoom conference with the parents on allowing their children to return to [[South Park Elementary|school]]. Millard Anderson is one of the parents who appears on the call. He appears on the top left corner of Mr. Mackey's computer screen.\n\n==Appearance==\nMillard has short curly black hair and wears a dark gray v-neck shirt with a green neckline, navy blue pants, and black shoes.\n\nWhen him and his wife was seen at the South Park Elementary Cows Baseball game in \"[[Child Abduction is Not Funny]]\", he wore a blue v-neck shirt with a white neckline and the Cows logo on it.\n\nIn \"[[More Crap]]\" when he was seen at the EFSM Award Ceremony, multiple of him were seen, with one who is seen sitting next to [[Citizen with Brown Hair and Blue Suit|Mr. Petuski]] wearing a formal suit with a yellow tie.\n\nIn \"[[Stanley's Cup]]\" he was seen wearing his average v-neck shirt except the colors are swapped to have the gray as white as well as his green neckline being red with the capitalized name ''Detroit'' written on it. He also was one of many holding a red stick with \"GO DETROIT\" printed on it. \n\nIn \"[[Christmas Snow]]\" he was seen wearing a brown coat with white fur.\n\nOn [[Mr. Mackey]]'s parent conference Zoom call in \"[[The Pandemic Special]]\", he wears a pair of large red headphones with a thick wire connected to both sides of them.\n\n<gallery widths=\"160\" spacing=\"small\" caption=\"Images of
