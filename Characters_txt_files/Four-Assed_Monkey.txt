"{{South Park character\n|name = Four-Assed Monkey\n|image = Four-assed-monkey.png\n|race = Primate\n|appearance = \"[[An Elephant Makes Love to a Pig]]\"\n}}\n:''For the card in [[South Park: Phone Destroyer]], see [[Four-Assed Monkey (Phone Destroyer)]].''\nThe '''Four-Assed Monkey''' is a genetically modified monkey and one of [[Dr. Alphonse Mephesto]]'s most prized creations. It first appears in [[Season One]] episode, \"[[An Elephant Makes Love to a Pig]]\".\n\n==Background==\nAs [[The Boys]] embark on a tour in the [[South Park Genetic Engineering Ranch]], Dr. Mephesto showed them a four-assed monkey, claiming it will change the world for the better.\n\nThe monkey was used as [[Terrance Mephesto]]'s science project, now with five asses, who eventually lost the award to [[Fluffy]] when the latter gave birth to a piglet that resembles [[Herbert Garrison]].\n\nIn later appearances, it is again seen with four asses.\n\n==Appearance==\nAs the name suggests, it is a brown monkey that was modified to have four asses, covering the base of its tail.
