"{{South Park character\n|name = Zhongnanhai Secretary\n|image = Zhongnanhai-secretary.png\n|gender = Female\n|race = Asian\n|hair = Black\n|job = Secretary to [[Xi Jinping]]\n|appearance = \"[[Tweek x Craig]]\"\n}}\nThe '''Zhongnanhai Secretary''' is the assistant to the Chinese Communist Party general secretary [[Xi Jinping]] in his office at [[Zhongnanhai]]. She appears in [[Season Nineteen]] episode \"[[Tweek x Craig]]\". \n\n==Background==\nAs [[Randy Marsh]] tries to call Xi Jinping to ask how his people decided who is gay or straight, the secretary enters the Xi Jinping's CCP leader office and informs him of the phone call from Randy.\n\nDuring the montage at the end of the episode, she was seen sharing a smile with Xi Jinping, before the pair started kissing.\n\nShe was seen again in \"[[Turd Burglars]]\" during a press conference with [[Tom Brady]].\n\n==Appearance==\nThe secretary has the stereotypical characteristics of Asians seen in the series such as small eyes. She wears a navy blue working dress over a pink blouse and a pair of stockings, along with a pair of brown-framed glasses and a white beaded necklace.\n\n==Trivia==\n*The secretary speaks Korean instead of Mandarin Chinese.\n\n[[zh:中南海秘书]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Female characters]]\n[[Category:Adult characters]]\n[[Category:Unnamed characters]]\n[[Category:Asian characters]]"
