Her first appearance was in the [[Season Two]] episode, \"[[Merry Christmas Charlie Manson!]]\". She appears again in the [[Season Five]] episode, \"[[Cartmanland]]\".\n\n==Appearance==\nShe wears a light green dress with a very bright collar and black shoes. She has curly red hair and is often seen carrying her son Elvin on her lap.
