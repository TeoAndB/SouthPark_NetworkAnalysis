She first appeared in the [[Season Eight]] episode, \"[[Up the Down Steroid]]\".\n\n==Background==\nFrancis first appeared in \"[[Up the Down Steroid]]\" where she is seen giving [[Jimmy Valmer|Jimmy]] good luck before the Olympics. She made a most notable appearance in \"[[Crippled Summer]]\" where she is seen on [[Lake Tardicaca]] with the other disabled children. Her first scene has her practicing with a bow and board to start a fire while saying hi to Jimmy and [[Timmy Burch|Timmy]]. She is on the red team with [[Nathan]] and [[Mimsy]].\n\nShe later appeared on \"[[Funnybot]]\" as Jimmy's stage assistant, where she is seen bringing the trophies.\n\nHer last major appearance was in \"[[Moss Piglets]]\" where she participates in the special education science fair. She alerts Nathan and Mimsy that Jimmy and Timmy's water bears have evolved.\n\n==Personality==\nShe seems to be kind, as she gave [[Jimmy Valmer|Jimmy]] good luck for the special Olympics in \"[[Up the Down Steroid]]\" and greeted Jimmy and [[Timmy]] in \"[[Crippled Summer]]\".\n\nDespite being female, her name uses the masculine spelling.\n\n==Appearance==\nFrancis has short, red hair with a bowl cut, and an askew face. Only one of her eyes can stay wide with the other one noticeably squinted. Her mouth is usually opened. She has worn numerous different outfits in different episodes. Her current outfit is a pink Turtleneck sweater, green shorts and black shoes. She has an adenoidal, somewhat masculine and slurred voice, similar to [[Shelly Marsh]]'s voice.\n\nIn \"[[Crippled Summer]]\", Francis wears a red short sleeve shirt, blue shorts, and black shoes.\n\nIn \"[[Funnybot]]\", she wears a green dress covered in sequins.
