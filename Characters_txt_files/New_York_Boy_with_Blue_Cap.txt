"{{South Park character\n|name = New York Boy with Blue Cap\n|image = <gallery>\nNew-york-kid-blue-hat-current.png|Current\nNewYorkKidTransparent2.png|Former\n</gallery>\n|gender = Male\n|hair = Brown\n|job = Student\n|grade = 3rd Grade\n|appearance = \"[[World Wide Recorder Concert]]\"\n}}\nThe '''New York Boy with Blue Cap''' is a character who appears in the [[Season Three]] episode, \"[[World Wide Recorder Concert]]\", where he is seen as one of the friends of the [[New York Kids]] [[Smitty|leader]]. He is also one of several cut characters from ''[[South Park: The Stick of Truth]]'' and he is seen on a poster at [[Raisins (Location)|Raisins]] in ''[[South Park: The Fractured But Whole]]''.\n\n==Appearance==\nHe has dark Brown hair and wears a light Blue cap with beige edges, a White shirt under a dark Blue Jacket with Red stripes on the sides and Blue pants. In his model found in the game files, he showed a slightly different appearance.\n\nIn later episodes, he wears a White jersey with the number 34 on it.\n\n==Prominence==\n*\"[[World Wide Recorder Concert]]\" - He and the other [[New York Kids]] antagonize the boys during the episode.\n*\"[[The Entity]]\" - He moves to South Park and is briefly mistaken for [[Kyle Broflovski|Kyle]]'s cousin.\n*\"[[Raisins]]\" - Seen on a poster at Raisins and also physically appears at one of the tables.\n*\"[[You Got F'd in the A]]\" - Seen on a poster at [[Raisins (Location)|Raisins]].\n*\"[[AWESOM-O]]\" - Seen with his parents.\n\n==Trivia==\n*Like Smitty, he also appears in the [[Raisins (Location)|Raisins restaurant]] on a poster.\n*In the ''[[South Park: The Stick of Truth]]'' game files, his character model is described as \"newyork_1\".\n[[Category:3rd graders]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Male characters]]\n[[Category:Minor characters]]"
