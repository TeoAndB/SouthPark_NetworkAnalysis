"{{South Park character\n|name = Don Heisman\n|image = Don-heisman.png\n|gender = Male\n|hair = Black\n|job = Talent Agent\n|voice = [[Trey Parker]]<ref>Credited as \"Mr. Daniels\" in {{cite web|url=https://www.imdb.com/title/tt1526694/|title=\"South Park\" Butters' Bottom Bitch (TV Episode 2009)|publisher = IMDb.com}}</ref>\n|appearance = \"[[Wing]]\"\n}}\n'''Don Heisman''' is a talent agent from the ''Creative Arts Agency'' (CAA) based in [[Los Angeles]]. He first appears in [[Season Nine]] episode \"[[Wing]]\".\n\n==Background==\nFollowing [[Token Black|Token's]] music performance at the ''Miss Colorado Pageant'' in [[Denver Convention Center]], Don Heisman approached Token and the boys (who were heading the [[Super Awesome Talent Agency]]) and offered Token to sign up with the CAA with shows in Los Angeles already lined up, to which Token agreed. Both Heisman and Token departed in a limousine, leaving the boys without talents for their own.\n\nHe was seen in \"[[Butters' Bottom Bitch]]\" as the manager of [[Association of Community Organizations for Reform Now|ACORN]], being addressed by [[Butters Stotch|Butters]] as \"Mr. Daniels\".\n\n==Appearance==\nDon Heisman appears with a neat, combed black hair with a mullet.
