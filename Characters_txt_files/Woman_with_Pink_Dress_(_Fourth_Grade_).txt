"{{South Park character\n|name = Woman with Pink Dress\n|image = Woman-in-Timmy-wheelchair.png\n|gender = Female\n|hair = Brown\n|voice = [[Trey Parker]]<ref>Credited as the \"Woman on Wheelchair\" in {{cite web|url=https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0769825/|title=\"South Park\" Fourth Grade (TV Episode 2000)|publisher=IMDb.com}}</ref>\n|appearance = \"[[Fourth Grade]]\"\n}}\nThe '''Woman with Pink Dress''' is a character who appears in the [[Season Four]] episodes, \"[[Fourth Grade]]\" and \"[[Trapper Keeper]]\".\n\n==Background==\nIn the episode, she poses for a photograph with another woman until she accidentally falls into [[Timmy Burch|Timmy]]'s wheelchair that is about to explode. Timmy speeds away while [[Hansen|S.W.A.T team members]] try to disarm the bomb. She jumps off right when they reach the bomb, all for Timmy and his wheelchair to vanish into the prehistoric past.\n\nIn \"[[Trapper Keeper]]\" she is seen with the same woman running away from [[Eric Cartman|Cartman]]'s mutant arm after it grabs a telephone booth near them.\n\n==Appearance ==\nShe has medium length brown hair and wears a pink dress, white socks and black shoes, as well as false eyebrows and red lipstick.\n\n==Prominence==\n{{incomplete|item = list}}\n*\"[[Timmy 2000]]\"\n*\"[[Trapper Keeper]]\"\n\n==References==\n{{Reflist}}\n\n{{Minor Characters from Season Four}}\n[[zh:粉衣女子]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Unnamed characters]]\n[[Category:Adult characters]]\n[[Category:Female characters]]\n[[Category:Characters voiced by Trey Parker]]\n[[Category:Townsfolk]]"
