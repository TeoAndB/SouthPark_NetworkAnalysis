"{{South Park character\n|name = Ms. Stevens\n|image = Miss-stevens.png\n|gender = Female\n|hair = Dark brown\n|job = Choir Teacher\n|voice = [[Jennifer Aniston]]<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0705955/|title=\"Rainforest Schmainforest\" on IMDB}}</ref>\n|appearance = \"[[Rainforest Shmainforest]]\"\n|last = \"[[The City Part of Town]]\"\n}}\n:''For [[Bebe Stevens]]' mother, see [[Mrs. Stevens]].''\n'''Ms. Stevens''' appears in the [[Season Three]] episode \"[[Rainforest Shmainforest]]\" as the choir teacher for the [[Getting Gay With Kids]] organization.\n\n==Background==\nMs. Stevens is very passionate about the rainforest at first. However, at the end of \"[[Rainforest Shmainforest]]\", she realizes that the rainforest is a dangerous place, and agrees with the construction workers that they should knock the whole forest down. Acting as his foil, she grows to hate [[Eric Cartman]]. She has a habit of cocking her head to one side.\n\nShe has also made at least one other non-speaking appearance in \"[[The City Part of Town]]\" as the director of the children's choir welcoming the Whole Foods inspector.\n\n==Appearance==\nMs. Stevens has short dark brown hair and she had several different outfits shown in the episode. Her hair is similar to [[Mrs. Testaburger]]'s.\n\nWhen she is wearing her GGWK uniform she wears a yellow t-shirt with an open mouth on it and 'SING!' written across the top. She also wears a knee length purple skirt and black shoes.\n\n===Casual Attire===\nHer casual clothes are a violet bonnet, with a pink shirt and a purple skirt.\n\n===Hiking Attire===\nWhen trekking in the wilderness she wears a brown jacket, with a rucksack on her back, a hat and khaki shorts.\n\n===Yanagopa Attire===\nWhen offered to the [[Yanagopa Tribe]]'s giant, obese leader, she wears a red and white cheerleader uniform. She also appeared to be barefoot.\n\n==Prominence==\n*\"[[Rainforest Shmainforest]]\" - Lead the organization throughout the rainforest.\n*\"[[I'm a Little Bit Country]]\" - She is seen in the crowd toward the end of the episode.\n*\"[[The City Part of Town]]\" - Seen as the dictator of the children's choir when South Park welcomes the Whole Foods inspector.\n\n==References==\n{{reflist}}\n{{Minor Characters from Season Three}}\n[[es:Srta.
