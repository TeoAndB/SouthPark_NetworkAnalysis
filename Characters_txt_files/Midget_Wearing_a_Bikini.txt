"{{South Park character\n|name = Midget wearing a bikini\n|image = Midget-wearing-bikini.png\n|gender = Male\n|hair = Black\n|job = News Reporter\n|voice = [[Trey Parker]]<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0705948/|title=\"South Park\" Pinkeye (TV episode 1997)|publisher=IMDb.com}}</ref>\n|appearance = \"[[Pinkeye]]\"\n}}\nThe '''Midget Wearing a Bikini''' (sometimes simply '''Midget in a Bikini''') is a news reporter for the [[Channel 4 News]]. He first appears in \"[[Pinkeye]]\", and reappears in the film ''[[South Park: Bigger, Longer & Uncut]]'', as well as in the video games ''[[South Park: The Stick of Truth]]'' and ''[[South Park: The Fractured But Whole]]''.\n\n==Background==\nMidget Wearing a Bikini is similar to the other reporters, who also have unusual and bizarre names, such as [[Creamy Goodness]], [[Quadriplegic Swiss Man on a Pony]], [[Tom Pusslicker]], [[Chris Swollenballs]], and \"[[A 34-Year-Old Asian Man Who Looks Strikingly Similar to Ricardo Montalbán]]\".\n\n==Appearance==\nAs his name suggests, he is a midget wearing a pink bikini. He has short black hair, a black mustache, and short, hairy legs.\n\n==Prominence==\n*\"[[Pinkeye]]\" - Reports the Pinkeye epidemic that is spreading throughout the town.\n===''[[South Park: Bigger, Longer & Uncut]]''===\nReports the effects [[Terrance and Phillip]] have on the [[The 4th Grade|children]] of [[South Park (Location)|South Park]].\n===''[[South Park: The Stick of Truth]]''===\nReports a [[Visitors|UFO]] crash, while reassuring the public that there is nothing to fret.\n===''[[South Park: The Fractured But Whole]]''===\nReports the disturbances made by an unknown public vigilante; the [[Douchebag|new kid]].\n\n==Trivia==\n*He has only ever appeared in a single episode of the main series, despite being the main news reporter in spinoff material for years afterward.\n*He has the second most appearances on any on-site news reporter character after [[Chris Swollenballs]], though he has fewer than [[Bill Keegan]]\n\n==References==\n{{Reflist}}\n{{Minor Characters from Season One}}\n[[es:Enano en Bikini]]\n[[zh:比基尼侏儒]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Characters voiced by Trey Parker]]\n[[Category:Minor characters]]\n[[Category:Male characters]]\n[[Category:Townsfolk]]\n[[Category:Adult characters]]\n[[Category:Characters with deviations]]\n[[Category:News reporters]]\n[[Category:Unnamed characters]]\n[[Category:The Stick of Truth: Characters]]\n[[Category:The Fractured But Whole: Characters]]"
