"{{South Park character\n|name = Dr. Carroll\n|image = Adults-doctors-doctor-carrol.png\n|gender = Male\n|race = Caucasian\n|hair = Brown\n|voice = [[Trey Parker]]<ref>Credited as \"Doctor #1\" in {{cite web|url=https://www.imdb.com/title/tt1198003/|title=\"South Park\" Tonsil Trouble (TV Episode 2008) - IMDb|publisher=IMDb.com}}</ref>\n|appearance = \"[[Tonsil Trouble]]\"\n}}\n'''Dr. Carroll''' is a medical doctor who works in [[Hells Pass Hospital]] and is also a Specialist of Pediatrics. Carroll also does private home-visits. He first appears on \"[[Tonsil Trouble]]\" as a catalyst.\n\n==Appearance==\nDr. Carroll wears a doctor's clothing and glasses. He is a white balding male with a formerly brown graying hair. In multiple episodes, he is seen wearing a wig, sometimes with eyebrows dyed a different color.\n\n<gallery spacing=\"small\" caption=\"Images of Dr. Carroll\" widths=\"160\">\nTonsilTrouble002.png|Dr. Carroll examining [[Eric Cartman|Cartman]] for tonsils in \"[[Tonsil Trouble]]\".\nFishsticks (51).png|Announcing that Kanye does not have gills.\nDeadCelebrities039.png|Dr. Caroll's appearance in \"[[Dead Celebrities]]\".\nGroundedVindaloop00026.png|Dr. Carroll and a nurse check up on [[Butters Stotch|Butters]].\n</gallery>\n\n==Background==\nHe was called to the [[Cartman Residence]] by [[Liane Cartman]] and diagnosed the sick [[Eric Cartman]] with tonsill problem and lured him into undergoing a surgery to remove them in return to a free ice cream.\n\nAfter completing the surgery, Dr. Carroll understood that the blood infusion given to Eric was actually an HIV positive carrier's blood. He enters the patient's room with his 2 nurses, [[Ted (Planned Parenthood Doctor)|Ted]] and [[Nurse Fran|Fran]], to deliver the bad news which caused the young douchebag to lose his mind in fury.\n\nIn \"[[Dead Celebrities]]\", he is seen wearing a brown wig and working in Children's Hospital of Denver, handing [[Ike Broflovski]]'s case to [[Dr. Phillips]] who is specialized in \"supernatural cases\".\n\nHe can be seen in \"[[Hummels & Heroin]]\" alongside other doctors for a seminar on pharmaceuticals.\n\n==Prominence==\n*\"[[Tonsil Trouble]]\" - Debut.\n*\"[[Fishsticks]]\" - Announces that [[Kanye West]] does not have gills.\n*\"[[Dead Celebrities]]\" - Mentions that he's a pediatric doctor, seen working in Denver.\n*\"[[You're Getting Old]]\" -  Tells Stan his musical tastes are naturally changing as he grows up.\n*\"[[Grounded Vindaloop]]\" - Seen treating [[Butters Stotch|Butters]] after he gets stabbed by a hooker.\n*\"[[Hummels & Heroin]] - Seen in the background at the pharmaceuticals seminar and convention.\n\n==References==\n{{Reflist}}\n\n{{Minor Characters from Season Twelve}}\n[[zh:卡罗尔医生]]\n[[Category:Townsfolk]]\n[[Category:Medics]]\n[[Category:Minor characters]]\n[[Category:Elderly people]]\n[[Category:Characters voiced by Trey Parker]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Adult characters]]\n[[Category:Male characters]]"
