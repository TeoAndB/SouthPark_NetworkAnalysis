They appeared in the [[Season Seventeen]] episode, \"[[Ginger Cow]]\".\n\n==Background==\n[[Eric Cartman|Cartman]] decides to prank [[Kyle Broflovski|Kyle]] by dressing up a cow to look ginger, and later shares it online. However, the post is discovered by various news networks around the world, the reporters began reporting on the ginger cow on live television. After which, they each drew a gun from under the desk and shot themselves in the head.\n\n==Appearance==\nThe male reporter is bald, and wears a dull brown suit with white shirt and black tie.\n\nThe female reporter wears a white shirt with brown overall, and a navy blue skirt.\n\nThey each have a microphone pinned onto their chests.\n\n==References==\n{{Reflist}}\n\n[[zh:肯尼亚新闻主播]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Adult characters]]\n[[Category:One-off characters]]\n[[Category:News reporters]]\n[[Category:Deceased characters]]"
