"{{South Park character\n|name = Cosette\n|image = Cosette.png\n|gender = Female\n|hair = Brown\n|job = Student\n|voice = [[Mary Kay Bergman]]<ref>Credited as the \"Starving Daughter\" in {{cite web|url=https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0766086/|title=\"South Park\" Roger Ebert Should Lay Off the Fatty Foods (TV episode 1998)|publisher=IMDb.com}}</ref>\n|appearance = \"[[Roger Ebert Should Lay off the Fatty Foods]]\"\n}}\n'''Cosette''' is a poverty-stricken girl who appears in the [[Season Two]] episode \"[[Roger Ebert Should Lay off the Fatty Foods]]\". She is based on the character of the same name from ''Les Misérables''.\n\n==Background==\nCosette appears alongside her mother as a finalist to appear in a [[Cheesy Poofs]] commercial to sing the [[Cheesy Poofs Theme Song]]. They both hope that she wins so that they can get a little food. Worried that Cosette might win, [[Eric Cartman|Cartman]] distracts them by saying that there is bread outside and when they leave, he closes the door behind them.\n\n==Appearance==\nCosette has messy brown hair that parted far to the right side. She wears a black beret, a white shirt, a black skirt, and black shoes.
