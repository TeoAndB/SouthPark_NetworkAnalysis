"{{South Park character\n|name = Howard Flannigan\n|image = Other-howard-j-flannigan.png\n|gender = Male\n|hair = Gray\n|job = Gardener\n|voice = [[Trey Parker]]<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.imdb.com/title/tt3169686/|title=Goth Kids 3: Dawn of the Posers on IMDB}}</ref>\n|relation1 = Wife\n|relativename1 = [[Sarah Flannigan]]\n|appearance = \"[[Goth Kids 3: Dawn of the Posers]]\"\n}}\n'''Howard Flannigan''', also referred to as '''Harold Flannigan''', is a gardener for [[Troubled Acres]] who appears in the [[Season Seventeen]] episode, \"[[Goth Kids 3: Dawn of the Posers]]\".\n\n==Background==\nHoward works for the plants that are turning people emo at Troubled Acres. He is implied to be responsible for turning [[Henrietta Biggle]] \"emo\". Later on, he is shown trying to turn the tall goth [[Michael]] emo. When the rest of the [[Goth Kids]] and a few of the [[South Park Vampire Society|Vamp Kids]] try to figure out how Henrietta was transformed, Howard is seen turning them into emos after [[Firkle Smith|Firkle]] reveals himself to already be emo. However, it is revealed by the end of the episode that the plants are simply ficus plants with vibrating pots and that Howard was set up by his wife [[Sarah Flannigan|Sarah]] for a pranking reality show called ''Yes! I'm Scared''.\n\n==Appearance==\nHoward has gray hair that is balding and gray eyebrows. He wears a brown gardening apron over a gray shirt, blue pants, green boots, and beige gloves.
