"{{South Park character\n|image = Aunt-jemima.png\n|name = Aunt Jemima\n|gender = Female\n|hair = Black\n|race = African-American\n|job = Breakfast Icon\n|appearance = \"[[Gluten Free Ebola]]\"\n}}\n'''Aunt Jemima''' is a breakfast icon for the Quaker Oats Company that appears in the [[Season Eighteen]] episode, \"[[Gluten Free Ebola]]\", where she tells [[Eric Cartman]], in his delirious dream, that the USDA's food pyramid is upside down.\n\n==Appearance==\nAunt Jemima wears a red, white, and blue dress with a white lace collar, pearl earrings, black shoes, and a yellow and white kerchief on her head. She is seen holding a plate of pancakes.\n\n==Trivia==\n*Aunt Jemima's role in the episode is a reference to Mother Abagail from Stephen King's book ''The Stand''.\n*She is the second ally of Cartman seen in a dream, the other being the [[Pharaoh of Egypt]].\n{{Minor Characters from Season Eighteen}}\n[[es:Aunt Jemima]]\n[[fr:Aunt Jemima]]\n[[zh:杰迈玛阿姨]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Female characters]]\n[[Category:One-off characters]]\n[[Category:African-American characters]]\n[[Category:Minority characters]]\n[[Category:Adult characters]]\n[[Category:Crossover characters]]"
