He was first seen in the [[Season Eight]], episode \"[[Goobacks]]\".\n\n==Appearance==\nHe has messy curly brown hair and a brown mustache. He wears a white jacket with green accents over a black collared T-shirt, and also wears dark blue trousers and black boots.
