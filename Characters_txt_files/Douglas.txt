In \"[[Dances with Smurfs]]\", he told [[Eric Cartman]] his name at the signing of the book, ''[[What Happened to My School?]]''. In \"[[Ass Burgers]]\", he said \"Wow!\" when [[Stan Marsh]] told the crowd he may become his new best friend.\n\n==Appearance==\nDouglas wears a light grey beanie, a dark grey jacket with green trim, and light grey pants.
