He has often been a rival to [[Ike Broflovski]], but they appear sometimes as friends. He is often accompanied by his best friend [[Quaid]].\n\n==Appearance==\nFilmore has short, spiky black hair. He wears a red shirt with a dump truck on it, and jeans. His name invokes that of [[Al Gore]], whom he was a stand-in for in his first appearance, \"[[Trapper Keeper]]\". According to the same episode, Filmore's aunt is [[Rosie O'Donnell]], whom he slightly resembles.\n\n<gallery widths=\"120\" caption=\"Images of Filmore Anderson\">\nImage:Filmore Anderson.png|Picture of Filmore\nImage:Spfilmoar.png|Another picture of Filmore\nImage:Filmore.jpg|Filmore on [[South Park Studios]]\nFilmoresMom.png|Filmore with his [[Mrs. Anderson|mother]].\n</gallery>\n\n==Prominence==\n*\"[[Trapper Keeper]]\" - His first appearance where he is nominated for class president and competes with [[Ike Broflovski]].\n*\"[[The Wacky Molestation Adventure]]\" - He is one of the kids at the Treasure Cove.\n*\"[[It Hits the Fan]]\" - He asks Mr. Garrison if saying \"I have to take a shit\" is okay, to which he is responded negatively.\n*\"[[Cartmanland]]\" - Seen at [[North Park Funland|Cartmanland]].\n*\"[[Proper Condom Use]]\" - He asks Mr. Garrison if he can do fingerpainting while in a sexual education class.\n*\"[[The Return of the Fellowship of the Ring to the Two Towers]]\" - Part of the fellowship along with [[the Boys]], [[Jimmy Valmer]], and [[Craig Tucker|Craig]], playing the role of Gimli.
