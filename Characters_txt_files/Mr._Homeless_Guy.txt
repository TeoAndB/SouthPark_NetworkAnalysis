"{{South Park character\n|name = Mr. Homeless Guy\n|image = mr-homeless-guy.png\n|gender = Male\n|race = Caucasian\n|hair = Dark Blond\n|job = Homeless\n|died = \"[[The New Terrance and Phillip Movie Trailer]]\"\n|voice = [[Trey Parker]]<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0705934/|title=\"South Park\" Ike's Wee Wee (TV Episode 1998)|publisher=IMDb.com}}</ref>\n|appearance = \"[[Ike's Wee Wee]]\"\n|final = \"[[The New Terrance and Phillip Movie Trailer]]\"\n}}\n'''Mr. Homeless Guy''' is an unemployed, alcoholic hobo who first appears in \"[[Ike's Wee Wee]]\".\n\n==Background==\nAfter a montage to \"[[Mountain Town]]\", [[The Boys|the boys]] are finally ready to see [[Terrance and Phillip]] in ''[[Asses of Fire]]''. When they get to the theater, the [[Bijou Cinema Ticket Operator]] denies them entry, citing the film to be rated R. The boys, not giving up, employ the help of Mr. Homeless Guy to buy them all tickets, in exchange for $10. He accepts, and purchases six tickets for the boys, and goes off to buy his bottle of vodka.\n\nHe briefly reappears in a cameo role in \"[[The New Terrance and Phillip Movie Trailer]]\". While the boys and a group of crack addicts (including Mr. Homeless Guy) are watching ''[[Russell Crowe Fightin' Around The World]]'', [[Chef]]'s rogue TV comes along and shoots Mr. Homeless Guy.\n\n==Appearance==\nMr. Homeless Guy is visibly disheveled, wearing tattered brown pants and shirt. He also wears black shoes, has poor dental hygiene, and is frequently seen holding a cigarette.
