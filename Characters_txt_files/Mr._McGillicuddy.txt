When they visit the T-Shirt Factory to have their T-shirt printed, Mr. McGillicuddy tells them of the gang's existence in [[Denver, Colorado|Denver]], but refuses to explain further.\n\n==Appearance==\nMr. McGillicuddy has balding gray with mustache.
