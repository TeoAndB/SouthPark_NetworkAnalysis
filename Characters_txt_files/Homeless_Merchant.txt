"{{South Park character\n|name = Homeless Merchant\n|image = Merchant-hobo.png\n|gender = Male\n|race = Caucasian\n|hair = Brown\n|appearance = ''[[South Park: The Stick of Truth]]''\n}}\nThe '''Homeless Merchant''' is a merchant in ''[[South Park: The Stick of Truth]]''.\n\n==Background==\nThe merchant can be found just outside [[U-STOR-IT]], where he sells general items. [[The New Kid]] is required to purchase a set of goth clothes from him for the [[Nonconformist]] quest.\n\n==Appearance==\nThe merchant has a disheveled brown hair and a few dirt patches on his face. He wears a green jacket over a black shirt with a partially tattered red scarf, light brown pants with a blue patch on the right, and a torn pair of gray socks.\n\n[[zh:流浪汉商人]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Male characters]]\n[[Category:The Stick of Truth: Characters]]\n[[Category:Video Game Exclusive characters]]"
