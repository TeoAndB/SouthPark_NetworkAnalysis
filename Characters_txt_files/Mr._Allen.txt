"{{South Park character\n|name = Mr. Allen\n|image = Bills-father.png\n|gender = Male\n|hair = Dark Brown\n|voice = [[Trey Parker]]<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0705919/|title=\"South Park\" Damien (TV Episode 1998)}}</ref>\n|relation1 = Son\n|relativename1 = [[Bill Allen]]\n|appearance = \"[[Mr. Hankey, the Christmas Poo]]\"\n|speaking = \"[[Damien]]\"\n}}\n'''Mr. Allen''' is [[Bill Allen]]'s father, who first appears in the [[Season One]] episode, \"[[Mr. Hankey, the Christmas Poo]]\".\n\n==Background==\nHe first appeared in the background watching the [[South Park Elementary]] Christmas play in \"[[Mr. Hankey, the Christmas Poo]]\". He later appeared prominently in \"[[Damien]]\", where he was at the bar laughing with [[Mr. McDonald]], using their sons' \"That's gay!\" catch phrase. He shares the same characteristics as his son, with the exception of the turquoise shirt; he wears a purple shirt which resembles that of [[Fosse]]'s.\n\nHe is shown to be friends with [[Mr. McDonald]] and was confirmed to be Bill's father in the creator commentary for \"[[Damien]]\".\n\n==Appearance==\nHis appearance is similar to his son's, the only difference being his shirt; he wears a purple shirt rather than a turquoise one.\n\n==Personality==\nMuch like his son, he likes to call things \"gay\" whilst laughing in a deep voice.\n\n==Prominence==\n*\"[[Mr. Hankey, the Christmas Poo]]\" - Seen in the background watching the South Park Elementary Christmas play.\n*\"[[Damien]]\" - Seen at the bar with Fosse's father, laughing whilst using their sons' \"That's gay!\" catch phrase.\n*\"[[Ike's Wee Wee]]\" - Drives by to make fun of [[Mr. Mackey]] after he loses his job for giving drugs to children.\n\n==References==\n{{Reflist}}\n{{Minor Characters from Season One}}\n[[zh:艾伦先生]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Male characters]]\n[[Category:Adult characters]]\n[[Category:Parents]]\n[[Category:Minor characters]]\n[[Category:Townsfolk]]\n[[Category:Characters voiced by Trey Parker]]\n[[Category:Unnamed characters]]"
