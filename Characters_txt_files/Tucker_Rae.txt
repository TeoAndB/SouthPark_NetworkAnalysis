"{{South Park character\n|name = Tucker \"Cleetus\" Rae\n|image = Rednecks-tucker-rae.png\n|gender = Male\n|race = Caucasian\n|hair = Dirty Blond\n|job = Digital Assistant {{s|(formerly)}}\n|voice = [[Matt Stone]]\n|appearance = \"[[White People Renovating Houses]]\"\n}}\n'''Tucker \"Cleetus\" Rae''' is an anti-[[Alexa]] protester who appeared in the [[Season Twenty-One]] premiere episode, \"[[White People Renovating Houses]]\".\n\n==Background==\nWhen he and the other protesters successfully got all digital assistants removed from [[South Park (Location)|South Park]], Tucker took the place of the ''Alexa'' used at the bar, [[Crunchy's Micro Brew]]. Unlike [[Darryl Weathers|Darryl]], [[Jim Bob]], and the other protestors, Tucker was happy with Alexa's job, answering [[Randy Marsh|Randy]]'s requests enthusiastically. Right before Darryl broke down and expressed his racist beliefs to him and Randy, Tucker attempted to calm him down, telling him he has to keep trying at his new job. Darryl disregarded him.\n\n==Appearance==\nCleetus has very messy dirty blond hair and a thick beard. He has many wrinkles on his face and his mouth is very close to his nose. He wears a blue jumpsuit, black shoes, and a dark blue hat\n\n==Personality==\nTucker found solace in Alexa's job, remaining level-headed and mild while working.\n\n==Prominence==\n*\"[[White People Renovating Houses]]\" - Debut appearance.
