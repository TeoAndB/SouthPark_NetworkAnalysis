"{{South Park character\n|name = Vladimir Putin\n|image = vladimir-putin.png\n|gender = Male\n|hair = Auburn\n|age = {{age|1952|10|7}}\n|dob = October 7, 1952\n|job = President of [[Russia]]\n|religion = Russian Orthodoxy\n|voice = [[Trey Parker]]<ref>Credited as the \"Russian President\" in {{cite web|url=http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0761285/|title=\"Free Willzyx\" on IMDB}}</ref>\n|wikipedia = [[Wikipedia:Vladimir Putin|Vladimir Putin]]\n|appearance = \"[[Free Willzyx]]\"\n}}{{LinkDisambig|Vladimir (Disambiguation)}}\n'''Vladimir Putin''' is the current President of [[Russia]]. He first appears in the [[Season Nine]] episode \"[[Free Willzyx]]\".\n\n==Background==\nIn \"[[Free Willzyx]]\", he receives a phone call from [[Kyle Broflovski|Kyle]] asking if Russia can send his whale to the moon, which was declined because Putin asked for $20,000,000.\n\nHe appears as one of the world leaders in \"[[Pinewood Derby]]\", agreeing to nuke [[Finland]] after its leader [[Matti Vanhanen]] plans to inform the [[Intergalactic Police]] about the \"space cash\" stolen on board [[Kevern Zaksor|Baby Fark McGee-zax's]] ship.\n\nHe later appears in \"[[Members Only]]\", where he is seen speaking to an adviser about [[TrollTrace.com]]. Concerned that his girlfriend will see all of his internet history, he declares that the service must be stopped. He receives a phone call from the [[White House]] expecting the President but instead speaks to several Memberberries reminiscing about the cold war.\n\nHe makes yet another appearance in \"[[Franchise Prequel]]\", where [[Stephen Stotch]] [[List of Groundings|grounds]] both him and Butters.\n\n==Appearance==\nPutin dresses formally, wearing a black suit and a red tie with yellow dots. He has auburn-red hair and wrinkles on his forehead, due to age.\n\n==Prominence==\n*\"[[Free Willzyx]]\" - Kyle calls him to help him and his class sends a whale to the moon.\n*\"[[Pinewood Derby]]\"\n*\"[[Members Only]]\" - He is worried that his girlfriend will go through all of his internet history, so he declares that [[TrollTrace.com]] must be stopped.\n*\"[[Franchise Prequel]]\" - Stephen grounds him and Butters for spreading misinformation on social media.\n*\"[[South ParQ Vaccination Special]]\" - A photo of Putin can be found on [[Bob White]]'s [[QAnon]] board.\n\n==References==\n{{reflist}}\n{{PoliticalCelebrities}}\n[[zh:弗拉基米尔·普京]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Celebrities]]\n[[Category:Political celebrities]]\n[[Category:Male characters]]\n[[Category:Minor characters]]\n[[Category:Adult characters]]\n[[Category:Characters voiced by Trey Parker]]\n[[Category:Elderly people]]"
