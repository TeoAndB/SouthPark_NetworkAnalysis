She often appears as a background character who has been seen in [[Herbert Garrison|Mr. Garrison]]'s class. She took [[Pip Pirrip]]'s seat in the classroom after he [[201|died]], as seen in \"[[Royal Pudding]]\".\n\n==Appearance==\nShe has shoulder-length brown hair. She wears a purple sweater, a grey skirt, and black shoes. She can usually be seen holding a teal trapper keeper.\n\nShe is quite notable for being one of the few girls at school who is always seen wearing a skirt, along with [[Girl with Blonde Hair]], [[Henrietta Biggle]], [[Kelly P. Gardner]], [[Monica Ryland]], [[Sally Darson]], [[Shauna]], [[Sophie Gray]], and [[Theresa]].\n\n<gallery widths=\"160\" spacing=\"small\" caption=\"Images of Allie\">\nTSOT allie.png|Allie Nelson in Pleases and Sparkles Club in \"[[The List]]\"\nAllie ring png.png|Allie Nelson at the Jonas Brothers concert in \"[[The Ring]]\"\n</gallery>\n\n==Prominence==\n*\"[[The List]]\"\n*\"[[Tonsil Trouble]]\"\n*\"[[Super Fun Time]]\" -
