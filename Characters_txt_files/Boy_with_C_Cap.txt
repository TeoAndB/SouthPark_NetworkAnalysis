"{{South Park character\n|name = Boy with C Cap\n|image = BoywithredcapC.png\n|gender = Male\n|hair = Brown\n|age = 9-10\n|job = Student\n|grade = [[The 4th Grade|4th Grade]]\n|appearance = \"[[The Succubus]]\"\n}} \nThe '''Boy with C Cap''' is a background character in ''[[South Park]]''. He occasionally appears in the background throughout the show, mostly in large crowds, at parties, field trips, or in the [[South Park Elementary|school]] hallways. He appears in the background of \"[[Tweek vs. Craig]]\", watching the fight between [[Tweek Tweak]] and [[Craig Tucker]]. In \"[[Funnybot]]\" he is seen sleeping in [[Jimmy Valmer]]'s comedy awards.\n\nHis name remains unknown and he has yet to have any speaking roles.\n\n==Appearance==\nHe wears gray-brown pants, black shoes, a blue button-down shirt with a collar, red gloves, brown hair and a red baseball cap with a black brim and the letter C on it. Sometimes in his cameos, the C on his cap is missing.\n\n==Prominence==\n*\"[[The Succubus]]\" - He is seen eating in the lunchroom.\n*\"[[Tweek vs. Craig]]\" - He is shown watching the fight.\n*''[[South Park: Bigger, Longer & Uncut]]'' - He is seen at the theater seeing the Terrance and Phillip movie.\n*\"[[Hooked on Monkey Fonics]]\"- Seen at the dance.\n*\"[[The Red Badge of Gayness]]\" - Seen in [[Topeka]], Kansas.\n*\"[[Cartman's Silly Hate Crime 2000]]\" - Seen in the crowd at sled race.\n*\"[[Probably]]\"\n*\"[[Fourth Grade]]\" - Seen in South Park Elementary.\n*\"[[A Very Crappy Christmas]]\" - Seen exiting his house to go watch the spirit of Christmas.\n*\"[[Scott Tenorman Must Die]]\" - Seen watching the Radiohead Interview and the Little Piggy Video.\n*\"[[Terrance and Phillip: Behind the Blow]]\"\n*\"[[Cartmanland]]\" - Seen having fun in the theme park.\n*\"[[Proper Condom Use]]\" - Seen in the [[South Park Elementary Cafeteria]].\n*\"[[How to Eat with Your Butt]]\" - Seen in line to take a school picture.\n*\"[[Butt Out]]\" - He is shown watching the Butt Out program.\n*\"[[Raisins]]\" - Shown eating at Raisins.\n*\"[[Stupid Spoiled Whore Video Playset]]\" - He is seen at [[Bebe Stevens|Bebe]]'s party.\n*\"[[Smug Alert!]]\" - Seen at Kyle's going away party.\n*\"[[ManBearPig]]\" - Seen in the auditorium.\n*\"[[Make Love, Not Warcraft]]\" - Seen in the playground.\n*\"[[With Apologies to Jesse Jackson]]\" - Background only.\n*\"[[Le Petit Tourette]]\" - Seen at the toy store.\n*\"[[Imaginationland]]\" - Seen at Cartman's party.\n*\"[[Eek, A Penis!]]\" - Seen in hallways;
