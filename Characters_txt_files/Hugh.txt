"{{South Park character\n|name = Hugh\n|image = Hugh.png\n|gender = Male\n|race = Caucasian\n|hair = Brown\n|appearance = \"[[Dead Kids]]\"\n}}\n'''Hugh''' is a ''[[South Park]]'' character based and drawn on the real-life person with the same name who had won the Omaze Campaign for \"NEXT for AUTISM\"<ref>{{cite web|url=https://youtu.be/36Ps4dH-Sm4|title=This Omaze Winner is Going to Get Drawn into South Park!|publisher=Omaze}}</ref>. He first appear in the [[Season Twenty-Two]] episode, \"[[Dead Kids]]\".\n\n==Background==\nIn \"[[Dead Kids]]\", he was seen as one of the [[City People|townsfolk]] surprising [[Sharon Marsh]] at [[South Park Elementary]].\n\nIn \"[[Nobody Got Cereal?]]\", he hosts the symposium titled \"When should I start to worry?\".\n\n==Appearance==\nHugh wears a buttoned shirt, with long trousers. He has brown eyebrows and a brown goatee.
