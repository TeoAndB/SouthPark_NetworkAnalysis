He later made a cameo appearance in the same role in \"[[Follow That Egg!]]\", where he gives Stan and Kyle a lift. \n\nLike most citizens of previous seasons, he does not appear much in current seasons.\n\n==Appearance==\nHe wears a white shirt under a brown coat, pale green pants and black shoes.
