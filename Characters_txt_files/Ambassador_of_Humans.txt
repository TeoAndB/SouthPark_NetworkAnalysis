"{{South Park character\n|image = Ambassador.png\n|name = Ambassador of Humans\n|gender = Male\n|race = Caucasian\n|hair = Dark Blond\n|appearance = \"[[Faith Hilling]]\"\n}}\nThe '''Ambassador of Humans''' appears in the [[Season Sixteen]] episode, \"[[Faith Hilling]]\", where he was sent to attempt to communicate with the [[Talking Cat]]. His interpretation of the cat's vocalizations led him to believe that cats would continue evolving, declare war on humans and that humans and cats could not coexist.\n\n==Appearance==\nThe Ambassador of Humans wears black boots, a black shirt with gray accents, a gray robe, a red belt with a gold star buckle, a red sash to hold several of his earned metals, a gray fez hat, and pink translucent glasses.
