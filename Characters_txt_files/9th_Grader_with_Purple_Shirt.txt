Scott tells them that he sold his pubes to [[Eric Cartman|Cartman]]. Later the 9th Grader with Purple Shirt and the other unnamed 9th Grader bring the pubes to Scott, which according to them were pubes of almost every child in town. They later appear at the end of the episode, witnessing Cartman reveal to Scott that his parents were killed by [[Farmer Carl Denkins|Farmer Denkins]].\n\nIt is unknown whether he and his friend know about Scott's revenge on Cartman in \"[[201]]\". It is also unknown if they know that Scott is Cartman's half brother.\n\n==Appearance==\nHe has long brown hair and wears a blue hat similar to Stan Marsh's, a long-sleeved purple shirt, gray pants, and blue white shoes.\n\n==Prominence==\n*\"[[Timmy 2000]]\" - Appears only in the HD version of the episode.\n*\"[[Scott Tenorman Must Die]]\"\n*\"[[The Return of the Fellowship of the Ring to the Two Towers]]\"\n*\"[[Cartmanland]]\" - (Cameo)\n*\"[[Fat Butt and Pancake Head]]\" - Seen at the beginning of the episode along with Scott and his other friend watching [[Kyle Broflovski|Kyle]]'s performance.\n*\"[[Major Boobage]]\" - Seen in the commercial.\n*\"[[Super Fun Time]]\"\n*\"[[Butters' Bottom Bitch]]\" - Seen at the [[Alpha Tau Omega]] fraternity house.\n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery widths=\"160\" caption=\"Images of the 9th Grader with Purple Shirt\">\nButtersBottomBitch71.png|Seen on the right side.\n</gallery>\n{{South Park Children}}\n[[zh:紫衣九年级生]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Unnamed characters]]\n[[Category:School children]]\n[[Category:Male characters]]\n[[Category:Minor characters]]\n[[Category:Townsfolk]]\n[[Category:Bullies]]\n[[Category:Teens]]"
