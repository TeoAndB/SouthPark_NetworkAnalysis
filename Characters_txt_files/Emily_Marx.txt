"{{South Park character\n|name = Emily Marx\n|image = Emily pic.png\n|aliases = Jen\n|gender = Female\n|race = Caucasian\n|hair = Blonde\n|age = 9-10\n|job = Student\n|grade = [[The 4th Grade|4th Grade]]\n|voice = [[April Stewart]]<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.imdb.com/title/tt2079298/|title=\"South Park\" Bass to Mouth (TV episode 2011)|publisher=IMDb.com}}</ref>\n|appearance = \"[[Marjorine]]\"\n|speaking = \"[[The List]]\"\n}}\n'''Emily Marx''' is a student at [[South Park Elementary]]. She first appear at [[Heidi Turner]]'s slumber party in \"[[Marjorine]]\", and later at the [[Jonas Brothers]] concert in \"[[The Ring]]\". Her name was confirmed in \"[[Bass to Mouth]]\".\n\n==Appearance==\nEmily has light blonde hair and wears a dark blue jacket and light green pants. In earlier appearances, she wore a navy blue jacket with blue-grey trousers. In \"[[Marjorine]]\", she wears a turquoise pajama top with green and orange hearts, and green pants.\n\n<gallery widths=\"160\" spacing=\"small\" caption=\"Images of Emily\">\nemilypajamas.png|Emily seen in her pajamas, from \"[[The Poor Kid]]\".\nTammy&emily.png|[[Tammy Nelson]] comforting Emily in \"[[Bass to Mouth]]\".\nTheRing01.jpg|Emily (top-middle right) at the [[Jonas Brothers]]' concert from \"[[The Ring]]\".\nEmilyTT.png|Emily in \"[[Super Fun Time]]\".\nEmily.png|Another image of Emily, sad.\nMarjorine52.png|Emily with [[Kal]] and [[Esther]] in \"[[Marjorine]]\".\n</gallery>\n\n==Personality==\nShe seems to be relatively sensitive to social criticism, considering in \"[[Bass to Mouth]]\" she was embarrassed, not because her mother was in rehab, but because the other girls might find out.\n\n==Relationships==\n===[[Tammy Nelson]]===\nIt was shown in \"[[Bass to Mouth]]\" that her and Tammy are very close friends, probably best friends, considering they both admitted and comforted each other over their mothers being addicts/alcoholics, a very personal matter. They are also seen sitting together in the school gymnasium in \"[[Cock Magic]]\". At the Board Girls club, they are playing a marvel game together.\n\n===[[Jason White]]===\nIn \"[[Skank Hunt]]\", Emily is seen breaking up with Jason toward the end of the episode.
