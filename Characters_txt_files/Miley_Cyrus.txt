In the former episode she appears singing \"[[Party Going On]]\" on the stage and in the latter episode, she appears swinging on a large Christmas ornament in the commercial for the special, referencing the music video for her song \"[[Wikipedia: Wrecking Ball (Miley Cyrus song)|Wrecking Ball]]\".\n\n==Appearance==\nIn \"[[Britney's New Look]]\", she appears as a live-action photograph on a news station wearing a black shirt. In her appearance in [[Season Eighteen]], she has blonde hair cut into a bowl cut. She wears a green unitard with many marijuana leaves on it and white boots.
