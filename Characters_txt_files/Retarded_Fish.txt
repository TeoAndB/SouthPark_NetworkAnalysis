"{{South Park character\n|name = Retarded Fish\n|image = Retarded-fish.png\n|gender = Male\n|race = Goldfish\n|voice = [[Trey Parker]]\n|appearance = \"[[A Scause For Applause]]\"\n}}\nThe '''Retarded Fish''' is a character who appears in the [[Season Sixteen]] episode \"[[A Scause For Applause]]\".\n\n==Background==\nAfter [[Stan Marsh|Stan]] goes on ''[[The Charlie Rose Show]]'' to defend himself for refusing to cut off his WWJD wristband despite the controversy, the retarded fish comes in to defend Stan and have an argument with [[Chris Martin]], who is against Stan for wearing the wristband.\n\n==Appearance==\nThe retarded fish is a cross-eyed goldfish wearing a pink bicycle helmet. He lives in a small, round fishbowl. While he was initially heard babbling to the questions asked on the show, he is capable of speaking clearly when asked if he had anything to add.\n\n[[zh:智障鱼]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Male characters]]\n[[Category:One-off characters]]\n[[Category:Animals]]\n[[Category:Characters with deviations]]\n[[Category:Characters voiced by Trey Parker]]"
