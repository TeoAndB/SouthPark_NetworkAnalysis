"{{South Park character\n|name = MMA Host\n|image = Adults-sports-mma-host.png\n|gender = Male\n|race = Caucasian\n|hair = Gray\n|job = Sports TV Show Host\n|appearance = \"[[Board Girls]]\"\n}}\nThe '''MMA host '''is the host of an MMA (Mixed Martial Arts) sports show. He appears in the [[Season Twenty-Three]] episode, \"[[Board Girls]]\".\n\n==Background==\nAfter the events of [[Heather Swanson]] winning the [[Strong Woman Competition]], the host congratulates Heather on her success. The show is however interrupted by [[PC Principal]], who is trying to defend [[Strong Woman]]. Heather then argues with PC Principal and an argument then ensues between the two, resulting in Heather deliberately crashing into a table of refreshments to gain sympathy.\n\n==Appearance==\nThe MMA host wears a coat, with a blue buttoned shirt underneath. He wears trousers and black shoes. He has a trimmed grey beard. His hair is also grey and receding.
