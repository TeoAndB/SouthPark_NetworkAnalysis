He appears in [[Season Twenty-Four]] episode \"[[South ParQ Vaccination Special]]\".\n\n==Background==\nQAnon members in [[South Park (Location)|South Park]] are rounded up in the basement of [[White Residence]] for a message from their alleged \"[[Herbert Garrison|Chosen One]]\". [[Bob White]] interprets his insult \"blow shit out of your dickhole\" as a message to relay their conspiracy theories to children, kickstarting the [[Tutornon]] organization. Jake Angeli is assigned to teach [[Scott Malkinson]]. He explains to Scott how the [[pedophilia|pedophilic]] [[Hollywood Elites]] abduct and kill children to obtain [[adrenochrome]] from their blood to stay in power and control the masses. Frightened, Scott shouts for [[Clark Malkinson|his dad]].\n\nScott is successfully brainwashed by Jake as he later joins the children's arm of QAnon, [[Lil' Qties]].\n\nJake later appears outside [[South Park Elementary]] preparing to fight the [[Kommunity Kidz]] to prevent them from administering [[COVID-19 Vaccine|vaccines]] to the school faculty.\n\n==Appearance==\nJake Angeli has a full brown beard, has red, white and blue face paint, and is shirtless. He wears a shaman headdress and blue pants that are torn on the left.
