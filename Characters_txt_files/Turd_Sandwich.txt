"{{South Park character\n|name = Turd Sandwich\n|image = Turd-sandwich.png\n|gender = Male\n|job = School Mascot\n|voice = [[Matt Stone]]<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0705922/|title=\"Douche and Turd\" on IMDB}}</ref>\n|appearance = \"[[Douche and Turd]]\"\n}}\n'''Turd Sandwich''' is one of the candidates for the [[South Park Elementary]] school mascot. He appeared in the [[Season Eight]] episode, \"[[Douche and Turd]]\", where he was seen competing against [[Giant Douche]]. The idea for having a turd sandwich as the school mascot was invented by [[Eric Cartman]], who disagreed with [[Kyle Broflovski]]'s idea to have a giant douche represent the school.\n\nThe \"Douche and Turd\" concept is redone in \"[[Member Berries]]\". In the election, [[Herbert Garrison]] is the [[Giant Douche]] and [[Hillary Clinton]] is the Turd Sandwich. The Turd Sandwich eventually loses.\n\n==Appearance==\nTurd Sandwich is an anthropomorphic sandwich, with cheese, lettuce, tomatoes, an olive, and a large piece of fecal matter.
