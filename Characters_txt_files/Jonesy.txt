"{{South Park character\n|name = Jonesy\n|image = Jonesy.png\n|gender = Male\n|hair = Black\n|job = Musician\n|voice = [[Trey Parker]]<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0766078/|title=\"South Park\" Cat Orgy (TV episode 1999)|publisher=IMDb.com}}</ref><br>[[Matt Stone]]<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0766092/|title=\"South Park\" Timmy 2000 (TV episode 2000)|publisher=IMDb.com}}</ref>\n|appearance = \"[[Clubhouses]]\"\n}}\n'''Jonesy''' is a musician and a member of [[Skyler]]'s band in \"[[Cat Orgy]]\" and \"[[Timmy 2000]]\". He first appears in the [[Season Two]] episodes \"[[Clubhouses]]\" and \"[[Chef Aid]]\".\n\n==Appearance==\nJonesy has long black hair. He wears black pants and a green shirt. In later episodes his shirt has a Zildjian logo on it.\n\n<gallery widths=\"160\" captionalign=\"left\" caption=\"Images of Jonesy\">\nJonesy.jpg|Jonesy at the drums.\n</gallery>\n\n==Personality==\nJonesy seems to have a negative attitude, even insulting himself and the other band members in \"[[Timmy 2000]]\".\n\n==Prominence==\n*\"[[Clubhouses]]\" -  He and his friend [[Scott (\"Clubhouses\")|Scott]] are invited by the [[Runaway Girls|runaway girls]] to the Cartman club house.\n*\"[[Chef Aid]]\" - Attends Chef Aid.\n*\"[[Cat Orgy]]\" -  Plays with the rest of Skyler's band at Cartman's house.\n*\"[[Timmy 2000]]\" - Allows Timmy to join the band and competes in the Battle of the Bands.\n*\"[[How to Eat with Your Butt]]\" - Asks Cartman why he isn't laughing at the movies.\n*\"[[Christian Rock Hard]]\" - Jonesy and the band follow suit after [[Moop]] strikes.\n\n==Trivia==\n*Jonesy wears a T-shirt which reads, \"[[Wikipedia:Avedis Zildjian Company|Zildjian]]\" which is a reference to a company of the same name which supplies music to the show.\n\n==References==\n{{Reflist}}\n[[pl:Jonesy]]\n[[zh:琼西]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Minor characters]]\n[[Category:Male characters]]\n[[Category:Adult characters]]\n[[Category:Characters voiced by Matt Stone]]\n[[Category:Characters voiced by Trey Parker]]\n[[Category:The Lords of the Underworld]]"
