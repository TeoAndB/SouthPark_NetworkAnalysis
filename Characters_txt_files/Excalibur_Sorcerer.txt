"{{South Park character\n|name = Excalibur Sorcerer\n|image = Misc-excalibur-sorcerer.png\n|gender = Male\n|hair = Gray\n|job = Sorcerer\n|voice = [[Trey Parker]]<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0705935/|title=\"South Park\" It Hits the Fan (TV episode 2001)|publisher=IMDb.com}}</ref>\n|appearance = \"[[It Hits the Fan]]\"\n}}\nThe '''Excalibur Sorcerer''' is an elderly sorcerer who works in the Inner Sanctum of the [[Excalibur]] Casino who appears in the [[Season Five]] episode \"[[It Hits the Fan]]\".\n\n==Background==\n[[The Boys]] and [[Jerome \"Chef\" McElroy|Chef]] go to the sorcerer to learn about the stone, also called the \"Rune Stone of Gaelic\", where he describes to them [[The Knights of Standards and Practices]]. Later on, he shows them a book with a list of \"curse\" words, revealing that a dragon named [[Geldon]] will show up if the word of curse is said too many times and that the world will be destroyed.\n\n==Appearance==\nThe sorcerer has long gray hair that is balding on the top as well as gray eyebrows and a long gray beard that obscures his mouth. He wears a large maroon robe with two yellow circles with a black design on them as well as a brown rope around his waist and purple pointy shoes. He also carries a long wooden cane with him.\n\n<gallery widths=\"160\" captionalign=\"left\" caption=\"Images of the Excalibur sorcerer\">\nItHitstheFan145.png|The sorcerer reading from the book.\nItHitstheFan148.png|The sorcerer holding the stone.\n</gallery>\n\n==Prominence==\n*\"[[It Hits the Fan]]\" \n*\"[[I'm a Little Bit Country]]\" - Seen in the crowd, near the end of the episode.\n\n==References==\n{{Reflist}}\n{{Minor Characters from Season Five}}\n[[zh:圣剑巫师]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Male characters]]\n[[Category:Adult characters]]\n[[Category:Unnamed characters]]\n[[Category:Characters voiced by Trey Parker]]\n[[Category:Elderly people]]\n[[Category:Minor characters]]"
