He appears in the [[Season Two]] episode, \"[[Prehistoric Ice Man]]\".\n\n==Background==\nAfter Larry escapes from [[Dr. Alphonse Mephesto|Dr. Mephesto]]'s lab, he seeks out his wife, Leslie. When he meets her, he learns that she has a new life with [[Buck|another man]] and the couple have two kids together, Buck and [[Calvin]].\n\n==Appearance==\nBuck Jr is a teenager with short, curly brown hair and brown eyebrows. He wears a dark red shirt under a big yellow hooded jacket with the top open and \"BUFU\" written in green and what appears to be a green stripe in the middle. He also wears big dark green pants with a blue feel and white shoes.
