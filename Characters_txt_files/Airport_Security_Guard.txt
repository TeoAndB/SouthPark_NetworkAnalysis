He appears in the [[Season Five]] episode \"[[The Entity]]\".\n\n==Background==\nIn an attempt to send [[Kyle Schwartz]] back to his home state of Connecticut, [[the boys]] trick the former into playing hide-and-seek in the airport. As the security guard is screening [[Kenny McCormick|Kenny]] at the boarding gate, he finds a nail clipper on Kenny's possession, and reacts by shooting Kenny, killing him.\n\n==Appearance==\nThe airport security guard has only three strands of black hair, large ears, and a mole on his right cheek.
