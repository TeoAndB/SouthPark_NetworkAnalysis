"{{South Park character\n|name = Handicapped Girl with Light Brown Hair\n|image = UnnamedSpecialKid1.png\n|gender = Female\n|hair = Light Brown\n|age = 9-10\n|job = Student\n|grade = 4th Grade\n|appearance = \"[[Up the Down Steroid]]\"\n}}\nThe '''Handicapped Girl with Light Brown Hair''' is a unnamed handicapped girl who first appear in the [[Season Eight]] episode, \"[[Up the Down Steroid]]\". She is on the [[Blue Team]], along with [[Timmy Burch|Timmy]] and [[Jimmy Valmer|Jimmy]]. She is generally seen as a background character in episodes focused on Jimmy or Timmy. For the time being she has played no major role in the series.\n\n==Appearance==\nShe has short, light brown hair with a frontal bang and has a crooked mouth usually open with unusual teeth. She has already worn several different clothes throughout the episodes. In \"[[Crippled Summer]]\" she wore the standard blue Tardicaca uniform and in \"[[Moss Piglets]]\" she wore a lab coat with a badge on the right side.\n\n==Prominence==\n*\"[[Up the Down Steroid]]\" - Seen training for the special Olympics.\n*\"[[The Death of Eric Cartman]]\"\n*\"[[Crippled Summer]]\" - Seen in the Blue Team and dancing with the other girls at the end of the episode.\n*\"[[Handicar]]\" - Seen with the other disabled children.\n*\"[[Moss Piglets]]\" - Seen at the special science fair.\n\n{{South Park Children}}\n[[zh:棕发残疾女孩]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Unnamed characters]]\n[[Category:Female characters]]\n[[Category:Characters with deviations]]\n[[Category:Minor characters]]\n[[Category:Background characters]]\n[[Category:Townsfolk]]\n[[Category:School children]]\n[[Category:4th graders]]\n[[Category:Female 4th graders]]\n[[Category:Tardicaca campers]]"
