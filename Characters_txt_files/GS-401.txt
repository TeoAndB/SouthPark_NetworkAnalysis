"{{South Park character\n|name = GS-401\n|image = Gary-0.png\n|gender = Male\n|race = Towel\n|died = \"[[Towelie]]\"\n|job = Weaponized Smart Towel\n|voice = [[Trey Parker]]<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0705977/|title=\"Towelie\" on IMDB}}</ref>\n|appearance = \"[[Towelie]]\"\n|final = \"[[Towelie]]\"\n}}\nThe '''GS-401''' is a weaponized, prototype smart towel, who was developed by [[Tynacorp]]. It appeared in the [[Season Five]] episode, \"[[Towelie]]\".\n\n==Background==\n[[The boys]] just want their [[2001 Okama Gamesphere|Okama Gamesphere]] back and to do so, they are forced into the middle of a debacle between Tynacorp and a militaristic resistance group. During the boys' infiltration of Tynacorp, the prototype replacement for [[Towelie (Character)|Towelie]], GS-401, is unleashed against them. GS-401 is quickly defeated apathetically by the boys and their Photon rifle.\n\nThe boys reacquire their Okama Gamesphere, but the Tynacorp building explodes. During their escape, Towelie and the boys form a chain to help save the Gamesphere. GS-401 tries to get Towelie to drop the boys in a fiery inferno, but falls in himself instead.\n\n==Appearance==\nGS-401 is an anthropomorphic blue towel and is similar in appearance to Towelie, except for large muscles and is  darker. \n<gallery widths=\"160\" caption=\"Images of GS-401\">\nTowelie093.png|GS-401 attempting to persuade Towelie into killing the boys.\n</gallery>\n\n==References==\n{{Reflist}}\n{{Minor Characters from Season Five}}\n[[es:GS-401]]\n[[zh:GS-401]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Male characters]]\n[[Category:One-off characters]]\n[[Category:Deceased characters]]\n[[Category:Monsters]]"
