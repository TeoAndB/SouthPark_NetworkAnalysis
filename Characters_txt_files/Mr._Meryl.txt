Mr. Meryl congratulated the boys' efforts, but they were again canceled due to Craig's revamped show, ''[[Animals Close-Up With a Wide-Angle Lens Wearing Hats]]''. \n\n==Appearance==\nMr. Meryl wears a brown button-up shirt with a black tie.
