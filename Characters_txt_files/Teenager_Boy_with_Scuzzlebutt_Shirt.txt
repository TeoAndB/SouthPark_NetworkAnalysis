"{{South Park character\n|name = Teenager Boy with Scuzzlebutt Shirt\n|image = Teen-boy-scuzzlebutt-shirt.png\n|gender = Male\n|hair = Brown\n|job = Student\n|appearance = \"[[Timmy 2000]]\"\n}}\nThe''' [[Teenagers|Teenager]] Boy with [[Scuzzlebutt]] Shirt''' is a character that used to appear in the background of previous seasons, usually seen with other teens. He made his first appearance in the [[Season Four]] episode \"[[Timmy 2000]]\". He can be seen speaking briefly on the episode \"[[Butters' Bottom Bitch]]\". He is currently one of the PC brothers of [[PC Delta]].\n\n==Appearance==\nHe wears a dark cyan shirt with a picture of the [[Scuzzlebutt]] head in a purple color. He has brown hair and freckles. In \"[[Butters' Bottom Bitch]]\" he wore a dark blue shirt with \"ATO\" on the right, a white shirt underneath and brown pants.\n\n==Quotes==\n*''ATOs!
