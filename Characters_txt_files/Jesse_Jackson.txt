"{{South Park character\n|name = Jesse Jackson\n|image = Jesse-jackson.png\n|gender = Male\n|race = African-American\n|hair = Black, Graying\n|age = {{age|1941|10|8}}\n|dob = October 8, 1941\n|job = Civil Rights Activist\n|religion = Baptist\n|voice = [[Trey Parker]]<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0940288/|title=\"South Park\" With Apologies to Jesse Jackson (TV episode 2007)|publisher=IMDb.com}}</ref>\n|wikipedia = [[Wikipedia:Jesse Jackson|Jesse Jackson]]\n|appearance = \"[[Trapper Keeper]]\"\n}}\n'''Jesse Jackson''' is an African-American civil rights activist. He appears in the episode \"[[With Apologies to Jesse Jackson]]\" where [[Randy Marsh]] \"apologized\" to him after saying the N-word on national TV, by literally kissing his ass.\n\nJackson also briefly appear in \"[[Trapper Keeper]]\" to support the African-American students in the [[Kindergartners|kindergarten class]] during the class vote, however he immediately left after [[Herbert Garrison|Mr. Garrison]] told him that there were no black students.\n\n==Appearance==\nJesse Jackson is African-American. He appears to be drawn with a strange eye shape, which are almost circular, as opposed to the elliptical eyes most characters are drawn with. He has black hair that is greying. In \"[[With Apologies to Jesse Jackson]]\", he is dressed in a grey suit and pants, a white shirt and a red tie.\n\n<gallery widths=\"160\" spacing=\"small\" caption=\"Images of Jesse Jackson\">\nJesse Jackson2.png|[[Randy Marsh]] kissing Jackson's ass.\nTrapperKeeper085.png|Jesse Jackson as he appeared in \"[[Trapper Keeper]]\".\n</gallery>\n\n==Personality==\nLike his true counterpart, Jackson is extremely passionate about civil rights, doing whatever he can to ensure African-Americans are not discriminated against and sufficiently represented. However, he is also implied to be slightly egotistical. Randy literally kissed Jackson's backside to apologise for a racial slur he uttered on live television. [[Stan Marsh]] thought this would make everything okay, however [[Token Black]] angrily remarked that Jesse Jackson was, \"not the emperor of black people!\" These events were a tongue-in-cheek reference to the apology Michael Richards made to the real Jesse Jackson for a highly insensitive [[wikipedia:Michael Richards#2006 Laugh Factory incident|outburst]] at the California Laugh Factory in 2006.\n\n==Prominence==\n*\"[[Trapper Keeper]]\" - Debut.\n*\"[[With Apologies to Jesse Jackson]]\"\n*\"[[200]]\" - Seen with the other celebrities suing South Park.\n\n==References==\n{{Reflist}}\n{{PoliticalCelebrities}}\n{{Minor Characters from Season Eleven}}\n[[zh:杰西·杰克逊]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Celebrities]]\n[[Category:Minority characters]]\n[[Category:Political celebrities]]\n[[Category:African-American characters]]\n[[Category:200 and 201 celebrities]]\n[[Category:Characters voiced by Trey Parker]]\n[[Category:Elderly people]]\n[[Category:Male characters]]\n[[Category:Minor characters]]"
