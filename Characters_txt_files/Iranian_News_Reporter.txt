He appeared in the [[Season Seventeen]] episode, \"[[Ginger Cow]]\".\n\n==Background==\n[[Eric Cartman|Cartman]] decides to prank [[Kyle Broflovski|Kyle]] by dressing up a cow to look ginger, and later shares it online. However, the post is discovered by the Iranian news station via a French news website, and the reporter began reporting on the ginger cow on live television. Afterwhich, the reporter committed suicide by a gunshot to his head.\n\n==Appearance==\nThe Iranian news reporter has black hair with thick mustache.
