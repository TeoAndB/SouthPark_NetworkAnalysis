"{{South Park character\n|name = Freja Ollegard\n|image = Celebrities-sports-freja-ollegard.png\n|gender = Female\n|race = Danish\n|hair = Light Blonde\n|age= 38 (deceased)<ref>Reported during a CNN news program in \"[[The Damned]]\"</ref>\n|died = \"[[The Damned]]\"\n|job = Olympic Gold Medalist in Volleyball<br>Breast Cancer Website Founder\n|appearance = \"[[Skank Hunt]]\"\n|final = \"[[The Damned]]\"\n}}\n'''Freja Ollegard''' was a female Olympic 4-time gold medal winner in volleyball and ran a Danish website for breast cancer. She was also a breast cancer survivor. She first appeared in the [[Season Twenty]] episode, \"[[Skank Hunt]]\", where she was seen on the news discussing [[Skankhunt42]].\n\n==Background==\nIn \"[[Skank Hunt]]\", CNN reports that Skankhunt42, an internet troll, had successfully taken down Ollegard's website. Giving a statement for CNN, she declares that she is unmoved by the event and vows to show how strong the Danish are.\n\nIn \"[[The Damned]]\", she appears on a talk show called ''[[Simply Heidi]]'', where she discusses her latest misfortunes with [[Skankhunt42]]. She tries to ignore the troll and his copycats, and maintains a belief in the goodness of humanity. At the end of the show, Heidi reads comments from faux-doctors with offensive names, much to the chagrin of Freja.\n\nUnable to cope with the persistent trolling, Ollegard takes her own life by jumping off of her apartment building's 17th floor. A large community funeral was held for her in [[Denmark]], which now endeavors to out Skankhunt42 and destroy him.\n\n==Appearance==\nFreja typically wore navy pants, a navy coat, a gray shirt, and black, flat shoes. She also wore pink earrings and has short blonde hair.\n\n==Prominence==\n*\"[[Skank Hunt]]\" - Debut appearance; talks about Skankhunt42's effects on her website.\n*\"[[The Damned]]\" - Speaks out against Skankhunt42, but fatally succumbs to the trolling.\n*\"[[Wieners Out]]\" - Cameo; seen in photographs in the beginning of the episode.\n*\"[[Fort Collins]]\" - Cameo; seen in a photograph.\n*\"[[Oh, Jeez]]\" - Cameo; seen in a presentation to [[Gerald Broflovski]] by [[Lennart Bedrager]] about how she was trolled online.\n*\"[[Not Funny]]\" - Mentioned only.\n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery widths=\"160\" spacing=\"small\" caption=\"Images of Freja Ollegard\">\nTheDamned00002.png|Freja on ''Simply Heidi''.\nFrejaOllegard3.png|Freja's photo on CNN.\nFrejaOllegardFuneral1.png|Freja's funeral in Denmark.\nFrejaOllegardFuneral2.png|Freja's funeral in Denmark.\nFrejaJumpsTheDamned.png|Freja jumps out of her apartment window\n</gallery>\n\n==References==\n{{Reflist}}\n\n{{Minor Characters from Season Twenty}}\n{{FictionalCelebrities}}\n[[pl:Freja Ollegard]]\n[[ru:Фрея Оллегард]]\n[[zh:芙蕾雅·奥伦甘]]\n[[Category:Characters]]\n[[Category:Female characters]]\n[[Category:Deceased characters]]\n[[Category:Fictional celebrities]]\n[[Category:Minor characters]]\n[[Category:Characters with unknown voice actor]]\n[[Category:Adult characters]]"
