They appear in the background of the episode \"[[Canada on Strike]]\", as two of the internet celebrities waiting for their theoretical payments. They may or may not be deceased, as they were not seen fighting or dead.\n\n==Appearance==\nAs in their video, they wear matching red Houston Rockets jerseys and the number 11 (Yao Ming) printed on them, along with black shirts and blue jeans. One (Wei Wei) also wears a white headband.
